Nissan LEAF Nismo vs. Nissan Ariya



Stock Motor

Acceleration (0-60 mph):

Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO, with its performance-oriented NISMO tuning, boasts quick acceleration. It can sprint from 0 to 60 mph in approximately 6.5 seconds, making it one of the swifter EVs in its class.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya, designed as a stylish and practical electric crossover, offers respectable acceleration. It reaches 0 to 60 mph in approximately 7.5 seconds, making it a well-rounded performer for everyday driving.


Torque:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO's performance-tuned motor delivers robust torque, providing impressive low-end power and acceleration. Specific torque figures may vary depending on the model year.

Nissan Ariya: With a slightly higher torque figure than the LEAF NISMO, the Ariya offers responsive acceleration and smooth power delivery, making it a capable and enjoyable daily driver.


Horsepower:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO's electric motor produces around 215 horsepower, offering a spirited and engaging driving experience. The NISMO-tuned suspension and handling contribute to its sporty character.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya's motor generates approximately 214 horsepower, offering ample power for comfortable cruising and confident passing maneuvers on the highway.


Top Speed:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO showcases a top speed of approximately 94 mph, providing sufficient highway performance for most driving situations.

Nissan Ariya: With a slightly higher top speed of around 99 mph, the Ariya exhibits a bit more top-end capability, making it suitable for long-distance driving.


Quarter Mile Time:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO completes the quarter-mile sprint in approximately 15 seconds, showcasing its impressive performance and acceleration.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya conquers the quarter-mile in around 15.5 seconds, offering solid performance for everyday driving scenarios.


Lap Times:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: While the LEAF NISMO delivers strong acceleration and handling, it may not be specifically designed for racetrack lap times. However, its sporty tuning makes it enjoyable on twisty roads.

Nissan Ariya: As a crossover SUV, the Ariya is not built for aggressive track driving. However, its handling and responsiveness make it well-suited for confident handling during city and highway driving.


Powertrain Configuration:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO utilizes a front-wheel-drive (FWD) configuration, delivering power to the front wheels.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya is available with both front-wheel-drive (FWD) and all-wheel-drive (AWD) configurations, providing added traction and stability in challenging road conditions.


Transmission Type:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: Like all electric vehicles, the LEAF NISMO employs a single-speed transmission, ensuring smooth power delivery and minimal maintenance.

Nissan Ariya: Similar to the LEAF NISMO, the Ariya also uses a single-speed transmission, offering the same benefits of seamless power delivery and ease of use.


Power-to-Weight Ratio:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO's power-to-weight ratio is optimized for sporty performance, allowing it to deliver thrilling acceleration and handling.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya's power-to-weight ratio is well-balanced for its crossover SUV class, providing a harmonious blend of power and efficiency.


RPM (Shaft Speed):

Both the Nissan LEAF NISMO and the Nissan Ariya operate at high RPM (Revolutions Per Minute) levels to harness the full potential of their electric motors, ensuring instant torque and quick acceleration.





Tuning Ability

Upgraded Intake:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: While the LEAF NISMO's electric motor does not rely on traditional air intake systems, some aftermarket companies offer high-flow air filters designed to improve airflow efficiency. This can potentially boost performance and responsiveness.

Nissan Ariya: Similarly, the Ariya's electric motor doesn't have a conventional air intake system, but aftermarket high-flow air filters can still enhance the motor's efficiency, contributing to a slight increase in performance.


Exhaust Upgrades:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: As an electric vehicle, the LEAF NISMO does not have a traditional exhaust system. However, some companies offer simulated sound modules that mimic the sound of a traditional engine, providing a more engaging driving experience.

Nissan Ariya: Like the LEAF NISMO, the Ariya's electric powertrain eliminates the need for a conventional exhaust system. However, some manufacturers offer exhaust tip enhancements to give the vehicle a sportier appearance.


Forced Induction:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: Traditional forced induction systems like turbochargers or superchargers are not applicable to electric vehicles like the LEAF NISMO.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya, being an electric vehicle, also cannot accommodate traditional forced induction upgrades.


Fuel Management:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO's power management system is carefully calibrated for a balance of performance and efficiency. While there may not be many aftermarket options for fuel management upgrades, software tuning and updates from Nissan could potentially offer performance gains.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya's power management system is similarly optimized for efficiency and performance. While aftermarket options may be limited, future software updates from Nissan could potentially unlock additional performance.


Ignition Options:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: Electric vehicles like the LEAF NISMO do not have spark plugs or traditional ignition systems. Performance upgrades in this area would involve software tuning to optimize power delivery and performance.

Nissan Ariya: Like the LEAF NISMO, the Ariya's ignition system relies on software management. Any modifications would depend on official software updates from Nissan.


Performance Software Tuning:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO's performance can be enhanced through software tuning, optimizing power delivery and throttle response. Some companies offer tuning services specifically tailored for the LEAF NISMO.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya's performance can also be improved through software tuning, offering a more engaging driving experience. However, it is essential to choose reputable and experienced tuners for EVs like the Ariya.


Suspension Upgrades:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: To enhance the LEAF NISMO's handling and cornering capabilities, aftermarket suspension upgrades such as stiffer springs and performance dampers can be considered.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya's sporty character can be further accentuated through suspension upgrades, allowing for more precise and responsive handling.


Brake Upgrades:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: Upgraded brake pads and rotors can improve braking performance and reduce stopping distances for the LEAF NISMO.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya can benefit from enhanced braking capabilities through upgraded brake components, offering improved safety and control.





Suspension

Ride Comfort: Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO, with its performance-oriented tuning, offers a more engaging and dynamic driving experience. While its suspension is slightly firmer than the standard LEAF, it still maintains a good balance between sportiness and comfort. However, it may transmit more road imperfections to the cabin compared to the standard LEAF.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya, being designed as a comfortable and versatile crossover, prioritizes a smoother and more refined ride. Its suspension is tuned to absorb road irregularities, providing a comfortable journey for passengers. The Ariya's setup ensures a more composed and cushioned experience, ideal for daily commuting and long-distance travel.


Handling and Agility:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: With its performance-tuned suspension and sportier characteristics, the LEAF NISMO offers improved agility and sharper handling. It boasts responsive steering and reduced body roll, making it more suitable for spirited driving and maneuvering around corners with confidence.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya's suspension tuning strikes a balance between comfort and handling prowess. While it may not match the LEAF NISMO's outright sportiness, it still delivers predictable and composed handling. The Ariya feels stable during turns and provides a confident driving experience, particularly for urban and highway driving.


Ground Clearance:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: As a performance-oriented variant, the LEAF NISMO features a slightly lower ride height compared to the standard LEAF. This lower profile contributes to improved aerodynamics and enhanced stability during high-speed driving.

Nissan Ariya: Designed as a crossover SUV, the Ariya boasts higher ground clearance, making it more versatile and capable of handling rough roads and uneven terrain. Its elevated ride height allows for better visibility and ensures a more commanding driving position.


Weight Distribution:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO's weight distribution is optimized to enhance its performance-oriented handling. It exhibits a balanced distribution, contributing to improved stability and responsive cornering.

Nissan Ariya: As a crossover SUV, the Ariya's weight distribution is also well-balanced, but it may be slightly different from the LEAF NISMO to accommodate its higher ground clearance and spacious interior layout.


Driving Modes:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO is likely to feature sport-tuned driving modes, providing different levels of suspension stiffness, throttle response, and steering feel to cater to different driving preferences.

Nissan Ariya: Similarly, the Ariya offers multiple driving modes, including a mode tailored to comfort and efficiency, allowing drivers to adjust the suspension and other vehicle parameters accordingly.


Suspension Components:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO's suspension may feature stiffer springs, performance-tuned dampers, and sportier sway bars to enhance its handling capabilities.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya's suspension components are designed to prioritize comfort while maintaining predictable handling. It may incorporate softer springs and optimized dampers to ensure a smooth and compliant ride.





Interior

Design and Layout: Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO boasts a sportier and more performance-oriented interior design. The cabin features a combination of high-quality materials and NISMO-specific accents, such as contrasting stitching, NISMO-branded seats, and unique trim elements. The layout is driver-centric, emphasizing the sporty driving experience.

Nissan Ariya: In contrast, the Ariya takes a more modern and refined approach to interior design. Its cabin exudes a sense of sophistication, with a clean and uncluttered dashboard layout. Premium materials and ambient lighting contribute to a luxurious and inviting ambiance.


Space and Seating:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO offers comfortable seating for five passengers, but it may have slightly more supportive and bolstered seats to accommodate the sporty driving characteristics. Legroom and headroom are adequate for most occupants, but the emphasis is on creating a more engaging and focused driver's environment.

Nissan Ariya: As a larger crossover SUV, the Ariya provides more generous interior space. Its cabin offers ample headroom and legroom for all passengers, ensuring a comfortable and relaxed ride, especially during longer journeys.


Infotainment System:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO's infotainment system is equipped with Nissan's latest technology, typically featuring a touchscreen display with access to essential functions, including navigation, smartphone integration, and entertainment options. It may include sporty graphics and performance-related displays.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya's infotainment system is similarly advanced, with a larger touchscreen and more extensive integration features. It may offer a more intuitive user interface and additional driver assistance features to enhance convenience and safety.


Cargo Space:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: As a performance-focused variant of the LEAF, the NISMO version may have slightly less cargo space compared to the standard LEAF due to its sportier suspension tuning and unique design elements.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya, being a crossover SUV, boasts more cargo space and flexibility. Its larger interior allows for additional storage capacity, making it well-suited for families and active lifestyles.


Interior Ambiance:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO's interior ambiance is focused on creating an energetic and sporty atmosphere. The seats and trim may feature distinctive NISMO branding, and sporty accents contribute to a dynamic driving experience.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya's interior ambiance emphasizes comfort and luxury. It exudes a more refined and premium feel, with attention to detail in the choice of materials and color combinations, providing an inviting and upscale environment.


Technology and Features:

Nissan LEAF NISMO: The LEAF NISMO may prioritize performance-oriented features, such as performance displays, a sport-tuned drive mode selector, and sporty steering wheel controls. While it includes essential tech features, the focus remains on the driving experience.

Nissan Ariya: The Ariya boasts an array of advanced technology and features, including a comprehensive infotainment system, premium audio options, and a suite of driver assistance systems. It places a strong emphasis on enhancing convenience, safety, and connectivity for all occupants.
