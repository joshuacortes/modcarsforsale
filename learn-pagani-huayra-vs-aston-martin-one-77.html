Pagani Huayra vs. Aston Martin One-77



Stock Motor

Pagani Huayra:

0-60 acceleration: Approximately 2.8 seconds

Torque: 738 lb-ft (1,000 Nm)

Horsepower: 720 hp

Top speed: 230 mph (370 km/h)

Quarter-mile time: Approximately 10.7 seconds

Lap times: Not widely available

Powertrain configuration: Rear-wheel drive

Transmission type: 7-speed sequential gearbox

Power-to-weight ratio: Approximately 526 hp/ton

RPM (shaft speed): 6,500


Aston Martin One-77:

0-60 acceleration: Approximately 3.5 seconds

Torque: 553 lb-ft (750 Nm)

Horsepower: 750 hp

Top speed: 220 mph (354 km/h)

Quarter-mile time: Approximately 11.1 seconds

Lap times: Not widely available

Powertrain configuration: Rear-wheel drive

Transmission type: 6-speed automated manual

Power-to-weight ratio: Approximately 491 hp/ton

RPM (shaft speed): 7,500


Comparative Analysis:


Acceleration and Speed:

The Pagani Huayra showcases exceptional acceleration, achieving 0-60 in approximately 2.8 seconds, compared to the Aston Martin One-77's approximately 3.5 seconds. In terms of top speed, the Huayra boasts a higher maximum speed of 230 mph, while the One-77 reaches a top speed of 220 mph.


Engine Performance:

The Pagani Huayra is powered by a 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine, producing 720 horsepower and an impressive 738 lb-ft of torque. The Aston Martin One-77 features a naturally aspirated 7.3-liter V12 engine, delivering a remarkable 750 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque.


Powertrain and Transmission:

Both cars have rear-wheel-drive configurations. The Huayra is equipped with a 7-speed sequential gearbox, enabling lightning-fast gear shifts and precise control. The One-77 incorporates a 6-speed automated manual transmission, offering smooth shifts and a refined driving experience.


Power-to-Weight Ratio:

The Pagani Huayra boasts a higher power-to-weight ratio, with approximately 526 horsepower per ton, compared to the Aston Martin One-77's roughly 491 horsepower per ton. This ratio contributes to the Huayra's impressive acceleration and overall performance.


RPM (Shaft Speed):

The Aston Martin One-77 has a higher redline, revving up to 7,500 RPM, allowing for a broader powerband and thrilling engine performance. The Pagani Huayra, with a maximum RPM of 6,500, also delivers exhilarating power and response.





Suspension

Pagani Huayra:

Design: Independent suspension with double wishbones and electronically controlled shock absorbers

Adjustability: Multi-mode adjustable suspension system

Technology: Electronically controlled active suspension, adaptive dampers, and active ride height control

Performance: Excellent handling, precise cornering, and outstanding stability


Aston Martin One-77:

Design: Independent suspension with double wishbones and adjustable coil springs

Adjustability: Three-stage adjustable suspension system

Technology: Adaptive dampers, active ride height control, and Sport mode

Performance: Dynamic handling, responsive cornering, and impressive stability


Comparative Analysis:


Suspension Design:

The Pagani Huayra utilizes an independent suspension design with double wishbones at all corners. This configuration allows for precise wheel control, offering superior handling and stability. The Aston Martin One-77 also features an independent suspension with double wishbones, providing excellent suspension geometry for enhanced handling and ride quality.


Adjustability:

The Huayra incorporates a multi-mode adjustable suspension system, allowing drivers to fine-tune the suspension settings according to their preferences. This feature enhances adaptability to different road conditions and driving styles. The One-77 offers a three-stage adjustable suspension system, providing drivers with the ability to select between different damping modes, enabling a customized driving experience.


Technology:

Both supercars incorporate advanced suspension technologies. The Huayra boasts an electronically controlled active suspension system, adaptive dampers, and active ride height control. These features adapt the suspension to changing road conditions, optimizing handling and ride comfort. The One-77 utilizes adaptive dampers, active ride height control, and a Sport mode, enhancing the car's dynamic capabilities while maintaining a refined ride quality.


Performance:

The Pagani Huayra delivers exceptional handling, precise cornering, and outstanding stability due to its advanced suspension setup. The combination of independent double wishbone suspension and electronically controlled shock absorbers provides exceptional road holding. Similarly, the Aston Martin One-77 offers dynamic handling, responsive cornering, and impressive stability, aided by its independent suspension design and adjustable coil springs.





Interior

Pagani Huayra:

Design: Exquisite attention to detail with a mix of futuristic and handcrafted elements

Materials: Hand-stitched leather, carbon fiber accents, and aluminum inserts

Technology: Advanced digital instrument cluster, multimedia system, and unique Pagani control interfaces

Comfort Features: Heated, power-adjustable seats, climate control, high-quality audio system

Ambience: A blend of modern and artisanal design, exuding exclusivity and performance-oriented luxury


Aston Martin One-77:

Design: Elegant and refined layout, combining timeless British luxury with contemporary aesthetics

Materials: Fine leather upholstery, hand-finished wood veneers, and premium metal trims

Technology: Infotainment system, premium audio system, and customizable digital displays

Comfort Features: Heated, power-adjustable seats, climate control, and a range of customizable options

Ambience: Luxurious and sophisticated environment, invoking a sense of grand touring elegance


Comparative Analysis:


Interior Design:

The Pagani Huayra showcases a design that seamlessly blends futuristic elements with handcrafted details. It features an ergonomic layout with a focus on the driver, creating an immersive and performance-oriented environment. The Aston Martin One-77 exudes elegance and refinement, with a sophisticated and driver-centric design that reflects British luxury at its finest.


Material Quality:

Both supercars utilize premium materials to create a lavish and tactile experience. The Pagani Huayra incorporates hand-stitched leather, carbon fiber accents, and aluminum inserts, combining performance-focused materials with meticulous craftsmanship. The Aston Martin One-77 features fine leather upholstery, hand-finished wood veneers, and premium metal trims, exuding traditional luxury and attention to detail.


Technology:

The Pagani Huayra incorporates advanced technology, including an advanced digital instrument cluster, a multimedia system, and unique Pagani control interfaces. While it provides essential features, the focus remains on creating an engaging and driver-centric experience. The Aston Martin One-77 offers an infotainment system, a premium audio system, and customizable digital displays, balancing modern technology with traditional craftsmanship.


Comfort Features:

Both supercars prioritize driver and passenger comfort during exhilarating drives. The Pagani Huayra offers heated, power-adjustable seats, climate control, and a high-quality audio system, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable ride. The Aston Martin One-77 provides similar features, including heated, power-adjustable seats, climate control, and a range of customizable options to tailor the interior to personal preferences.


Ambience:

The Pagani Huayra creates an ambience that combines modern aesthetics with artisanal craftsmanship, emphasizing exclusivity and a performance-oriented luxury experience. The Aston Martin One-77 presents a luxurious and sophisticated environment, invoking a sense of grand touring elegance and timeless British heritage
