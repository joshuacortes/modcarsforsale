BMW E36



BMW 3 Series (E36)

The third generation of the BMW 3 Series line of small executive vehicles, known as the BMW E36, was manufactured by the German automaker BMW from 1990 to 2000. The four-door sedan body style was used for the first models, then in subsequent years, the coupe, convertible, wagon ("Touring"), hatchback ("Compact"), and the uncommon four-door convertible Baur TC4 were added.


Body Styles

1990-1998: 4-Door Sedan
1990-1999: 2-Door Coupe
1993-1999: 2-Door Convertible
1994-1995: 5-Door Wagon/Touring
1994-2000: 3-Door Hatchback

Drivetrain

1994-1999: 5 Speed Manual
1996-1999: 6 Speed Manual
4 Speed Automatic
5 Speed Automatic

All models are rear-wheel drive.


Modified BMW E36

Performance Enhancements:

Engine Upgrades: The E36's inline-six engine is known for its potential. Performance upgrades can range from simple modifications like cold air intakes and exhaust systems to more extensive swaps and forced induction. A supercharged or turbocharged E36 can yield substantial power gains.

Suspension Tuning: Enhancing the suspension is crucial for improving handling. Upgrades like adjustable coilovers, sway bars, and polyurethane bushings can significantly transform the car's cornering abilities, reducing body roll and enhancing grip.

Brake Systems: Upgrading the braking system with larger rotors, high-performance pads, and stainless steel lines enhances stopping power, making it safer and more suitable for spirited driving.


Aftermarket Support:

The BMW E36 enjoys a robust aftermarket support system. Enthusiasts can easily find a wide range of parts and accessories, including engine components, suspension upgrades, body kits, and more. This extensive support makes it relatively straightforward to personalize and enhance your E36 to your liking.


Design Upgrades:

Body Kits: Aesthetic customization can transform your E36's appearance. Body kits from manufacturers like M Tech and AC Schnitzer can add aggressive styling, improve aerodynamics, and give your car a distinct look.

Wheels and Tires: The right set of wheels can significantly impact your E36's design and performance. A new set of rims, accompanied by performance-oriented tires, can enhance both the look and grip of your car.

Interior Modifications: Personalizing the interior with racing seats, custom steering wheels, and modern audio systems can elevate the driving experience and add a touch of luxury or sportiness to your E36.


Motorsports Success:

The BMW E36 has a rich history in motorsports. Its balanced chassis and tunable engine make it a popular choice in various racing disciplines. Whether it's drifting, touring car racing, or amateur track days, the E36 has seen considerable success on the racetrack. Many enthusiasts turn to the E36 for its winning potential and continue its racing legacy.


Driving Experience:

Improved Handling: With the right modifications, the E36's handling can be transformed. Upgraded suspension components and wider wheels enhance grip and make the car feel more planted in corners.

Enhanced Power: Engine upgrades not only increase power but also provide an exhilarating driving experience. The improved throttle response and acceleration make every drive more enjoyable.

Customized Style: Personalizing the E36's design and interior allows you to make a statement on the road. Whether you prefer a sleek, understated look or an aggressive, track-inspired appearance, the E36 can be tailored to your style.


Modifying a BMW E36 is not just about improving its performance; it's about enhancing the overall driving experience and making a statement. With a wide range of performance upgrades, a supportive aftermarket community, and a legacy of motorsports success, the E36 offers a canvas for enthusiasts to create a car that reflects their individual style and driving aspirations. Whether you prioritize track performance, daily driving enjoyment, or a blend of both, the E36 is a versatile and thrilling platform for modification.

COMMON BMW E36 MODIFICATIONS:

Performance Upgrades:

The E36 is already known for its impressive driving dynamics, but for those seeking more power and agility, various performance upgrades are available:

a. Engine Modifications: Consider upgrading your engine with performance-enhancing modifications like cold air intakes, exhaust systems, and ECU tuning. These changes can boost horsepower and torque, resulting in a more exhilarating driving experience.

b. Suspension and Handling: Upgrading your suspension with components such as coilovers, sway bars, and performance bushings can significantly improve the E36's handling characteristics. A lower stance and improved road-holding capabilities can turn your car into a track-ready beast.

c. Brake Upgrades: Performance brake kits with larger rotors and better brake pads can enhance stopping power, making your E36 more capable on the street or track.


Aftermarket Support:

The E36 has a robust aftermarket community that provides a wide range of options for customization:

a. Body Kits: Enhance the visual appeal of your E36 with aftermarket body kits, including front splitters, rear diffusers, and spoilers, to give your car a more aggressive and aerodynamic look.

b. Wheels and Tires: Upgrading your wheels and tires is a great way to improve the E36's stance and handling. Custom alloy wheels and high-performance tires not only enhance aesthetics but also contribute to improved traction and cornering abilities.

c. Interior Upgrades: Personalize your cabin with aftermarket seats, steering wheels, shift knobs, and gauges. These modifications not only improve aesthetics but also provide a more comfortable and driver-focused experience.


Design Enhancements:

E36 owners often appreciate the car's timeless design, and modifications can help you make it even more unique:

a. Custom Paint: Consider a fresh coat of paint or vinyl wraps to create a one-of-a-kind appearance. Custom paint jobs can range from subtle and classic to bold and eye-catching.

b. Lighting Upgrades: Modern LED or HID headlights, taillights, and interior lighting can provide a significant visual improvement while also enhancing nighttime visibility.


Motorsports Success:

The E36 has a rich history in motorsports, particularly in touring car racing. Many modifications can prepare your E36 for the track:

a. Roll Cages: Safety is paramount on the track. Installing a roll cage ensures added protection in case of an accident and often meets motorsport regulations.

b. Performance Exhaust Systems: A high-performance exhaust system can improve engine breathing and enhance the exhaust note, making your car sound and perform like a true racing machine.

c. Racing Seats and Harnesses: Upgrade to racing seats and harnesses for improved support and safety during high-speed driving.


Driving Experience:

Ultimately, all these modifications contribute to a superior driving experience:

a. Sound System: Enhance your driving experience with a quality sound system that complements the car's interior while delivering outstanding audio quality.

b. Comfort and Convenience: Upgrade the interior with modern conveniences like a touchscreen infotainment system, air conditioning, and power accessories to enjoy a comfortable ride, even after significant modifications.


Modifying a BMW E36 offers a world of possibilities to transform it into your dream car. Whether you're seeking improved performance, a unique aesthetic, or motorsports readiness, the E36's versatility and the rich aftermarket support make it a prime candidate for customization. Just remember that safety, quality, and attention to detail should always be top priorities when embarking on the journey of E36 modification. With the right modifications, your BMW E36 can become a true masterpiece that delivers an exhilarating driving experience on the road and the track.




BMW E36 Turbo

Precision Turbo & Engine - PT6266 Turbocharger

The Precision PT6266 is a popular choice among BMW E36 owners looking for serious power gains. This turbocharger features a ball-bearing design for quick spooling and is available in various configurations to match your power goals. It offers excellent response and a wide powerband, making it suitable for both street and track use.


BorgWarner EFR 6758 Turbocharger

The BorgWarner EFR 6758 is a well-regarded turbocharger known for its exceptional spool time and transient response. It's a great choice for those who want to maintain low-end torque while benefiting from turbocharged top-end power. The integrated wastegate design and modern engineering make it a reliable and efficient option for the BMW E36.


Garrett GTX3576R Gen II Turbocharger

Garrett is a name synonymous with turbocharging, and the GTX3576R Gen II is a fantastic choice for the E36. It offers incredible performance potential with its dual ball-bearing design, and the Gen II improvements make it even more efficient. This turbocharger is suitable for high-power builds and can be customized to fit your specific needs.


Precision Turbo & Engine - PT5558 Turbocharger

For those seeking an excellent balance between response and top-end power, the PT5558 from Precision Turbo & Engine is an attractive option. It offers a good compromise between spool time and peak performance, making it versatile for both street and track applications. The PT5558 is available in various trims to accommodate different horsepower goals.


Garrett GT3071R Turbocharger

The Garrett GT3071R is known for its excellent mid-range power delivery. If you're interested in a responsive setup that delivers substantial gains in the mid-range RPMs, this turbocharger is worth considering. Its dual ball-bearing design ensures reliability and longevity.


Compatibility and Supporting Mods

When selecting a turbocharger for your BMW E36, it's essential to consider compatibility with your engine, exhaust, and fuel system. You may need to make supporting modifications to achieve optimal performance and reliability. Some key modifications include:


Upgraded Fuel Injectors: Larger fuel injectors are often necessary to supply the increased fuel required for turbocharged applications.

High-Flow Exhaust: A free-flowing exhaust system is essential to maximize the benefits of a turbocharger. Consider a high-quality downpipe and a larger exhaust system.

Intercooler: An efficient intercooler helps reduce intake air temperatures, improving power and engine safety.


ECU Tuning: Custom engine tuning is crucial to ensure your E36 runs smoothly with the new turbocharger. Consider a reputable tuner with experience in BMW E36 turbo builds.


Adding a turbocharger to your BMW E36 can transform it into a high-performance machine, taking your driving experience to the next level. The options mentioned above represent some of the best turbochargers available for the E36, each with its unique characteristics and advantages. When selecting a turbocharger, consider your power goals, driving preferences, and budget. Remember that proper installation and supporting modifications are key to unlocking the full potential of your turbocharged E36, so consult with experts and reputable mechanics to ensure a successful and thrilling transformation.

Compatibility and Supporting Mods

Before selecting a suspension upgrade for your BMW E36, it's crucial to consider your specific driving needs and preferences. Additionally, other modifications may be necessary to ensure that the suspension components work optimally:


Upgraded Bushings: Replacing suspension bushings with polyurethane or solid options can further enhance handling precision.

Alignment and Corner Balancing: After installing new suspension components, it's essential to have a professional alignment and corner balancing to maximize performance and tire wear.

Tires and Wheels: Your choice of tires and wheels can significantly impact the handling and ride quality of your E36. Be sure to select compatible options that suit your suspension setup.


Upgrading your BMW E36's suspension can significantly improve its performance, handling, and overall driving experience. When selecting a suspension upgrade, consider your driving preferences, budget, and intended use. Hawk Performance's HP Plus Brake Pads are well-regarded for their track-ready performance without sacrificing daily drivability. These pads offer excellent bite, fade resistance, and high-temperature stability, making them ideal for E36 owners who participate in track days or autocross events.

Compatibility and Supporting Mods

Before selecting a brake upgrade for your BMW E36, it's crucial to consider compatibility with your existing brake system. Depending on the specific kit or components you choose, you may need to make additional modifications:

Brake Lines: Consider upgrading to stainless steel brake lines to enhance brake pedal feel and reduce brake line expansion under heavy braking.

Brake Calipers: Upgrading to larger calipers may require modification to your vehicle's suspension components or wheels to ensure a proper fit.

Brake Master Cylinder: In some cases, a larger brake master cylinder may be needed to provide the necessary hydraulic pressure for improved braking performance.


Enhancing your BMW E36's braking system is a wise investment in safety and performance. The brake options listed above represent some of the best choices available, each catering to different driving preferences and requirements. When choosing a brake upgrade, consider your driving style, budget, and intended use, whether it's spirited street driving, track days, or a combination of both. Professional installation and a thorough brake system flush are essential to ensure a seamless and effective upgrade. With the right brake components, you can optimize your E36's stopping power, ensuring safer and more exhilarating drives.
