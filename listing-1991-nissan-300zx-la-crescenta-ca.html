Title Status: Clean

Fully optioned when new with Automatic climate control, leather seats, and Bose sound system. The sound system has been upgraded since with Pioneer head unit, upgraded speakers, and also a Alpine rear subwoofer. Vehicle has been tastefully modified with good quality parts. Full 2.5" stainless steel AMS exhaust system with titanium burnt tips.
