The Racing Experience At the core of MIS's allure lies its meticulously designed half-mile oval track. Whether it's the roar of powerful stock cars or the finesse of late models, every lap on the MIS oval is a demonstration of precision, strategy, and unyielding determination. Common Events at Madison International Speedway ARCA Midwest Tour Races: MIS regularly hosts races as part of the ARCA Midwest Tour, featuring a competitive field of late model stock cars. These events showcase top-tier talent and attract fans from across the region. Weekly Racing Series: MIS is known for its weekly racing series, featuring various stock car divisions that provide local racers with a platform to compete and hone their skills. These events offer fans an opportunity to witness thrilling wheel-to-wheel action. Super Late Model Events: The speedway hosts super late model races, attracting some of the best drivers in the region to compete in high-stakes battles on the half-mile track. Specialty Race Nights: MIS organizes specialty race nights, such as the "Salute to America" event, which includes fireworks and festivities to celebrate the Fourth of July weekend. Kids' Nights and Family Events: The track embraces family-friendly entertainment with kids' nights, allowing young fans to engage with the world of motorsports and meet their favorite drivers. Enduro and Figure 8 Races: MIS occasionally features enduro races and figure 8 events, adding a unique and thrilling dimension to the racing calendar.
