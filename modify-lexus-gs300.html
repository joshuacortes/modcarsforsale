Lexus GS300

The GS300 was an executive vehicle offered by Lexus, Toyota's high-end division. In Japan, the Toyota Aristo had been replaced by the same vehicle in 1991. It was introduced as the Lexus GS for non-Japanese markets in February 1993. For the Japanese market, it kept retaining the Toyota Aristo moniker until January 2005.

The GS is a mid-luxury performance sedan that competes in the market between the small executive IS and large/flagship LS. Up until 2011, it shared a chassis with one of Toyota's most enduring nameplates, the Toyota Crown premium sedans.

Modifying a Lexus GS300, building a showcar/project drift car, racecar or anything in between? -Be aware of the aftermarket ability and overall support available for your GS300 build.

The Lexus GS300, a symbol of luxury and refinement, often leaves its factory with a reputation for comfort and elegance. But for some automotive enthusiasts, it's just the beginning of a journey toward unlocking its hidden potential. In this article, we'll explore the world of modifying the Lexus GS300, delving into categories like performance, aftermarket support, design, motorsports history, driving experience, and additional topics to understand how enthusiasts are elevating this luxury sedan into a performance powerhouse.


Performance: Unleashing Hidden Power

Modifying the Lexus GS300 often starts with a quest for more power and agility. Enthusiasts explore various performance upgrades, including engine tuning, turbocharging, or supercharging to boost horsepower and torque. With the right modifications, the GS300 can go from a comfortable cruiser to a high-performance sports sedan. These improvements turn the car into a formidable force on the road, delivering thrilling acceleration and impressive top speeds.


Aftermarket Support: A World of Possibilities

One of the advantages of modifying a Lexus GS300 is the availability of aftermarket parts and support. From suspension upgrades to performance exhaust systems, and even body kits, a wide range of options exists to tailor the GS300 to your specific preferences. Brands like AEM, Eibach, and Borla offer a variety of performance parts, making it easier than ever for enthusiasts to transform their GS300.


Design: Creating an Aesthetic Masterpiece

Enhancing the GS300's visual appeal is a vital part of the modification journey. Body kits, custom wheels, and unique paint schemes allow owners to create a one-of-a-kind aesthetic that not only turns heads but also improves aerodynamics. This combination of style and performance is a key element of the GS300 modification process, turning the luxury sedan into a true showstopper.


Motorsports Success/History: A Track-Ready Transformation

The Lexus GS300 has seen its share of motorsports success, especially in drifting competitions. Many enthusiasts take inspiration from professional drifters who have transformed the GS300 into formidable drift machines. This history of track success serves as motivation for those who want to take their own GS300 to the racetrack and experience the thrill of sliding through corners with precision.


Driving Experience: Handling and Suspension Upgrades

Modifying the GS300 isn't just about power; it's also about improving its handling and driving experience. Upgrades such as stiffer suspension components, high-performance brakes, and lightweight wheels help the GS300 tackle corners with confidence and precision. The result is a transformed luxury sedan that delivers a thrilling and dynamic driving experience.


Additional Topics: Customization and Reliability

Additional aspects of GS300 modification include customizing the interior with high-quality materials and technology upgrades. Moreover, maintaining the reliability of the vehicle is a priority. Enthusiasts often strike a balance between performance improvements and preserving the GS300's renowned Lexus reliability, ensuring that their modified sedan remains a dependable daily driver.

Modifying a Lexus GS300 is a journey that allows enthusiasts to unlock the car's hidden potential, transforming it into a high-performance machine that excels in both power and style. The aftermarket support, motorsports history, and driving experience all play crucial roles in elevating the GS300, proving that this luxury sedan can indeed be a canvas for automotive excellence.

Common Lexus GS300 Modifications:

Several common modifications have become popular among GS300 owners over the years. One of the most prominent changes is the installation of aftermarket wheels and tires. These upgrades not only give the car a more striking and aggressive appearance but also improve handling and grip on the road. Larger wheels provide a sportier look, while performance-oriented tires enhance traction, ensuring that the GS300 feels more connected to the road. Additionally, many enthusiasts opt for suspension upgrades, such as coilovers, sway bars, and strut bars, to transform the car's handling characteristics. These modifications lower the vehicle's center of gravity and enhance cornering, creating a more engaging driving experience. However, it's crucial to strike a balance between improved performance and maintaining ride comfort, as overly aggressive suspension setups can compromise the smooth and luxurious ride for which the GS300 is known.

Another sought-after modification for the Lexus GS300 is upgrading the exhaust system. The stock exhaust is designed to maintain a quiet and refined cabin environment, but many enthusiasts desire a more aggressive exhaust note and increased performance. Aftermarket exhaust systems crafted from materials like stainless steel provide a deeper and more resonant sound, giving the GS300 a sportier character. These systems often reduce exhaust restrictions, leading to noticeable improvements in horsepower and torque. The combination of a more potent engine note and enhanced performance creates a more exhilarating driving experience. However, choosing a reputable brand is essential to ensure proper filtration and prevent potential issues. These common modifications allow Lexus GS300 owners to customize their vehicles in ways that reflect their personalities and enhance their overall driving experience, whether through visual appeal, improved handling, or enhanced engine performance.




Lexus GS300 Turbocharger

Precision Turbochargers: Precision Turbo offers a range of turbochargers known for their quality and performance. Models like the Precision Turbo 6266, 6262, or 5858 are popular choices for the GS300. These turbos come in various sizes and can provide a substantial increase in power.

Garrett Turbochargers: Garrett is a renowned brand in the turbocharging industry. Turbos like the Garrett GT35R, GT3582R, or GT3071R are often used for GS300 applications. Garrett turbos are known for their reliability and performance.

BorgWarner EFR Series: The BorgWarner EFR (Engineered for Racing) series turbos are highly regarded for their advanced technology and efficiency. Models like the BorgWarner EFR 6258 or 6758 are popular for GS300 enthusiasts seeking both power and responsiveness.

Turbonetics: Turbonetics offers a variety of turbochargers suitable for the GS300. The T3/T4 Hybrid turbos and the GTK series are options to consider, offering a balance between power and response.


It's essential to note that choosing the best turbocharger for your Lexus GS300 involves more than just selecting a specific model. You should consult with a professional tuner or mechanic who can assess your engine's current setup, your desired power levels, and your budget. They can help you select the appropriate turbocharger and plan the necessary supporting modifications, such as an intercooler, exhaust, and engine management system, to ensure your GS300 operates efficiently and reliably with the new turbo.

Keep in mind that adding a turbocharger is a significant modification, and it's important to ensure it is installed correctly and professionally tuned to maximize performance and reliability while avoiding potential issues.




Lexus GS300 Suspension

Coilovers: Coilover suspension systems offer a great deal of adjustability. You can fine-tune ride height, damping, and rebound settings to tailor the suspension to your preferences. Brands like KW, Tein, and BC Racing offer high-quality coilovers that can improve both ride quality and handling performance.

Air Suspension: Air suspension provides the ultimate in ride height adjustability. It allows you to lower the car for an aggressive stance when parked and raise it for a smoother ride when driving. Brands like Air Lift Performance and AccuAir offer well-engineered air suspension kits for the Lexus GS300.

OEM-Plus Suspension: Some Lexus GS300 enthusiasts prefer retaining a near-factory ride quality while improving handling. Upgrading to high-quality OEM-style replacement components, such as KYB shocks or Eibach lowering springs, can provide a subtle performance boost without compromising comfort significantly.

Sport Suspension Kits: Brands like Bilstein and Koni offer sport suspension kits that combine improved handling with a comfortable ride. These kits are often designed to work seamlessly with the factory suspension geometry and may include dampers and springs specifically tuned for sportier driving.

Adjustable Sway Bars and Strut Bars: These suspension components can enhance handling and reduce body roll. Brands like Hotchkis and Eibach offer adjustable sway bars and strut bars that are easy to install and can make a noticeable difference in cornering performance.

Lexus F Sport Suspension: If you're looking for an option with factory support and reliability, consider the F Sport suspension from Lexus itself. This suspension setup is designed to provide a sportier feel while maintaining a high level of ride comfort. It's a suitable option for GS300 owners who prefer the OEM experience with an added sporty touch.


Ultimately, the best suspension for your Lexus GS300 will depend on your individual driving preferences and the specific characteristics you want to enhance in your vehicle. It's crucial to consult with an experienced suspension specialist or a professional mechanic to help you determine the ideal suspension setup that aligns with your goals, budget, and expected usage of the car.
