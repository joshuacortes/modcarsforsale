BMW i8 vs Audi R8



Stock Motor

BMW i8:

0-60 Acceleration: The BMW i8 accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in approximately 4.2 seconds, offering impressive acceleration for a plug-in hybrid sports car.

Torque: The i8's stock motor generates around 420 lb-ft of torque, providing strong low-end power for swift acceleration.

Horsepower: The combined output of the i8's gasoline engine and electric motor is approximately 369 horsepower, offering a balanced blend of performance and efficiency.

Top Speed: The BMW i8 can reach a top speed of around 155 mph, making it a capable high-speed cruiser.

Quarter Mile Time: The i8 completes the quarter-mile run in approximately 12.2 seconds, showcasing its respectable straight-line performance.

Lap Times: While not designed for track dominance, the i8 delivers competitive lap times due to its electric torque and handling capabilities.

Powertrain Configuration: The BMW i8 features a plug-in hybrid powertrain with a 1.5-liter turbocharged three-cylinder gasoline engine and an electric motor.

Transmission Type: The i8 comes with a six-speed automatic transmission for seamless gear changes.

Power-to-Weight Ratio: With a curb weight of around 3,500 lbs and a horsepower output of 369 hp, the i8 achieves a commendable power-to-weight ratio.

RPM (Shaft Speed): The i8's gasoline engine can rev up to approximately 6,500 RPM, while the electric motor operates at significantly higher RPMs.



Audi R8:

0-60 Acceleration: The Audi R8 accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in approximately 3.5 seconds, showcasing its quick and exhilarating performance.

Torque: The R8's stock motor generates around 413 lb-ft of torque, providing strong and linear power delivery.

Horsepower: The Audi R8 boasts approximately 562 horsepower from its naturally aspirated V10 engine, offering a thrilling driving experience.

Top Speed: The R8 can reach a top speed of around 205 mph, making it a high-performance sports car.

Quarter Mile Time: The R8 completes the quarter-mile run in approximately 11.4 seconds, highlighting its exceptional straight-line speed.

Lap Times: The Audi R8 is renowned for its track prowess, delivering impressive lap times due to its performance-oriented design.

Powertrain Configuration: The Audi R8 features a mid-engine layout with a naturally aspirated 5.2-liter V10 engine, providing a thrilling driving experience.

Transmission Type: The R8 comes with a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, offering lightning-fast gear changes.

Power-to-Weight Ratio: With a curb weight of around 3,700 lbs and a horsepower output of 562 hp, the R8 achieves an excellent power-to-weight ratio.

RPM (Shaft Speed): The Audi R8's V10 engine can rev up to approximately 8,700 RPM, delivering exhilarating engine sounds and high-revving performance.





Suspension

Suspension of BMW i8:


Adaptive Suspension:

The BMW i8 comes equipped with an adaptive suspension system that continuously adjusts damping rates based on road conditions and driving dynamics.

This adaptive feature provides a comfortable ride for daily driving while enhancing handling during spirited driving.


Lightweight Construction:

The i8's suspension components are constructed with lightweight materials such as aluminum and carbon-fiber-reinforced plastic.

This reduces unsprung weight, contributing to improved agility and responsiveness.


Electronic Damper Control:

The i8's adaptive suspension system is complemented by electronic damper control, which further enhances ride comfort and stability.


Front Double Wishbone Suspension:

At the front, the BMW i8 features a double wishbone suspension, which provides precise wheel control and improves handling.


Rear Multi-Link Suspension:

The rear suspension of the i8 is a multi-link design, ensuring optimal stability and traction during acceleration and cornering.


Low Ground Clearance (Optional):

Some models of the i8 offer an optional feature to lower the ground clearance, improving aerodynamics and enhancing high-speed stability.



Suspension of Audi R8:

Magnetic Ride Suspension (Optional):

The Audi R8 offers an optional magnetic ride suspension system, utilizing magnetorheological dampers that adjust the suspension's firmness in milliseconds.

This feature allows the driver to select between comfort and sport modes, tailoring the suspension setup to their preferences.


Aluminum Double Wishbone Suspension:

The Audi R8 boasts an aluminum double wishbone suspension setup at both the front and rear axles.

This design provides precise wheel control, offering exceptional handling and stability.


Performance-Oriented Tuning:

The R8's suspension is tuned to prioritize sportiness and dynamic driving characteristics, making it an excellent choice for enthusiasts.


Quattro All-Wheel-Drive System:

The Audi R8's legendary quattro all-wheel-drive system complements its suspension, providing outstanding traction and grip on various road conditions.


Fixed Ground Clearance:

Unlike the BMW i8, the Audi R8 does not offer adjustable ground clearance, maintaining a fixed ride height.



Comparison:

Ride Comfort - BMW i8:

The i8's adaptive suspension provides a comfortable and refined ride for daily driving, while its lightweight construction contributes to nimble handling.

Ride Comfort - Audi R8:

With the optional magnetic ride suspension, the Audi R8 offers a range of comfort and sport modes, allowing drivers to tailor the ride to their liking.

Handling - BMW i8:

The i8's adaptive suspension, combined with lightweight construction, offers a balanced blend of comfort and sportiness, making it suitable for both cruising and spirited driving.

Handling - Audi R8:

The R8's performance-oriented suspension tuning, coupled with its quattro all-wheel-drive system, delivers precise handling and exceptional grip, emphasizing its track capabilities.





Interior

Interior of BMW i8:


Futuristic Design:

The interior of the BMW i8 exudes a futuristic vibe with modern lines, sleek surfaces, and a minimalist approach.

It reflects the car's forward-thinking hybrid technology and emphasizes its status as an eco-friendly sports car.


Premium Materials:

The i8 boasts high-quality materials, including leather, carbon fiber, and sustainable luxury materials like recycled plastics and renewable fabrics.

These materials add to the car's eco-conscious image while providing a luxurious feel to the cabin.


Driver-Oriented Cockpit:

The i8's cockpit is driver-focused, with the center console angled towards the driver for easy access to controls.

This layout enhances the driving experience, emphasizing the car's sporty character.


Digital Instrument Cluster:

The BMW i8 features a digital instrument cluster with customizable display options.

It provides vital information and can show energy flow, battery charge levels, and driving efficiency data.


Infotainment System:

The i8's infotainment system features a high-resolution touchscreen display with BMW's iDrive interface.

It supports smartphone integration, navigation, and various connectivity options.


Comfortable Seating:

The i8's seats are comfortable and supportive, with options for premium leather upholstery and electric adjustment.


Interior of Audi R8:

Performance-Oriented Design:

The Audi R8's interior showcases a performance-oriented design, combining luxurious materials with a driver-centric layout.

The focus is on providing a captivating and exhilarating driving experience.


Fine Craftsmanship:

The R8 features premium materials, including leather, Alcantara, and real carbon fiber trim.

The craftsmanship emphasizes Audi's commitment to luxury and attention to detail.


Virtual Cockpit:

The R8 comes with Audi's virtual cockpit, a fully digital instrument cluster that displays essential information and can be customized to the driver's preferences.

It provides a high-tech and visually stunning interface.


Audi MMI Infotainment System:

The R8's infotainment system includes the Audi MMI interface, featuring a high-resolution display with touch and voice control capabilities.

The system supports various smartphone integrations, navigation, and multimedia options.


Sport Seats:

The Audi R8 features sport seats with ample bolstering, designed to keep occupants secure during spirited driving.

They are available with various upholstery options, including Nappa leather and diamond-stitched patterns.

Bang & Olufsen Sound System (Optional):

The R8 offers an optional Bang & Olufsen premium sound system, delivering exceptional audio quality for an immersive driving experience.


Comparison:

Interior Design - BMW i8:

The i8's interior is futuristic and eco-conscious, reflecting its hybrid technology and green philosophy.

Interior Design - Audi R8:

The R8's interior emphasizes performance and luxury, showcasing fine craftsmanship and driver-centric elements.

Infotainment System - BMW i8:

The i8's infotainment system features BMW's iDrive interface, offering a user-friendly experience with customizable display options.

Infotainment System - Audi R8:

The R8's infotainment system includes Audi's MMI interface, known for its seamless integration and intuitive controls.

Seats - BMW i8:

The i8's seats are comfortable and can be upholstered with premium materials, offering a balance of luxury and sustainability.

Seats - Audi R8:

The R8's sport seats provide ample support and are available with luxurious upholstery options for an elevated driving experience.
