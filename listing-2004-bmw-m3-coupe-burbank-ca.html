2004 BMW m3 Coupe 2D


Location: Burbank, CA

Title Status: Clean

I am selling me 2004 BMW E46 M3. The car is an absolute joy to drive, and looks the part as well; I get compliments all the time everywhere I go. It handles like a dream, stays planted to the ground and feels exactly the same going 20mph as it does 100mph.

I have had the car for 6 years and in that time have invested over 20k into it. Mechanically the car is flawless. The engine was completely rebuilt about 10k miles ago by Castro Motorsports and Minicorsa, everything was replaced with OEM or better variants including the Dr Vanos kit. Two out of the big three are done, the Vanos has been upgraded as just mentioned, the rod bearings were obviously re done when the engine was completely rebuilt, the rear subframe reinforcement has not been done but I do have quotes from local reputable shops to have it done if the new owner wants to do that.

Cosmetically the car looks great too, there are a few non steel parts of the car where the paint has faded (rear bumper, hood, roof rails area). All of the essential and steel portions of the body are still in great shape, no dents, no dings, no scratches etc. A simple respray on a few parts and the car would be absolutely mint. The interior was dove grey from the factory, I completely swapped the interior to schwartz with Impulse Cloth seats (Rare in the US); every single piece that is required for the swap was done including a large portion of parts being brand new if I could not source an acceptable condition used part.

I am asking $32k obo for the car as it is. I am not looking to part out or sell separately or with different wheels etc. The car comes as is or not at all. I do have the stock rear diffuser and spoiler that will be included with the sale, as well as a binder full of all documentation I have on everything that has been done to the car since I purchased it. If it does not sell on here, I will most likely put it up on BAT.
