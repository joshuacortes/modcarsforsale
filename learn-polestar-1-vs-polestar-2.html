Polestar 1 vs. Polestar 2



Stock Motor

Polestar 1:


0-60 Acceleration:

The Polestar 1 accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in approximately 4.2 seconds, showcasing its impressive electric and gasoline-powered performance.


Torque:

The Polestar 1 produces a combined torque output of around 738 lb-ft, providing instant acceleration and thrilling power delivery.


Horsepower:

The stock motor of the Polestar 1 generates approximately 619 horsepower, making it a true high-performance grand tourer.


Top Speed:

The Polestar 1 boasts an electronically limited top speed of 155 mph, ensuring controlled and exhilarating performance.


Quarter Mile Time:

The Polestar 1 completes the quarter-mile run in approximately 12.4 seconds, demonstrating its rapid acceleration capabilities.


Lap Times:

As a grand tourer, the Polestar 1 excels in long-distance driving comfort rather than setting record lap times.



Powertrain Configuration:

The Polestar 1 is a plug-in hybrid electric vehicle (PHEV) featuring an electric rear axle and a front-mounted internal combustion engine, providing both all-electric and hybrid driving modes.


Transmission Type:

The Polestar 1 is equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission, contributing to smooth gear shifts and optimum power delivery.


Power-to-Weight Ratio:

With a curb weight of around 5,200 lbs and a horsepower output of 619 hp, the Polestar 1 achieves a commendable power-to-weight ratio for its size.


RPM (Shaft Speed):

The Polestar 1's electric rear axle can rev up to approximately 13,000 RPM, offering quick acceleration from a standstill.



Polestar 2:


0-60 Acceleration:

The Polestar 2 accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in approximately 4.7 seconds, delivering impressive electric performance.


Torque:

The Polestar 2 produces 487 lb-ft of torque, offering robust and immediate acceleration from its electric powertrain.


Horsepower:

The stock motor of the Polestar 2 generates approximately 408 horsepower, providing a powerful electric driving experience.


Top Speed:

The Polestar 2 is electronically limited to a top speed of 127 mph, ensuring controlled performance in line with its electric focus.


Quarter Mile Time:

The Polestar 2 completes the quarter-mile run in approximately 13.1 seconds, showcasing its spirited electric performance.


Lap Times:

While not designed as a dedicated track car, the Polestar 2's electric powertrain delivers instant torque and responsiveness for an enjoyable driving experience.


Powertrain Configuration:

The Polestar 2 is a fully electric vehicle (BEV) featuring dual electric motors - one on each axle - providing all-wheel-drive capability.


Transmission Type:

As an electric vehicle, the Polestar 2 uses a single-speed direct-drive transmission, eliminating the need for traditional gear shifting.


Power-to-Weight Ratio:

With a curb weight of around 4,680 lbs and a horsepower output of 408 hp, the Polestar 2 achieves a respectable power-to-weight ratio for an electric vehicle.


RPM (Shaft Speed):

The Polestar 2's electric motors can rev up to approximately 16,000 RPM, showcasing the high-revving nature of electric powertrains.





Suspension

Polestar 1 Suspension:

Double Wishbone Front Suspension: The Polestar 1 is equipped with a double wishbone front suspension, providing precise wheel control and handling responsiveness.

Integral Link Rear Suspension: At the rear, the Polestar 1 utilizes an integral link suspension, contributing to a smoother ride and enhanced stability during spirited driving.

Adaptive Suspension System: The Polestar 1 features an advanced adaptive suspension system, offering multiple drive modes for various driving conditions.

Continuous Electronic Damping: The adaptive suspension system continuously adjusts damping based on road conditions and driver inputs, ensuring optimal handling and comfort.

Carbon Fiber Reinforcements: To reduce weight and enhance rigidity, the Polestar 1's suspension components feature carbon fiber reinforcements, improving overall performance.

Adjustable Ride Height: The Polestar 1 offers an adjustable ride height, allowing drivers to lower the car for improved aerodynamics or raise it for better ground clearance.

Performance-oriented Handling: The Polestar 1's suspension tuning prioritizes sportiness and cornering capabilities, making it a dynamic grand tourer.


Polestar 2 Suspension:

Front MacPherson Strut: The Polestar 2 is equipped with a front MacPherson strut suspension, providing a simple and cost-effective design for a high-volume electric vehicle.

Rear Integral Link: At the rear, the Polestar 2 features an integral link suspension, offering a balance of ride comfort and handling.

Standard Fixed Damping: Unlike the Polestar 1, the Polestar 2's suspension features standard fixed damping without an adaptive system.

Steel Suspension Components: To optimize cost and achieve mass production, the Polestar 2's suspension components are made of steel rather than carbon fiber.

Single Motor AWD System: The Polestar 2's electric powertrain includes a single motor on each axle, providing all-wheel-drive capability for enhanced traction.

Comfort-oriented Ride: The Polestar 2's suspension tuning emphasizes ride comfort, making it an ideal choice for daily commuting and long-distance driving.

High Ground Clearance: The Polestar 2 offers higher ground clearance, providing practicality for navigating urban road conditions and speed bumps.

Responsive Electric Power Steering: Both the Polestar 1 and Polestar 2 feature responsive electric power steering, offering precise control and feedback.





Interior

Polestar 1 Interior:

Grand Touring Ambience: The Polestar 1's interior exudes a sense of grand touring luxury, offering a premium and upscale atmosphere.

High-Quality Materials: The Polestar 1 boasts premium materials, such as Nappa leather, carbon fiber accents, and metal trims, adding to its luxurious feel.

Four-Seat Layout: The Polestar 1 features a 2+2 seating arrangement, with individual sport seats for all passengers, ensuring comfort and support during spirited driving.

Handcrafted Details: The Polestar 1's interior includes handcrafted elements, showcasing meticulous attention to detail.

Advanced Infotainment System: The Polestar 1 is equipped with an advanced infotainment system, offering a large touchscreen display and the latest connectivity features.

Driver-Centric Cockpit: The Polestar 1's cockpit design is focused on the driver, providing easy access to essential controls and enhancing the driving experience.

Crystal Gear Selector: The Polestar 1 features a unique Orrefors crystal gear selector, adding a touch of elegance and luxury to the interior.


Polestar 2 Interior:

Contemporary Scandinavian Design: The Polestar 2's interior showcases a modern Scandinavian design, combining minimalism with functionality.

Vegan Interior Option: The Polestar 2 offers a vegan interior option, featuring synthetic materials for environmentally-conscious customers.

Five-Seat Layout: The Polestar 2 comes with a traditional five-seat layout, providing ample space for passengers and luggage.

Sustainable Materials: The Polestar 2 incorporates sustainable materials, such as recycled plastics and natural fibers, aligning with its eco-friendly ethos.

Android-Powered Infotainment: The Polestar 2 is the world's first car to feature an infotainment system powered by Android Automotive OS, offering seamless integration with Google services.

Ergonomic Seating: The Polestar 2's seats are designed for ergonomic support and comfort during daily driving.

Digital Instrument Cluster: The Polestar 2 features a digital instrument cluster, providing customizable information and a futuristic display.

Open-Pore Wood Trim: The Polestar 2 offers an optional open-pore wood trim, enhancing the cabin's ambiance with a touch of elegance.


On-Road Comfort - Polestar 1:

Luxury and Elegance: The Polestar 1's interior provides a luxurious and elegant environment, catering to discerning customers seeking a premium experience.


2+2 Seating: The 2+2 seating configuration is suitable for couples or small families, offering a more intimate driving experience.


On-Road Comfort - Polestar 2:

Modern Simplicity: The Polestar 2's interior design emphasizes modern simplicity, creating an uncluttered and user-friendly environment.

Versatile Seating: The five-seat layout is practical for families or those who need more seating capacity.


Quality and Materials - Polestar 1:

Exquisite Craftsmanship: The Polestar 1's interior reflects meticulous craftsmanship, with high-quality materials throughout the cabin.


Quality and Materials - Polestar 2:

Sustainable Focus: The Polestar 2 showcases a commitment to sustainability, utilizing recycled and vegan materials without compromising on quality.
