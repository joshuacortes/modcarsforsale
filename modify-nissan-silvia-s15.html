Nissan Silvia S15

In 1999, a new Silvia model called the S15 was released in Japan. Its SR20DET Inline-four engine now produces 275 Nm, or 203 lb-ft (28 kg-m), of torque at 4,800 rpm and 250 PS (247 bhp; 184 kW) at 6,400 rpm, thanks to an upgraded ball bearing turbocharger and engine management system. The SR20DE without a turbo generated 165 PS (163 bhp; 121 kW).

The interior and exterior of the S15 Silvia featured aggressive style that updated the previous Silvia's appearance to correspond with contemporary automotive design trends. In order to comply with the Japanese Government compact class, the body dimensions were decreased from the previous generation, which had an impact on the prior model's sales.

Only Australia and Japan sold new Silvias of this generation.


Spec-R

The Spec-6-speed R's manual gearbox and 4-speed automatic transmission set it apart from earlier Silvia versions. Through the employment of larger anti-roll bars and strut bracing, the Spec-R also featured considerable chassis and suspension strengthening. The S15 had a bigger brake booster along with the same 4-piston front brake calipers as the Z32 300ZX.

Spec-S

In the Spec-S, a 5-speed manual transmission was available (in addition to a 4-speed automatic available on both the Spec-S and the Spec-R). It had a 4-piston front brake caliper and a slightly smaller brake booster but lacked the extra chassis support of the Spec-R. Only an open differential and an optional LSD were standard on the Spec-S. The Spec-S versions sold in Australia had the same helical differential, chassis bracing, and 6-speed manual transmission as the Spec-R models sold in that country.Â 



Modified Nissan Silvia S15

Performance Enhancements

One of the primary motivations for modifying a Nissan Silvia S15 is to unlock its full performance potential. The stock S15 already boasts a powerful turbocharged 2.0-liter SR20DET engine, but there's always room for improvement. Enthusiasts commonly upgrade their S15s with a variety of performance-enhancing modifications, including:

Turbo Upgrades: Larger turbos, such as Garrett GT28 or GT30, provide increased boost and power gains.

Engine Management Systems: Upgrading to aftermarket ECUs like the Haltech or AEM allows for precise tuning and performance optimization.

Exhaust Systems: A high-flow exhaust system enhances engine efficiency and produces an aggressive, distinctive sound.

Suspension Upgrades: Coilovers, sway bars, and adjustable suspension components improve handling, cornering, and overall balance.

Brake Kits: High-performance brake systems ensure the S15 can stop as impressively as it accelerates.


Aftermarket Support

The S15's popularity ensures a robust aftermarket support system. Owners have access to a wide array of specialized parts and components that can be tailored to their preferences. There are numerous aftermarket manufacturers, such as Greddy, HKS, and APEXi, that offer products specifically designed for the Silvia S15. This support facilitates customization and allows owners to create a vehicle that suits their individual tastes and performance goals.


Design Upgrades

The S15 is already known for its attractive, timeless design. However, modifying enthusiasts often take the opportunity to add a personal touch and elevate the aesthetics of their cars. Popular design upgrades include:

Body Kits: Aggressive front bumpers, side skirts, and rear diffusers can give the S15 a more menacing appearance.

Wheels and Tires: Aftermarket wheels in various styles and sizes, paired with low-profile tires, enhance the car's stance and handling.

Interior Customization: High-quality leather seats, steering wheels, and upgraded audio systems offer a more comfortable and personalized cabin.


Motorsports Success/History

The Nissan Silvia S15 has an impressive motorsports legacy. It has been a standout performer in various racing disciplines, including drifting, time attack, and even touring car competitions. Modified S15s have graced racetracks and drift courses around the world, earning their place in motorsports history and further fueling the enthusiasm of owners to optimize their own vehicles for performance and competition.


Driving Experience

Owning and driving a modified Nissan Silvia S15 is a unique and exhilarating experience. The responsive steering, lightweight chassis, and potent engine offer a thrilling driving experience. The car's well-balanced rear-wheel-drive setup, combined with the modifications, transforms it into a dynamic and engaging machine on both the road and the track. The S15 provides drivers with a genuine sense of connection and control, making it a favorite for spirited driving.


Modifying a Nissan Silvia S15 is a passion-driven pursuit that transforms an already impressive sports coupe into a personalized, high-performance masterpiece. With a rich history in motorsports, an enthusiastic aftermarket community, and countless opportunities for customization, the Silvia S15 is a canvas for automotive enthusiasts to express their passion for performance, design, and driving experience.
