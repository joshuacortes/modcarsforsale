Nissan 350Z

Nissan produced the Nissan 350Z from 2002 to 2009. It is a two-door sports car with two seats. It is part of Nissan's Z-car line and is the fifth iteration. In Japan, it is known as the Nissan Fairlady Z (Z33). The 350Z started being produced in 2002 and began being sold and promoted as a 2003 model in August of the same year. Since the roadster did not make its debut until the following year, there was only a coupe the initial year. The Roadster initially came in Enthusiast and Touring versions. The coupe was first available in Base, Enthusiast, Performance, Touring, and Track models. The Track model had Brembo brakes and lightweight wheels, but the suspension settings of all the other coupes were the same.

Interior Upgrades: From racing seats and steering wheels to advanced audio systems, there are countless ways to improve your comfort and enjoyment inside the car.

NISSAN 350Z Turbocharger

GReddy Twin Turbo Kit:

GReddy, a trusted name in the automotive world, offers a comprehensive twin-turbo kit for the 350Z that's engineered for maximum power and reliability. The kit includes twin TD06SH-20G turbochargers, upgraded fuel injectors, a large front-mount intercooler, and a complete tuning solution. With this kit, your 350Z can achieve substantial horsepower gains while maintaining exceptional driveability.


Stillen VQ35HR Supercharger System:

While not a turbo, the Stillen Supercharger System is an attractive option for those who want a reliable and powerful forced induction setup. It's designed specifically for the 350Z's VQ35HR engine, offering instant throttle response and impressive power gains. The system includes a Vortech V-3 supercharger, a larger intercooler, and custom engine management.


APS Twin Turbo Kit:

APS (Air Power Systems) offers a popular twin-turbo kit for the 350Z. This kit is known for delivering significant power gains and high-quality components. It includes two Garrett GT2860RS turbochargers, an efficient front-mount intercooler, and a robust engine management system.


GTM Stage 2 Twin Turbo Kit:

The GTM (GT Motorsports) Stage 2 Twin Turbo Kit is well-regarded for its combination of performance and quality. It includes twin Garrett GT2871R turbochargers, larger fuel injectors, a front-mount intercooler, and custom tuning. This kit offers impressive power gains while maintaining good reliability.


Boosted Performance Single Turbo Kit:

For those looking for a single-turbo setup, the Boosted Performance Single Turbo Kit is a strong contender. It features a precision turbocharger, a front-mount intercooler, and custom tuning. This kit is known for its power gains and cost-effectiveness.


TurboXS Turbo Kit:

The TurboXS Turbo Kit is another option for single-turbo enthusiasts. It includes a Garrett GT3071R turbocharger, an intercooler, and a custom engine management system. This kit offers solid power gains and is suitable for those who prefer a single-turbo setup.


When considering a turbo setup for your Nissan 350Z, it's important to weigh factors like your budget, intended use, and desired power gains. The options listed above provide a range of choices, from twin-turbo kits to supercharger systems and single-turbo setups, each tailored to different driving styles and preferences. With the right turbo setup, your 350Z can experience a substantial boost in power, transforming it into an even more thrilling and capable sports car. Whether your goal is track performance or enhanced daily driving, the best turbo setup for your 350Z awaits, ready to provide an exhilarating driving experience.

NISSAN 350Z Wheels

Wheel Style:

When choosing wheels for your Nissan 350Z, the first consideration should be your preferred style. The Z-car's aesthetic appeal can be further heightened with the right choice of wheels. Here are some popular styles to consider:

Alloy Wheels: Lightweight and available in a wide range of designs, alloy wheels are a popular choice for 350Z owners. They can be sleek and modern or feature intricate spoke patterns for a more classic look.

Forged Wheels: Forged wheels are exceptionally strong and lightweight, making them ideal for performance-oriented 350Zs. They often come in unique designs and finishes, allowing for a customized appearance.

Multi-piece Wheels: These wheels consist of multiple components, often with customizable finishes and designs. They offer a high level of flexibility for achieving a truly personalized look.


Fitment and Sizing:

Proper fitment is crucial for both aesthetics and performance. The right wheel size can significantly impact your 350Z's handling and stance. Consider these factors:

Diameter: Common sizes for the 350Z range from 18 to 20 inches. Smaller wheels tend to offer better acceleration, while larger wheels improve braking performance and cornering stability.

Width: Wider wheels can accommodate wider tires, providing better grip and a more aggressive look. However, they can also impact handling, so it's essential to balance your choices.

Offset: Wheel offset affects how far the wheels sit within the wheel wells. The right offset ensures your wheels fit properly without rubbing against the fenders.


Material:

The material of your wheels plays a significant role in performance, weight, and durability. Consider the following options:

Aluminum Alloy: Most aftermarket wheels are made of aluminum alloy due to its lightweight properties. It enhances acceleration and handling while improving fuel efficiency.

Forged Aluminum: Forged wheels are made from a single piece of aluminum, resulting in higher strength and reduced weight. They are preferred for high-performance 350Zs.


Performance Wheels:

If you plan to use your Nissan 350Z for spirited driving or track days, you may want to invest in performance-oriented wheels. These wheels are designed to be lightweight and durable, providing enhanced handling and responsiveness. Look for brands that offer features such as flow-formed or forged construction.

Brands and Budget:

There are numerous reputable wheel brands to choose from, each with its own range of designs and prices. While top-tier brands often come with a higher price tag, they may offer superior build quality and design options. Consider your budget and research different brands to find the best combination of style and quality that suits your needs.


Choosing the best wheels for your Nissan 350Z is a crucial decision, as it can significantly impact both the appearance and performance of your beloved sports car. Keep in mind that your choice of wheels should align with your driving style, aesthetic preferences, and budget. With the right set of wheels, your 350Z can truly stand out on the road, showcasing both your personality and the car's innate capabilities. Whether you opt for alloy, forged, or multi-piece wheels, your 350Z will look and perform its best with the perfect set of shoes.
