Polestar 2 vs. Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered



Stock Motor

Polestar and Volvo have joined forces to create electrified performance vehicles that exemplify cutting-edge technology and dynamic driving experiences. The Polestar 2 and the Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered are two prime examples of this collaboration. In this article, we'll compare the stock motors of these models, providing details about their 0-60 acceleration, torque, horsepower, top speed, quarter mile time, lap times, powertrain configuration, transmission type, power to weight ratio, and RPM (shaft speed).


0-60 Acceleration:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2, being an all-electric vehicle, delivers instantaneous torque and quick acceleration, achieving 0-60 mph in approximately 4.5 seconds.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered, with its hybrid powertrain, offers impressive acceleration, reaching 0-60 mph in around 4.3 seconds.


Torque:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2's electric drivetrain generates a peak torque of over 400 lb-ft, providing immediate and consistent power delivery.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered, with its hybrid setup, produces a substantial torque figure of around 494 lb-ft, combining both internal combustion and electric power.


Horsepower:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2 delivers an impressive power output of approximately 408 horsepower, harnessing the full potential of its electric motors.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered combines the power of its internal combustion engine and electric motor to produce an astounding horsepower of around 415.


Top Speed:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2 boasts an electronically-limited top speed of 127 mph, catering to a balance of performance and efficiency.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered's top speed is electronically limited to 155 mph, providing exhilarating high-speed capabilities.


Quarter Mile Time:

Polestar 2: The quarter mile time for the Polestar 2 is around 13.1 seconds, making it a competitive performer in its class.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered covers the quarter mile in approximately 12.8 seconds, showcasing its sporty nature.


Lap Times:

Polestar 2: As an all-electric vehicle with a focus on daily driving and efficiency, the Polestar 2's lap times are not a primary focus.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered, designed for performance, exhibits impressive lap times, excelling in agility and handling on the track.


Powertrain Configuration:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2 features an all-electric powertrain, equipped with two electric motors, one on each axle, providing all-wheel drive capabilities.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered is a plug-in hybrid, combining a turbocharged and supercharged four-cylinder engine with an electric motor to offer both performance and efficiency.


Transmission Type:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2 has a single-speed transmission, typical of electric vehicles, optimizing power delivery and acceleration.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered features an eight-speed automatic transmission, optimizing gear shifts for a smooth and responsive driving experience.


Power-to-Weight Ratio:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2's power-to-weight ratio is commendable due to its lightweight electric drivetrain, ensuring dynamic performance.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered boasts an impressive power-to-weight ratio, thanks to its hybrid powertrain, offering a powerful and agile driving experience.


RPM (Shaft Speed):

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2's electric motors provide instant torque across a wide RPM range, ensuring constant power delivery and responsiveness.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered's hybrid powertrain provides an efficient RPM range, with electric assistance boosting performance at lower RPMs.





Suspension

Polestar 2 Suspension:

MacPherson Strut Front Suspension: The Polestar 2 features a MacPherson strut front suspension, providing excellent stability and precise steering response. This suspension setup is commonly used in front-wheel-drive vehicles to optimize weight distribution.

Integral Link Rear Suspension: At the rear, the Polestar 2 employs an integral link suspension system, enhancing its handling characteristics and overall stability, particularly during cornering.


Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered Suspension:

Double Wishbone Front Suspension: The Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered is equipped with a double wishbone front suspension, optimizing wheel control and handling. This setup is commonly found in performance-oriented vehicles for improved cornering abilities.

Integral Link Rear Suspension: Similar to the Polestar 2, the S60 Polestar Engineered uses an integral link rear suspension, providing stability and agility during dynamic driving.


Comfort vs. Sportiness:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2's suspension tuning strikes a balance between comfort and sportiness, making it an ideal choice for drivers seeking a refined and engaging driving experience.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: With a focus on performance, the S60 Polestar Engineered's suspension leans towards sportiness, offering sharper handling and a more dynamic ride.


Adaptive Damping System:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2 is equipped with an optional adaptive damping system, allowing drivers to choose between different driving modes and adjust the suspension's stiffness accordingly. This feature enhances the vehicle's versatility and adaptability to various road conditions.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered also offers an adaptive damping system, optimizing ride comfort and handling capabilities. This system allows drivers to switch between different driving modes for a personalized driving experience.


Ground Clearance and All-Wheel Drive:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2 has a higher ground clearance, suitable for handling rough roads and off-road adventures. Additionally, it comes with all-wheel drive, providing enhanced traction and stability in various driving conditions.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered, being a sedan, offers a lower ground clearance, enhancing aerodynamics and contributing to its sporty character. It also features all-wheel drive for improved handling and stability.


Weight Distribution:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2's suspension is engineered to complement the vehicle's weight distribution, contributing to its balanced handling and agility.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered's suspension setup is designed to maintain stability and agility, ensuring a confident and engaging driving experience.


Ride Quality:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2's suspension provides a comfortable and smooth ride, absorbing road imperfections and delivering a refined driving experience.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered's suspension delivers a sportier and more connected feel, offering precise feedback and dynamic handling.





Interior

Interior Design:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2 features a minimalist and contemporary interior design, reflecting its electric and environmentally conscious identity. The cabin showcases clean lines, high-quality materials, and a focus on creating a spacious and clutter-free environment.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered embraces a luxurious and sporty interior design, featuring premium materials, exquisite craftsmanship, and a driver-centric layout.


Infotainment and Connectivity:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2 is equipped with a state-of-the-art infotainment system centered around a large, vertically-oriented 11-inch touchscreen display. The system, based on Android Automotive OS, supports smartphone integration, voice commands, and over-the-air updates.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered comes with Volvo's Sensus infotainment system, featuring a vertically-mounted 9-inch touchscreen. The system is user-friendly, providing seamless connectivity and access to various in-car functions.


Digital Instrument Cluster:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2 boasts a 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster that offers a customizable display, allowing drivers to configure the information they want to see, including navigation and vehicle data.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered features a 12.3-inch digital driver display, providing essential information with a sporty and performance-oriented design.


Interior Space:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2 offers a spacious and open cabin, courtesy of its electric vehicle architecture and flat floor design. It provides ample legroom and headroom for all occupants, creating a comfortable environment for both front and rear passengers.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered is a sedan, which offers a slightly cozier cabin space compared to the Polestar 2. However, it still provides comfortable seating for all passengers.


Materials and Finishes:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2 showcases high-quality materials, including vegan and recycled materials, to uphold its commitment to sustainability. Premium synthetic leather and elegant trims add to the cabin's upscale feel.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered is adorned with premium Nappa leather upholstery, carbon fiber accents, and metal trimmings, creating a luxurious and sporty atmosphere.


Seating Comfort:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2 offers supportive and comfortable seats with optional heating and ventilation features for enhanced passenger comfort during all seasons.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered's seats are designed with a focus on both comfort and performance, providing excellent support during spirited driving.


Ambient Lighting:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2 offers customizable ambient lighting, allowing drivers to select from a range of colors to create a calming and serene atmosphere inside the cabin.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered also features ambient lighting, with a focus on sportiness, illuminating the cabin with a dynamic and vibrant flair.


Sound System:

Polestar 2: The Polestar 2 is equipped with a premium Harman Kardon sound system, delivering exceptional audio quality for an immersive driving experience.

Volvo S60 Polestar Engineered: The S60 Polestar Engineered comes with a high-end Bowers & Wilkins sound system, providing crystal-clear sound reproduction and concert-like acoustics.
