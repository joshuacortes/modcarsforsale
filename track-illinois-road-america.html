With a rich history dating back to the 1950s, this iconic track has become synonymous with heart-pounding racing action and a haven for both drivers and spectators alike. From classic cars to cutting-edge racing machines, Road America offers a diverse array of events that cater to a wide range of tastes. A Historical Gem Road America Race Park was established in 1955 and quickly gained a reputation as one of the most challenging and exciting tracks in the United States. The 4-mile, 14-turn road course is a rollercoaster of elevation changes, sweeping turns, and exhilarating straightaways. The park's proximity to Chicago and Milwaukee makes it easily accessible to motorsport enthusiasts from across the Midwest and beyond. Common Events Road America Race Park hosts a variety of events that showcase the prowess of different racing disciplines and capture the hearts of motorsport fans. Here are some of the common events that grace the track: 1. IMSA WeatherTech SportsCar Championship: One of the premier events at Road America, the IMSA WeatherTech SportsCar Championship brings together a diverse range of sports cars and endurance racing. Teams compete in various classes, such as Prototype, GT Le Mans, and GT Daytona, navigating the challenging course while battling competitors and the clock. 2. NASCAR Xfinity Series: Road America's unique road course setting offers a distinctive challenge for NASCAR drivers. The NASCAR Xfinity Series event on the track showcases the adaptability of stock car drivers as they navigate the twists and turns of the course instead of the usual ovals. 3. Vintage Racing Festivals: Vintage racing events pay homage to the roots of motorsport, featuring classic cars from bygone eras. These festivals allow spectators to witness the charm and elegance of vintage race cars as they tackle the challenging Road America course. 4. Motorcycle Races: Motorcycle enthusiasts are not left out, as Road America hosts a range of two-wheel racing events. From superbikes to MotoAmerica competitions, the park's track offers an exhilarating playground for motorcycle racers to showcase their skills and bravery. 5. Sports Car and Club Racing: Road America's versatile layout accommodates a plethora of sports car and club racing events. From regional racing series to amateur racing clubs, these events provide aspiring racers with a platform to hone their skills and share their passion for motorsport. 6. Driving Schools and Experiences: Road America offers driving schools and experiences for individuals seeking to improve their driving skills or experience the thrill of the track firsthand. Novices and experienced drivers alike can learn from professional instructors and take to the legendary tarmac themselves. The Road America Experience Beyond the on-track action, Road America Race Park offers a complete experience for attendees. The lush green surroundings and ample viewing areas provide spectators with optimal vantage points to witness the race unfolding before their eyes. The park also features amenities such as grandstands, concession stands, souvenir shops, and picnic areas, ensuring that visitors can enjoy the racing while being comfortable and well-catered to.
