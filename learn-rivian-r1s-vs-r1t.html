Rivian R1S vs. Rivian R1T



Stock Motor

Acceleration (0-60 mph):

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S, an all-electric SUV, boasts impressive acceleration, achieving 0 to 60 mph in approximately 3.0 seconds for the high-performance version. This makes it one of the quickest electric SUVs on the market.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T, as a high-performance electric truck, delivers equally impressive acceleration, reaching 0 to 60 mph in approximately 3.0 seconds for the top-tier variant. It showcases the EV's ability to provide thrilling acceleration even for a pickup truck.


Torque:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S generates a substantial amount of torque, which is characteristic of electric vehicles. Specific torque figures may vary depending on the chosen powertrain variant.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T boasts similar high torque figures, making it well-suited for heavy hauling and off-road adventures.


Horsepower:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S offers a powerful electric powertrain, resulting in an impressive combined horsepower output. The exact horsepower figures may vary based on the selected model configuration.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T shares a similar powertrain with the R1S, providing a robust combined horsepower output across different trim levels.


Top Speed:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S has an electronically limited top speed, ensuring a safe and controlled driving experience. The specific top speed may vary based on the selected powertrain variant.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T also has an electronically limited top speed, matching the R1S's safety-oriented speed limit.


Quarter Mile Time:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S showcases its impressive acceleration by completing the quarter-mile sprint in an exceptionally fast time.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T's quarter-mile time is equally impressive, rivaling some high-performance sports cars in its acceleration capabilities.


Lap Times:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S, being an electric SUV, is not typically designed for track use. While it offers impressive acceleration and handling, its primary focus is on delivering a luxurious and comfortable driving experience.

Rivian R1T: Similarly, the Rivian R1T, being an electric truck, is not intended for track use. Its suspension and powertrain are tailored for off-road capability and hauling, ensuring a smooth and confident ride on various terrains.


Powertrain Configuration:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S features a quad-motor all-wheel-drive configuration, with one electric motor driving each wheel. This setup optimizes traction and stability, making it suitable for various road conditions.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T also comes with a quad-motor all-wheel-drive configuration, providing the same level of all-terrain capability and stability.


Transmission Type:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S, like most electric vehicles, utilizes a single-speed transmission. This simplicity ensures seamless and efficient power delivery to all wheels.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T shares the same single-speed transmission with the R1S, offering the same advantages of simplicity and efficiency.


Power to Weight Ratio:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S's power-to-weight ratio is influenced by its power output and overall weight, resulting in impressive acceleration and handling characteristics.

Rivian R1T: Similarly, the Rivian R1T's power-to-weight ratio is optimized for its intended purpose, offering a balance between performance and hauling capability.


RPM (Shaft Speed):

Rivian R1S: Electric motors in the Rivian R1S operate at high RPMs, delivering instant torque and a responsive driving experience. The precise RPM value varies depending on driving conditions and power demands.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T's electric motors also operate at high RPMs, ensuring quick and seamless power delivery, particularly when off-roading or towing heavy loads.





Suspension

Ride Height:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S is designed as a full-size electric SUV, offering a higher ride height to provide better visibility and a commanding view of the road. This higher ride height is also beneficial for off-road adventures and navigating rough terrains.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T, being a pickup truck, also features a higher ride height, which contributes to its rugged appearance and off-road capability. The elevated stance allows the R1T to tackle challenging trails and obstacles with ease.


Suspension Tuning:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S's suspension is specifically tuned to prioritize a smooth and comfortable ride on both city streets and highways. The focus on ride comfort enhances passenger comfort and reduces fatigue during long journeys.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T's suspension is tuned for versatility, offering a balance between comfort and off-road capability. It delivers a compliant ride while also providing the necessary suspension articulation for challenging off-road conditions.


Adaptive Damping:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S may come equipped with an adaptive damping system that adjusts the suspension settings based on road conditions and driving style. This feature optimizes the ride comfort and handling dynamics in various driving scenarios.

Rivian R1T: Similarly, the Rivian R1T may also offer an adaptive damping system, enhancing the truck's on-road stability and off-road performance by adapting to different terrain surfaces.


Off-Road Capability:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S, though designed as an electric SUV, is not to be underestimated when it comes to off-road capability. Its adjustable air suspension and all-wheel-drive system allow it to handle light off-road trails with confidence.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T is engineered to excel in off-road environments. Its suspension system, combined with advanced four-wheel-drive technology, enables the truck to conquer rugged terrains and challenging off-road obstacles.


Load-Carrying Capacity:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S is primarily designed for passenger comfort and luxury, but it still offers a respectable towing capacity. Its suspension system is tuned to handle the weight of passengers and luggage while maintaining a comfortable ride.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T, as a pickup truck, is specifically engineered to carry heavy loads. Its suspension system is designed to handle the weight of cargo in the bed while ensuring a stable and composed ride.


Handling and Cornering:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S provides precise and responsive handling, allowing it to navigate urban roads and highways with ease. While not a performance-focused SUV, its suspension system strikes a balance between comfort and agility.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T's suspension is calibrated to provide stable handling, even when carrying heavy loads. Its higher center of gravity, due to the pickup truck body style, influences its cornering capabilities compared to the R1S.





Interior

Seating Capacity:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S is a full-size SUV that offers seating for up to seven passengers. It comes with a standard three-row seating configuration, making it an ideal choice for families and larger groups.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T, being a pickup truck, offers a standard two-row seating configuration with seating for up to five passengers. It provides ample space for front and rear occupants, making it a practical choice for daily commuting and off-road adventures.


Interior Design:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S boasts a refined and luxurious interior design. It features high-quality materials, such as premium leather upholstery, wood trims, and aluminum accents, creating an upscale ambiance.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T's interior design leans more towards a rugged and utilitarian theme. It incorporates durable materials like textured surfaces and rubberized accents, complementing the truck's adventurous spirit.


Infotainment System:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S comes equipped with a state-of-the-art infotainment system that features a large touchscreen display. It provides seamless smartphone integration, advanced navigation, and access to various connected services.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T shares the same infotainment system as the R1S, offering the same level of functionality and connectivity for drivers and passengers.


Interior Space:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S, as an SUV, provides generous interior space for passengers and cargo. The three-row seating configuration ensures ample legroom and headroom for all occupants.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T's cabin offers comfortable space for front and rear occupants. While it may not have the same passenger capacity as the R1S, it still provides a spacious and practical interior for daily use.


Storage Solutions:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S features versatile storage solutions, including a spacious cargo area behind the third row and folding seats to accommodate larger items.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T's interior includes lockable storage compartments in the bed and the front trunk, known as the "gear tunnel," providing ample space for storing outdoor gear and valuable items securely.


Utility Features:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S focuses on passenger comfort and luxury, offering amenities like heated and ventilated seats, multiple climate zones, and premium audio options for a first-class driving experience.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T, designed with adventure in mind, features practical utility elements such as a built-in air compressor, a power outlet in the bed, and adjustable tie-downs to secure cargo during off-road journeys.


Charging Infrastructure:

Rivian R1S: The Rivian R1S is designed with long-range driving in mind, making it compatible with the growing network of fast-charging stations to ensure convenience during road trips.

Rivian R1T: The Rivian R1T also benefits from the same fast-charging capabilities, allowing it to recharge efficiently at compatible charging stations.
