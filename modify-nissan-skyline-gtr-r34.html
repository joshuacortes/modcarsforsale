Modified Nissan GT-R R34

Modifying a Nissan GTR R34, building a showcar/project drift car, racecar or anything in between? Be aware of the aftermarket ability and overall support available for your R34 build:

The Nissan GTR R34 stands as a timeless icon, revered for its perfect fusion of power, precision, and enduring design. First introduced in the late '90s, the GTR R34 has solidified its place as a classic, offering an unforgettable driving experience. In this article, we delve into the remarkable facets that make the GTR R34 a legend in the automotive world.


Aftermarket Support: Tailoring Perfection: One of the GTR R34's most striking features is the extensive aftermarket support it receives. Car enthusiasts have at their disposal a treasure trove of performance parts, allowing for limitless customization possibilities. From turbocharger kits to suspension enhancements and body modifications, the GTR R34 has served as a blank canvas for personal expression. Whether you're interested in subtle tweaks or pushing the boundaries of power and handling, the R34's aftermarket community caters to every aspiration.


Design: A Timeless Masterpiece: The R34's design is nothing short of iconic. Its sharp, angular lines, quad circular taillights, and aggressive stance grant it an unmistakable presence on the road. The beloved "Skyline" script emblazoned across the rear and the GT-R emblem on the grille are symbols cherished by car enthusiasts. Even in today's modern landscape of sports cars, the R34's design remains captivating and continues to influence contemporary automotive aesthetics.


Motorsports History: A Champion on the Track: The R34 has earned its place in motorsports history as a dominant force on the racetrack. Its remarkable track record and numerous wins in various racing categories, including the renowned Group A and Group N competitions, have left an indelible mark on racing history. The R34's racing legacy led to its affectionate nickname, "Godzilla," signifying its monstrous performance. This motorsports heritage cements the GTR R34 as a symbol of Nissan's engineering excellence.


Driving Experience: A Symphony of Power and Precision: Behind the wheel of the GTR R34, one experiences a driving journey that seamlessly combines power, precision, and excitement. The R34's 2.6-liter, twin-turbocharged inline-six engine delivers an exhilarating burst of power, while the advanced all-wheel-drive system and finely-tuned suspension provide impeccable handling and control. The result is an unparalleled driving experience that leaves enthusiasts craving more.


The Nissan GTR R34 represents the epitome of automotive excellence, combining aftermarket customization possibilities, an iconic design, a storied motorsports history, and an unforgettable driving experience. It's a testament to Nissan's commitment to pushing the boundaries of automotive engineering and a symbol of timeless performance that continues to capture the hearts and imaginations of car enthusiasts worldwide.
