New England Dragway

Unleashing Horsepower: The Thrills and Traditions of New England Dragway Introduction Nestled in the heart of Epping, New Hampshire, the New England Dragway is a mecca for speed enthusiasts and a revered institution in the world of drag racing. With a legacy spanning over half a century, this iconic quarter-mile strip has become a playground for adrenaline junkies, professional racers, and families alike. Let's dive into the captivating world of New England Dragway and explore the common events that make it a haven for drag racing aficionados. A Legacy of Speed Since its opening in 1966, New England Dragway has remained a focal point for the drag racing community. Founded by a group of racing enthusiasts led by Ben Bossons, the dragway quickly established itself as a premier venue for high-speed showdowns, attracting competitors and fans from across the region. Over the years, the dragway has undergone transformations and expansions, cementing its place as one of the most iconic drag racing facilities in the United States. The Dragway Experience At the heart of New England Dragway's allure is its meticulously maintained quarter-mile drag strip. The track's smooth asphalt surface provides the perfect canvas for drivers to unleash the raw power of their machines. The unmistakable roar of engines, the scent of burning rubber, and the electrifying atmosphere create an unparalleled sensory experience for all who step foot on the dragway. Common Events at New England Dragway Friday Night Under the Lights: A beloved tradition at New England Dragway, Friday Night Under the Lights is a weekly event that offers amateur racers and street enthusiasts the opportunity to test their skills on the strip. Participants can bring their own vehicles and engage in friendly competition in a safe and controlled environment. It's a perfect way for newcomers to dip their toes into the world of drag racing. Points Series Racing: The dragway hosts a series of points races throughout the season, featuring a range of classes and divisions. From high-powered Top Fuel dragsters to nimble Sportsman racers, these events attract a diverse array of participants vying for championship glory. Jet Cars and Exhibition Runs: Spectacle takes center stage during jet car exhibitions and exhibition runs. Jet-powered dragsters and funny cars blaze down the track at mind-boggling speeds, creating breathtaking visuals and ear-splitting roars that leave the crowd in awe. Street Outlaws and No Prep Racing: Inspired by the popular television show, Street Outlaws events bring the underground world of street racing to the dragway. These no-prep races emphasize skill and strategy, as drivers tackle a track with minimal traction preparation. Import Face-Off: Embracing the import tuner culture, Import Face-Off events showcase a vibrant mix of modified imports, sport compacts, and European cars. These events include drag racing, car show competitions, and a lively atmosphere that celebrates automotive diversity. Super Chevy Show: A paradise for Chevrolet enthusiasts, the Super Chevy Show brings together classic and modern Chevy vehicles for a weekend of drag racing, car shows, and vendor displays. It's a celebration of all things Bowtie and a must-attend event for Chevy fans. Conclusion New England Dragway stands as a beacon of speed, excitement, and community in the heart of New Hampshire. With its storied history, iconic drag strip, and a diverse array of events that cater to racers and fans of all backgrounds, the dragway has solidified its place as a cherished destination for automotive enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned racer seeking championship glory or a family looking to experience the thrill of drag racing firsthand, New England Dragway offers an unforgettable journey into the world of high-speed competition.
