Nissan RB26DETT Engine



The Nissan RB26DETT engines were found in the:

Nissan Skyline GT-R BNR32
Nissan Skyline GT-R BCNR33
Nissan Skyline GT-R Autech Version 40th Anniversary BCNR33
Nissan Skyline GT-R BNR34
Nissan Stagea 260RS


The Nissan RB26DETT is powerful because it's a motor that truly thrives at higher engine speeds/rpms. With a strong cast iron block, closed deck and a short 73.7mm stroke, this over square assembly is ready to be pushed at rpms.


In 2023 people commonly find a tuned Nissan RB26DETT Engine in a modified Nissan R34, modified Datsun 240Z, modified Nissan 300Zx, and modified Nissan Silvia.

Nissan RB26DETT Engine Specs

Configuration: Inline-six

Displacement: 2.6L (2568cc)

Horsepower: 276 hp at 6,800 rpm

Torque (Nm): 260 lb-ft at 4,400 rpm

Aspiration: Twin Turbocharged

Block Material: Cast Iron

Head Material: Aluminum alloy

Valvetrain: DOHC, 24 valves

Bore x Stroke: 86.9mm x 73.77mm

Compression Ratio: 8.5:1
