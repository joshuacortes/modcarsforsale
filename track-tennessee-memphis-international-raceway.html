Common Events at Memphis International Raceway IHRA Drag Racing: Memphis International Raceway is a prominent host for International Hot Rod Association (IHRA) drag racing events. Road Course Racing: The road course at MIR hosts a range of events, including track days, high-performance driving schools, and road racing competitions for both amateur and experienced drivers. Midnight Madness: MIR hosts Midnight Madness events, offering a safe and controlled environment for enthusiasts to unleash their vehicles' horsepower on the drag strip under the moonlight. Test and Tune Days: Enthusiasts have the opportunity to test and tune their vehicles during designated test and tune days, honing their skills and achieving optimal performance. Specialty Events: Memphis International Raceway occasionally hosts specialty events, such as car shows, swap meets, and automotive festivals, bringing together a diverse community of car enthusiasts.
