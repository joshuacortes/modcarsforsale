Nissan S20 (Datsun 240Z)

Nissan Motors, Ltd. of Japan produced the first generation of Z GT 3-door, 2-seat coupés from 1969 to 1978. The trend-setting S30 was created by Yutaka Katayama, President of Nissan Motor Corporation U.S.A., and designed by a team under the direction of Yoshihiko Matsuo, Head of Nissan's Sports Car Styling Studio.


Drivetrain

Models are available in rear-wheel drive and front-wheel drive. 4-speed and 5-speed are available in manual transmissons. 3-speed is available in an automatic transmission.



Modified Datsun 240Z

The Datsun 240Z, often referred to as the "Fairlady Z" in its home market of Japan, is a timeless classic that has captured the hearts of automotive enthusiasts for generations. Known for its sleek design and sporty performance, the 240Z is a perfect canvas for modification. In this article, we'll explore the world of modifying a Datsun 240Z, covering categories such as performance, aftermarket support, design, motorsports success/history, and the driving experience.


Performance: Unleashing the Z's True Potential

The Datsun 240Z is celebrated for its superb handling and well-balanced chassis. However, its original inline-six engine, while reliable, can leave some enthusiasts craving more power. Modifying the 240Z for performance often includes engine swaps or upgrades. Many enthusiasts opt for installing a more potent inline-six or even V8 engine to increase power output. Tuning the suspension, upgrading the brakes, and fitting wider tires can further enhance the 240Z's already impressive handling characteristics.


Aftermarket Support: A Treasure Trove of Options

The 240Z enjoys a robust aftermarket ecosystem, with a plethora of suppliers offering performance parts, body kits, and interior upgrades. You can find everything from performance exhaust systems, camshafts, and carburetor kits to suspension components and brake upgrades. Whether you're looking to improve power, handling, or aesthetics, the 240Z's extensive aftermarket support ensures you can create a personalized masterpiece.


Design: Classic Lines with Modern Flair

The Datsun 240Z's design is a timeless classic, with its iconic long hood and graceful curves. When modifying the 240Z's design, enthusiasts often focus on tasteful enhancements that preserve its essence. This includes body kits, wheel upgrades, and paint schemes that add a modern twist while maintaining its classic appeal. The result is a beautiful blend of vintage charm and contemporary aesthetics that pays homage to the original design.


Motorsports Success/History: The Legend on the Track

The Datsun 240Z boasts a rich motorsports history that has solidified its place in automotive lore. In the early 1970s, the 240Z achieved remarkable success in various competitions, including the SCCA (Sports Car Club of America) races. The Z-car's racing pedigree inspired countless enthusiasts to modify their 240Z models for track use, paying homage to its iconic past.


Driving Experience: Nostalgia Meets Thrills

Modifying a Datsun 240Z isn't just about making it look and perform better; it's about enhancing the driving experience. The 240Z is celebrated for its direct, connected, and driver-focused feel. When you modify it for performance, you're creating a car that not only looks great but also provides a thrilling and engaging drive. The handling improvements, additional power, and personalized interior all contribute to a unique and exhilarating driving experience.


Modifying a Datsun 240Z is a passionate pursuit of excellence in performance, aesthetics, and driving enjoyment. Whether you aim to pay homage to its racing history, build a jaw-dropping show car, or create the ultimate driving machine, the 240Z provides an incredible platform for your automotive dreams. Embracing the past while looking towards the future, modifying a Datsun 240Z is a journey filled with satisfaction, and it allows you to take part in a rich automotive tradition that shows no signs of fading away.

6. Precision Turbo GEN2 5558: The Precision Turbo GEN2 5558 is a versatile turbocharger that balances power and responsiveness. When choosing a turbocharger for your Datsun 240Z, it's essential to consider your goals, budget, and the level of performance you desire. Turbocharging a classic like the 240Z is a significant modification that can transform your car into a high-performance machine. Consulting with experienced professionals or tuners who specialize in turbocharging can help ensure your chosen turbocharger aligns with your overall build and provides reliable, safe performance.


Turbocharging your Datsun 240Z can elevate its performance to a whole new level. 4. EBC Yellowstuff Brake Pads: If you're looking to enhance your 240Z's braking performance without changing the entire braking system, EBC Yellowstuff Brake Pads are an excellent option. These high-performance brake pads offer improved stopping power, better pedal feel, and resistance to brake fade. They are a cost-effective way to upgrade your braking system.

5. Hawk Performance HPS Brake Pads: Hawk Performance HPS Brake Pads are a popular choice for those who want a noticeable improvement in braking performance without breaking the bank. These pads provide better stopping power, reduced brake dust, and improved pedal feel, making them a great choice for daily drivers and occasional track use.

When selecting brake upgrades for your Datsun 240Z, it's essential to consider your driving style, intended use, and budget. Each of the options mentioned above offers a unique blend of performance, durability, and affordability, allowing you to tailor your 240Z's braking system to your specific needs. Professional installation and proper bedding-in procedures are crucial to ensure that your brake upgrades deliver the expected benefits and maintain the 240Z's renowned driving dynamics and safety.


Upgrading the brakes on your Datsun 240Z is a valuable investment in safety and performance.
