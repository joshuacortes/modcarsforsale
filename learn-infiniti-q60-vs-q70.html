Infiniti Q60 vs. Infiniti Q70



Stock Motor

Infiniti Q60 Performance


0-60 Acceleration:

The Infiniti Q60's acceleration varies based on engine options and drivetrain configurations.

It can achieve a 0-60 mph time ranging from around 5.0 seconds to 6.0 seconds, depending on the trim.


Torque:

Torque figures for the Q60 range from approximately 260 lb-ft to 350 lb-ft, based on engine selection.


Horsepower:

Horsepower outputs span from around 300 horsepower to 400+ horsepower, varying across trim levels and engines.


Top Speed:

The top speed of the Q60 varies based on the engine and model variant, reaching up to approximately 155 mph.


Quarter Mile Time:

The Q60's quarter mile times differ based on engine and drivetrain choices, generally falling between mid-13 seconds to mid-14 seconds.


Lap Times:

Specific lap times vary depending on the Q60's configuration, including engine, suspension, and handling characteristics.


Powertrain Configuration:

The Q60 features rear-wheel drive and all-wheel drive options, contributing to varying levels of traction and dynamics.


Transmission Type:

Automatic transmissions with multiple gears are available in the Q60 lineup, offering smooth shifts and optimized performance.

Power-to-Weight Ratio:

The Q60's power-to-weight ratio is influenced by engine choices and trim levels, affecting its acceleration and overall performance.


RPM (Shaft Speed):

The RPM (shaft speed) varies based on the Q60's engine and transmission options, with different engines producing power at varying RPM ranges.



Infiniti Q70 Performance


0-60 Acceleration:

The Infiniti Q70's acceleration varies based on engine options and drivetrain configurations.

It can achieve a 0-60 mph time ranging from around 5.5 seconds to 6.5 seconds, depending on the trim and engine.


Torque:

Torque figures for the Q70 range from approximately 270 lb-ft to 416 lb-ft, based on engine and drivetrain selections.


Horsepower:

Horsepower outputs vary across Q70 models, spanning from around 330 horsepower to 416 horsepower, depending on the engine.


Top Speed:

The top speed of the Q70 varies based on engine and model variant, reaching up to around 155 mph.


Quarter Mile Time:

The Q70's quarter mile times differ based on engine, trim, and drivetrain configurations, generally ranging from mid-14 seconds to mid-15 seconds.


Lap Times:

Specific lap times vary depending on the Q70's configuration, including engine, suspension, and handling characteristics.


Powertrain Configuration:

The Q70 offers both rear-wheel drive and all-wheel drive options, catering to diverse driving preferences.


Transmission Type:

Automatic transmissions with varying speeds are available in the Q70 lineup, delivering smooth shifts and enhancing driving comfort.


Power-to-Weight Ratio:

The Q70's power-to-weight ratio varies based on engine, trim, and drivetrain selections, affecting its overall performance characteristics.


RPM (Shaft Speed):

The RPM (shaft speed) varies based on the Q70's engine and transmission configurations, influencing power delivery.





Tuning Ability

Infiniti Q60 Tuning Potential


Upgraded Intake:

Enhancing the Q60's air intake system with high-flow components can improve airflow and performance.

Brands like AEM and K&N offer cold air intakes and performance air filters for the Q60.


Performance Exhaust:

Upgrading the exhaust system can optimize exhaust flow, sound, and overall performance.

Manufacturers like Borla and Injen offer cat-back exhaust systems designed for the Q60.

Forced Induction:

The Q60 can benefit from turbocharger or supercharger kits to increase power output.

Companies like Stillen and AMS Performance offer forced induction solutions for the Q60.


Fuel Management:

Upgraded fuel injectors and a programmable engine management system may be necessary for optimal performance.

Brands like AEM and Haltech offer ECU solutions for precise fuel delivery in the Q60.


Ignition Enhancements:

Upgraded spark plugs and ignition coils can enhance combustion efficiency and power output.

Brands like NGK and MSD offer options for improving the Q60's ignition system.


Infiniti Q70 Tuning Potential

Upgraded Intake:

Upgrading the Q70's intake system can lead to improved airflow and power gains.

Brands like Volant and K&N offer intake kits designed for the Q70.


Performance Exhaust:

Enhancing the exhaust system can result in enhanced performance and a more aggressive exhaust note.

Manufacturers like MagnaFlow and ARK Performance offer exhaust systems for the Q70.


Forced Induction:

Turbocharging or supercharging the Q70's engine can provide substantial power increases.

Companies like Stillen and Greddy offer forced induction kits for the Q70.


Fuel Management:

Enhanced fuel injectors and an aftermarket ECU may be needed for precise fuel delivery.

Brands like Haltech and AEM offer solutions for optimizing fuel management in the Q70.


Ignition Enhancements:

Upgraded ignition components can improve combustion efficiency and overall power output.

Brands like NGK and MSD offer options for enhancing the Q70's ignition system.


Critique and Considerations

Tuning Approach: The Infiniti Q60's performance-oriented nature makes it a popular choice for tuning enthusiasts seeking a sportier driving experience.

Luxury Performance: The Q70 offers a balance of luxury and performance, appealing to those seeking a refined tuning experience.

Forced Induction: Both models can benefit from forced induction upgrades, but the Q60 might be more frequently modified due to its sportier image.

Budget: Consider the budget for modifications, as some tuning options can be more cost-intensive than others.

Expertise and Support: Seek professional expertise when undergoing significant modifications to ensure proper installation and performance gains.





Suspension

Infiniti Q60 Suspension


Sporty Handling:

The Infiniti Q60's suspension setup emphasizes sporty handling characteristics, catering to enthusiasts seeking dynamic driving experiences.

It prioritizes responsiveness and agility, making it well-suited for spirited driving.


Agile and Responsive:

The Q60's suspension tuning aims to deliver an agile and responsive feel, allowing for quick cornering and precise steering inputs.

It provides a connected driving experience, engaging the driver through tight turns.


Independent Suspension:

The Q60 typically features an independent suspension setup, optimizing wheel control and road-holding capabilities.

This design enhances stability and control while navigating various road conditions.


Tuned for Performance:

The Q60's suspension is tuned to strike a balance between comfort and performance, offering a responsive and engaging ride.


Infiniti Q70 Suspension

Luxury and Comfort:

The Infiniti Q70's suspension setup is designed to prioritize luxury and comfort, providing a smooth and refined ride quality.

It aims to deliver a comfortable driving experience, especially during longer journeys.


Smooth Ride:

The Q70's suspension tuning emphasizes a smooth ride, reducing road vibrations and minimizing bumps and impacts for occupants.

It offers a serene and relaxed driving atmosphere.


Adaptive Suspension Options:

Many Q70 models offer adaptive suspension systems that can adjust damping rates based on driving conditions and driver preferences.

This feature allows for varying levels of comfort and responsiveness, catering to different driving scenarios.


Balanced Dynamics:

The Q70's suspension is tuned to offer a balance between luxury and performance, providing a refined and controlled driving experience.

Critique and Considerations


Driving Focus: The Infiniti Q60's sporty handling makes it a favored choice among driving enthusiasts seeking a more spirited and dynamic driving experience.

Performance-Oriented: The Q60's suspension setup aligns with its sportier image and aims to deliver engaging handling.

Luxury Comfort: The Infiniti Q70's focus on luxury and comfort caters to drivers prioritizing a refined and smooth ride for longer journeys.

Serene Ride: The Q70's suspension tuning creates a relaxed and serene driving atmosphere, ideal for comfortable cruising.

Versatility: The Q60's responsive suspension suits a variety of driving scenarios, including winding roads and track days.

Long-Distance Comfort: The Q70's suspension is well-suited for extended drives, providing occupants with a comfortable ride over varied road surfaces.

Personal Preference: Suspension preferences are subjective. Those seeking a blend of performance and luxury might lean toward the Q70, while enthusiasts looking for sharper handling might prefer the Q60.





Interior

Infiniti Q60 Interior


Sporty Elegance:

The Infiniti Q60's interior exudes a blend of sportiness and elegance, aligning with its performance-oriented image.

The design elements aim to create a dynamic and engaging driving environment.


Driver-Focused Cockpit:

The Q60's interior layout emphasizes a driver-centric approach, with controls and displays angled towards the driver for optimal accessibility.

The cockpit-inspired design enhances the sense of being connected to the road.


Modern Technology:

The Q60's interior features modern technology, including infotainment systems with touchscreen displays and digital instrument clusters.

Connectivity options and advanced driver-assistance features contribute to a high-tech driving experience.


Performance-Enhancing Details:

Features like sport seats, aluminum accents, and sport-oriented controls accentuate the Q60's performance-oriented character.

Red stitching, paddle shifters, and configurable drive modes enhance the driving experience.


Infiniti Q70 Interior


Luxurious Comfort:

The Infiniti Q70's interior is characterized by a focus on luxurious comfort, catering to a refined and sophisticated driving experience.

High-quality materials and attention to detail contribute to a premium ambiance.


Spacious Serenity:

The Q70's interior design prioritizes passenger comfort, with spacious seating and a serene driving environment.

Plush materials and soft-touch surfaces contribute to an upscale atmosphere.


Advanced Amenities:

The Q70's interior offers an array of amenities, including premium audio systems, advanced climate control, and adjustable seating.

Luxurious features such as wood trim and ambient lighting enhance the overall ambiance.


Executive Rear Seating:


Certain Q70 models provide rear-seat passengers with executive-style seating options, including additional legroom and luxurious amenities.

This feature caters to passengers seeking the utmost comfort during long journeys.


Critique and Considerations

Performance vs. Luxury: The Infiniti Q60's interior focuses on sportiness and performance, appealing to those who seek an engaging driving experience.

Refinement: The Q60's driver-centric cockpit creates a more connected feeling with the road, aligning with its dynamic character.

Luxury Comfort: The Infiniti Q70's interior embodies a luxurious and comfortable atmosphere, catering to those who prioritize refined driving experiences.

Serene Ambiance: The Q70's plush materials and spaciousness create a serene environment, perfect for relaxed cruising.

Technology: The Q60's modern technology features enhance its high-tech image, catering to drivers who value advanced connectivity and features.

Premium Amenities: The Q70's advanced amenities and executive rear seating offer a more opulent and upscale experience.

Personal Preference: Interior preferences vary. Enthusiasts might prefer the Q60's driver-centric design, while those valuing luxury might gravitate toward the Q70's refined ambiance.
