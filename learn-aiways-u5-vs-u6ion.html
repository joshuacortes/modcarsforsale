Aiways U5 vs. Aiways U6ion



Stock Motor

0-60 Acceleration:

Aiways U5: The U5 features a responsive electric motor that can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in approximately 8.5 seconds, delivering decent acceleration for urban commuting and highway merging.

Aiways U6ion: The U6ion's electric powertrain excels in acceleration, reaching 0 to 60 mph in approximately 4.5 seconds, making it a high-performance electric vehicle.


Torque:

Aiways U5: The U5's electric motor produces an impressive torque of around 214 lb-ft, providing sufficient power for daily driving and city commutes.

Aiways U6ion: With its high-performance powertrain, the U6ion boasts a powerful torque output of approximately 590 lb-ft, delivering exhilarating acceleration and responsiveness.


Horsepower:

Aiways U5: The U5's electric motor delivers a respectable horsepower of approximately 190 hp, providing ample power for efficient and smooth driving.

Aiways U6ion: The U6ion's powerful electric motor generates an impressive horsepower of approximately 430 hp, catering to enthusiasts seeking a thrilling driving experience.


Top Speed:

Aiways U5: The U5's top speed is electronically limited to approximately 100 mph, prioritizing safety and efficiency for everyday driving.

Aiways U6ion: The U6ion boasts an electronically limited top speed of approximately 125 mph, reflecting its performance-oriented design.


Quarter Mile Time:

Aiways U5: The U5 completes the quarter-mile in approximately 16.5 seconds, showcasing its efficiency and responsiveness.

Aiways U6ion: With its sporty and high-performance capabilities, the U6ion completes the quarter-mile in approximately 12.5 seconds, displaying its exceptional acceleration.


Lap Times:

Aiways U5: The U5 is designed primarily for daily driving and commuting, and while it is not intended for track racing, it still offers a smooth and comfortable driving experience.

Aiways U6ion: The U6ion is a performance-oriented electric vehicle, capable of achieving competitive lap times on the track, thanks to its powerful motor and agile handling.


Powertrain Configuration:

Aiways U5: The U5 features a front-wheel-drive (FWD) configuration, with the electric motor powering the front wheels, offering a balanced and efficient setup.

Aiways U6ion: The U6ion boasts an all-wheel-drive (AWD) configuration, with electric motors powering both the front and rear wheels, providing superior traction and performance.


Transmission Type:

Aiways U5: As an electric vehicle, the U5 uses a single-speed transmission, simplifying the driving experience and eliminating the need for gear shifting.

Aiways U6ion: Similar to the U5, the U6ion utilizes a single-speed transmission, maintaining seamless power delivery without the need for gear changes.


Power-to-Weight Ratio:

Aiways U5: With its curb weight of approximately 4,300 lbs and horsepower of 190 hp, the U5 achieves a reasonable power-to-weight ratio, ensuring a smooth driving experience.

Aiways U6ion: The U6ion's curb weight of approximately 4,400 lbs and horsepower of 430 hp result in an impressive power-to-weight ratio, contributing to its outstanding performance.


RPM (Shaft Speed):

Aiways U5: The U5's electric motor can rev up to approximately 12,000 RPM (Revolutions Per Minute), translating into efficient power delivery and responsiveness.

Aiways U6ion: The U6ion's high-performance electric motor can achieve revs of approximately 14,000 RPM, showcasing its capabilities for quick acceleration and smooth power delivery.





Suspension

Comfort-Oriented Ride: The U5's suspension system is designed with an emphasis on delivering a smooth and comfortable ride, ideal for daily city commuting and long highway journeys.

MacPherson Strut Front Suspension: The U5 features a MacPherson strut front suspension, a common design known for its simplicity, cost-effectiveness, and ride comfort.

Torsion Beam Rear Suspension: At the rear, the U5 employs a torsion beam suspension, which complements the front suspension to provide balanced handling and stability during normal driving conditions.

Urban and Highway Versatility: The U5's suspension setup is well-suited for navigating urban environments and tackling highway speeds, providing passengers with a comfortable and enjoyable ride.


Aiways U6ion Suspension:

Performance-Focused Handling: The U6ion's suspension is engineered for performance, emphasizing agile handling and dynamic driving characteristics, catering to enthusiasts seeking a thrilling driving experience.

Multi-Link Front Suspension: The U6ion features a multi-link front suspension, a sophisticated setup that optimizes handling, cornering, and steering response, enhancing overall driving dynamics.

Multi-Link Rear Suspension: At the rear, the U6ion also utilizes a multi-link suspension, contributing to improved stability and traction during high-speed cornering and aggressive maneuvers.

Track-Worthy Performance: The U6ion's suspension tuning is geared towards delivering precise handling and responsive feedback, making it suitable for spirited driving on both roads and racetracks.


On-Road Comfort - Aiways U5:

Soft Suspension Tuning: The U5's suspension is tuned to prioritize ride comfort, absorbing road imperfections and providing a smooth and plush driving experience.

Road Noise Isolation: The U5's suspension, combined with effective noise insulation, ensures a quiet cabin, minimizing road noise and vibrations for a pleasant ride.

Enhanced Feedback: The U6ion's suspension offers improved road feedback, allowing the driver to feel connected to the road surface and the vehicle's dynamics.


Off-Road Capabilities - Aiways U5:

Limited Off-Road Capability: The U5's suspension is primarily optimized for on-road driving, and while it can handle occasional light off-road situations, it is not intended for rugged terrains or extreme conditions.


Off-Road Capabilities - Aiways U6ion:

Not Suitable for Off-Roading: The U6ion's performance-oriented suspension is not designed for off-road driving. Instead, it excels in providing precise handling and stability on paved surfaces.


Driving Dynamics - Aiways U5:

Predictable Handling: The U5's suspension delivers predictable and stable handling, making it an easy-to-drive vehicle suitable for a wide range of drivers, including novices.


Driving Dynamics - Aiways U6ion:

Dynamic Performance: The U6ion's suspension enhances its agility, responsiveness, and cornering capabilities, creating a thrilling driving experience for enthusiasts who enjoy spirited driving.
