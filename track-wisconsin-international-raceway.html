The Racing Experience At the core of WIR's allure lies its versatile tracks, which include an asphalt oval, a quarter-mile drag strip, and a road course. Common Events at WIR NASCAR-sanctioned Racing: WIR hosts NASCAR-sanctioned events that showcase a variety of stock car racing divisions. These events provide a platform for both aspiring racers and seasoned veterans to compete in wheel-to-wheel action on the oval track. Thursday Night Thunder: A beloved tradition at WIR, Thursday Night Thunder features a mix of local stock car racing divisions, providing an exciting midweek racing fix for fans and drivers. Fox River Racing Club (FRRC) Events: WIR is home to the Fox River Racing Club, which organizes a series of stock car races throughout the season. FRRC events attract local racers and serve as a breeding ground for emerging talent. Red, White & Blue State Championships: This annual event showcases the top Late Model drivers in the region, competing for the prestigious Red, White & Blue State Championships title. Eve of Destruction: WIR hosts the Eve of Destruction, an action-packed event featuring thrilling stunts, demolition derbies, and other non-traditional forms of motorsports entertainment. Drag Racing Extravaganza: The drag strip at WIR comes alive during drag racing events, featuring a mix of dragsters, muscle cars, and other high-performance vehicles vying for supremacy on the quarter-mile strip. Fostering Community and Legacy WIR's significance extends beyond its asphalt.
