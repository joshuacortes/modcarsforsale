Common Events at Sandia Speedway Oval Racing: Sandia Speedway is renowned for its oval racing events, where drivers compete on the traditional circular track layout. These events feature various divisions, including stock cars, sprint cars, modifieds, and more, providing fans with close-quarters racing and thrilling overtaking maneuvers. Drag Racing: The speedway hosts drag racing events on its dedicated drag strip, allowing drivers to unleash the full power of their vehicles in a straight-line sprint. These events attract a diverse range of vehicles, from high-performance cars to motorcycles, all vying for the quickest elapsed times. Dirt Track Racing: Sandia Speedway occasionally transforms its oval track into a dirt surface, hosting dirt track racing events that showcase the art of sliding around corners, controlled power slides, and thrilling dirt-slinging action. Muscle Car Shootouts: The speedway organizes muscle car shootout events, inviting owners of muscle cars and modern sports cars to compete against the clock and each other. These events celebrate American muscle and performance. Test and Tune Days: Sandia Speedway offers test and tune days, allowing drivers to fine-tune their vehicles, practice launches, and refine their racing techniques in a controlled environment. Specialty Racing Series: The speedway hosts specialty racing series that cater to specific vehicle types or racing disciplines. These series may include vintage car racing, truck racing, or themed events that celebrate unique aspects of automotive culture.
