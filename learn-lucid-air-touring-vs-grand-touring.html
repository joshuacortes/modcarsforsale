Suspension

Air Suspension with Adaptable Damping:

The Lucid Air Grand Touring is equipped with an advanced air suspension system with adaptable damping technology.

The system adjusts the suspension firmness and damping characteristics in real-time based on road conditions and driving modes.


Smart Adaptive Air Springs:

The Grand Touring variant features smart adaptive air springs that can automatically adjust their height for better aerodynamics and handling.

This system provides a comfortable ride while maintaining stability at high speeds.


Active Ride Height Control:

The Grand Touring's suspension includes active ride height control, enabling the vehicle to lower itself at higher speeds for enhanced aerodynamics and stability.

Performance-Oriented Tuning:

The suspension of the Lucid Air Grand Touring is tuned to prioritize sportiness and responsiveness, providing a dynamic driving experience.



Suspension of Lucid Air Touring:

Air Suspension with Fixed Damping:

The Lucid Air Touring also comes with an air suspension system, but it has fixed damping settings.

Unlike the Grand Touring, the damping characteristics do not adapt in real-time based on driving conditions.

Comfort-Oriented Tuning:

The Touring variant's suspension tuning focuses on delivering a smooth and comfortable ride, perfect for luxury-oriented driving experiences.

Passive Air Springs:

The Touring model uses passive air springs that maintain a constant ride height without automatic adjustments based on driving conditions.


Height Adjustment Options:

While not as dynamic as the Grand Touring, the Air Touring allows drivers to manually adjust the ride height for improved clearance or comfort.



Comparison:

Ride Comfort - Lucid Air Grand Touring:

The Grand Touring variant delivers a comfortable ride, thanks to its adaptive damping and active ride height control, which optimizes comfort at varying speeds.

Ride Comfort - Lucid Air Touring:

The Lucid Air Touring prioritizes ride comfort, providing a smooth and luxurious driving experience on various road surfaces.

Handling - Lucid Air Grand Touring:

With its performance-oriented tuning, the Grand Touring offers precise and agile handling, making it suitable for spirited driving.

Handling - Lucid Air Touring:

The Air Touring model focuses on comfort and stability, making it well-suited for relaxed cruising and long-distance journeys.
