A Legacy of Racing Excellence Ozarks International Raceway's legacy dates back to its inception, when it first opened its gates to roaring engines and excited spectators. The Racing Experience At the heart of Ozarks International Raceway's allure lies its meticulously designed 1/4-mile drag strip. The roar of engines, the scent of burning rubber, and the excitement of side-by-side racing create an atmosphere that encapsulates the essence of grassroots motorsports. Common Events at Ozarks International Raceway Weekly Drag Racing Series: Ozarks International Raceway's weekly drag racing series serves as the backbone of the track's activities. The series features a variety of classes, ranging from street cars to highly modified dragsters, providing a platform for drivers of all skill levels to compete in friendly competition. Test and Tune Nights: Test and tune nights offer racers an opportunity to fine-tune their vehicles and improve their skills on the drag strip. These sessions are perfect for drivers looking to perfect their launches, refine their strategies, and prepare for upcoming events. Street Car Shootouts: Street car shootout events showcase high-performance street cars going head-to-head in intense drag racing battles. These events attract a diverse array of vehicles, from daily drivers to purpose-built street machines. Midnight Drags: Midnight drag racing events add an exciting twist to the typical racing schedule. Held under the cover of darkness, these events create a unique atmosphere as drivers unleash their horsepower and battle for supremacy. Import vs. Domestic Showdown: Import vs. Domestic events bring together cars from different automotive cultures to compete in a friendly and exciting showdown. These events celebrate the diversity of the automotive world and offer thrilling matchups. Classic Car and Hot Rod Shows: In addition to high-speed action, Ozarks International Raceway occasionally hosts classic car and hot rod shows. These events provide a platform for enthusiasts to showcase their beautifully restored vehicles and connect with fellow car lovers.
