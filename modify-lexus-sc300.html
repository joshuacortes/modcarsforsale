Lexus SC300

The Lexus SC300 was a grand tourer that was produced between 1991 and 2010 and sold by Lexus, a luxury division of Toyota. Up to four persons can ride in it, and it has a front engine and rear-wheel drive configuration. The V8-powered SC 400 made its debut as the first-generation SC in 1991, and the I6-powered SC 300 followed in 1992. Up to 2000, both first-generation variants were manufactured.

In 2001, production of the SC 430, the second-generation vehicle, began. The SC 430 has a V8 engine and a hardtop convertible style.

Community and Camaraderie: Building or modifying an SC300 connects enthusiasts with a passionate community of like-minded owners who share knowledge, experiences, and support. Being a part of this community creates a sense of camaraderie and fosters a shared passion for the platform.

Lexus SC300 Turbocharger

The Lexus SC300, known for its timeless design and luxurious comfort, can transform into a high-performance machine with the right modifications. One of the most effective ways to unleash its full potential is by adding a turbocharger. A turbocharger provides a significant boost in power and torque, delivering an exhilarating driving experience.


Garrett GT Series Turbochargers:

Garrett is a trusted name in the world of turbocharging, and their GT Series offers a wide range of options suitable for the Lexus SC300. The GT Series provides excellent performance and reliability, with various sizes to match different power goals. The GT2860R and GT3076R are popular choices for enthusiasts seeking a balanced combination of response and top-end power. These turbochargers come with advanced aerodynamics, water-cooled center housing, and ball-bearing systems for enhanced durability.


BorgWarner EFR Series Turbochargers:

BorgWarner's EFR (Engineered For Racing) Series turbochargers are renowned for their cutting-edge technology and performance capabilities. These turbos utilize Gamma-Ti turbine wheels, low-inertia ball bearings, and integrated wastegates for precise boost control. For moderate to high-power applications, the EFR 6258 and EFR 7163 provide a wide powerband and excellent transient response, making them ideal choices for spirited driving on both streets and tracks.


Precision Turbo & Engine PT Series Turbochargers:

Precision Turbo & Engine (PTE) has been a long-standing player in the performance turbocharger market. Their PT Series offers various turbo options catering to different horsepower targets. The PT6266 and PT6466 are popular selections for those seeking significant power gains, while the PT5858 strikes a balance between response and top-end performance. PTE turbochargers boast billet compressor wheels and dual ceramic ball bearing systems, ensuring long-lasting performance.


BorgWarner AirWerks Series Turbochargers:

BorgWarner's AirWerks Series turbos are engineered for high-performance applications, combining reliability and versatility. The S200SX-E and S300SX-E models offer a range of compressor and turbine sizes, allowing customization based on power goals and engine requirements. These turbos come with adjustable compressor and turbine housing options, making them suitable for various engine bay configurations.


Holset HX Series Turbochargers:

Holset, a division of Cummins Turbo Technologies, is known for producing robust and efficient turbochargers. The HX Series, such as the HX35 and HX40, are popular choices among the SC300 tuning community. These turbos offer excellent mid-range torque and can handle higher boost levels. Their durability and cost-effectiveness make them attractive options for enthusiasts on a budget.

HKS is a renowned name in the automotive aftermarket, and their Hipermax IV GT Coilover Kit lives up to the brand's reputation. For an affordable yet capable option, the Tein Flex Z Coilover Kit is a popular choice among SC300 owners. BC Racing BR Series Coilover Kit:

The BC Racing BR Series Coilover Kit offers a combination of performance, adjustability, and affordability. With its strong build and aluminum components, the BC Racing BR Series provides an excellent upgrade for Lexus SC300 owners seeking improved handling dynamics.

The EBC Brakes Stage 5 Superstreet Brake Kit is a comprehensive solution for enthusiasts seeking improved braking performance at a reasonable price point. Lexus SC300 Wheels

Achieving the ideal fitment and stance for a Lexus SC300 is an essential part of creating a striking and personalized appearance. Modifying the fitment involves optimizing the wheel and tire setup to achieve the perfect balance between form and function. In this article, we'll explore various options for modifying the fitment and stance of the Lexus SC300, complete with examples of specific wheel and rim specifications to inspire enthusiasts to create their dream setup.


Flush Fitment with Staggered Wheels:

A popular fitment choice for the Lexus SC300 is the flush fitment with staggered wheels. This setup involves using wider wheels and tires at the rear than at the front, resulting in a more aggressive stance. For instance, a common staggered fitment for the SC300 could be 18x8.5-inch wheels with 225/40R18 tires at the front and 18x9.5-inch wheels with 255/35R18 tires at the rear. This combination not only enhances aesthetics but also improves traction and handling dynamics.


Widebody Fitment with Deep Concave Wheels:

For those seeking an eye-catching and bold appearance, widebody fitment with deep concave wheels is an excellent option. A widebody kit allows for more aggressive fender flares, accommodating wider wheels and tires. For instance, a setup of 19x10-inch wheels with 265/30R19 tires on the front and 19x12-inch wheels with 305/30R19 tires on the rear would provide a stunning wide stance, making the SC300 stand out from the crowd.


Tucked Fitment with Air Suspension:

The tucked fitment with air suspension is perfect for SC300 owners who desire versatility and adjustability. Air suspension allows for on-the-fly height adjustments, offering the flexibility to go from an aggressive tuck to a more functional ride height with the push of a button. A typical setup could include 18x9-inch wheels with 215/40R18 tires on air suspension, providing a sleek and adjustable stance.


VIP Style Fitment with Deep Dish Wheels:

VIP style fitment exudes luxury and elegance. This setup involves using deep-dish wheels and stretched tires, giving the SC300 a refined and sophisticated look. For example, a VIP-style fitment could consist of 20x10-inch wheels with 235/30R20 tires all around, complemented by a smooth and lowered stance.


Track-Inspired Fitment with Lightweight Wheels:

For enthusiasts who prioritize performance, a track-inspired fitment with lightweight wheels is an excellent choice. Lightweight forged or flow-formed wheels enhance handling and responsiveness. A suitable setup could include 17x9-inch wheels with 245/40R17 tires, which not only offer improved performance but also create a sporty and purposeful appearance.
