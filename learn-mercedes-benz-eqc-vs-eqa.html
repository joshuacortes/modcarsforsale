Mercedes-Benz EQC vs. Mercedes-Benz EQA



Stock Motor

Acceleration (0-60 mph):

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC delivers impressive acceleration, achieving 0 to 60 mph in approximately 4.8 seconds. Its electric motors provide instantaneous torque, resulting in quick and smooth acceleration.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA offers competitive acceleration, reaching 0 to 60 mph in approximately 7.4 seconds. Its electric powertrain delivers a responsive and enjoyable driving experience.


Torque:
Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC generates a substantial torque of approximately 561 lb-ft. This high torque output contributes to its rapid acceleration and confident performance.


Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA's electric motor produces an impressive torque of approximately 277 lb-ft, showcasing its capable power delivery.


Horsepower:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC boasts a combined horsepower output of approximately 402 horsepower. Its electric motors provide ample power for a dynamic driving experience.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA generates a combined horsepower of approximately 188 horsepower, making it a capable performer in its class.


Top Speed:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC has an electronically limited top speed of approximately 112 mph, ensuring a controlled and safe driving experience.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA also has a top speed of approximately 112 mph, matching the EQC's electronically limited maximum speed.


Quarter Mile Time:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC completes the quarter-mile sprint in about 13.4 seconds, showcasing its impressive acceleration and performance.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA achieves a quarter-mile time of approximately 15.8 seconds, highlighting its competitive performance capabilities.


Lap Times:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC, being an electric SUV, is not typically considered a performance-oriented vehicle for track use. While it offers excellent acceleration and a smooth ride, its primary focus is on delivering a comfortable and luxurious driving experience.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: Similarly, the Mercedes-Benz EQA is not intended for track use, but it does offer agile handling and a composed driving experience, making it suitable for city driving and daily commutes.


Powertrain Configuration:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC features an all-wheel-drive powertrain configuration, with its electric motors driving both the front and rear wheels. This setup enhances traction and stability, especially in adverse road conditions.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA also utilizes an all-wheel-drive powertrain configuration, providing improved grip and traction across all four wheels.


Transmission Type:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC's electric powertrain utilizes a single-speed transmission, ensuring smooth and seamless power delivery.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA's electric motor also uses a single-speed transmission, providing optimal efficiency and performance.


Power to Weight Ratio:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC's power-to-weight ratio is influenced by its power output and overall weight, ensuring a balance of performance and efficiency.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: Similarly, the Mercedes-Benz EQA's power-to-weight ratio is influenced by its power output and weight, providing a capable and responsive driving experience.


RPM (Shaft Speed):

Mercedes-Benz EQC: Electric motors in the Mercedes-Benz EQC operate at high RPMs, delivering instant torque and a responsive driving experience. The precise RPM value varies depending on driving conditions and power demands.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA's electric motor also operates at high RPMs, ensuring quick and seamless power delivery.





Suspension

Ride Comfort:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC, being an electric luxury SUV, places a strong emphasis on ride comfort. Its suspension is tuned to absorb road imperfections and deliver a smooth and plush ride. This setup prioritizes passenger comfort, making it an ideal choice for long journeys and daily commuting.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA, while also offering a comfortable ride, features a slightly firmer suspension setup compared to the EQC. This choice provides a more engaging and dynamic driving experience without compromising on overall comfort.


Handling and Agility:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC's suspension is tuned to prioritize ride comfort over sporty handling. While it delivers a stable and composed ride, it may not match the agility and responsiveness of a sports sedan.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA, with its slightly firmer suspension tuning, offers more agile and responsive handling characteristics. It feels more nimble and engaging, making it well-suited for spirited driving on winding roads.


Ground Clearance:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC, being an SUV, offers a higher ground clearance compared to the EQA. This feature allows for a more commanding view of the road and better handling of uneven terrain and speed bumps.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA's lower ground clearance contributes to its sportier driving dynamics and a more car-like feel on the road. It also enhances aerodynamics and energy efficiency.


Suspension Components:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC is equipped with a multi-link rear suspension and a MacPherson strut front suspension. This setup ensures a smooth and stable ride, ideal for a luxury SUV.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA also features a MacPherson strut front suspension and a multi-link rear suspension, providing a well-balanced combination of comfort and handling.


Driving Modes:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC offers selectable driving modes, allowing the driver to choose between different suspension settings to match their driving preferences. The Comfort mode prioritizes a smoother ride, while the Sport mode offers a slightly firmer and more responsive feel.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: Similarly, the Mercedes-Benz EQA comes with driving modes that allow the driver to adjust the suspension characteristics. The Comfort mode offers a balanced ride, while the Sport mode delivers a more engaging and spirited driving experience.


Adaptive Damping:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: Some EQC models may come equipped with an adaptive damping system that adjusts the suspension settings based on road conditions and driving style. This feature further enhances ride comfort and handling dynamics.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA may also be available with an adaptive damping system, offering adjustable suspension settings for a more personalized driving experience.





Interior

Design and Layout:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC's interior exudes a sense of opulence and luxury. It features premium materials, such as leather upholstery, wood trims, and aluminum accents, creating an elegant and upscale ambiance. The cabin layout is ergonomic and driver-focused, providing easy access to essential controls and features.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA's interior design leans towards a sporty and youthful aesthetic. It incorporates modern materials like leatherette, brushed aluminum, and textured surfaces, presenting a contemporary and dynamic atmosphere. The cabin layout is practical and functional, catering to the needs of active and adventurous drivers.


Infotainment System:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC comes equipped with the latest iteration of the MBUX (Mercedes-Benz User Experience) infotainment system. It features a large central touchscreen display and an optional digital instrument cluster, offering crisp graphics and intuitive controls. MBUX provides seamless smartphone integration, voice command functionality, and a wide range of connected services.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA also features the MBUX infotainment system with a central touchscreen display and digital instrument cluster. While the display size may vary depending on the trim level, it still offers user-friendly interfaces and advanced connectivity features.


Seating and Comfort:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC offers plush and comfortable seating for both the driver and passengers. It provides ample legroom and headroom, ensuring a relaxed and luxurious ride. The seats are power-adjustable, heated, and ventilated in higher trims, offering customizable comfort.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA's seating provides ample support and comfort for occupants, with a focus on a sporty and ergonomic design. The front seats offer good bolstering for a dynamic driving experience.


Interior Space:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC, being a larger SUV, provides generous interior space for passengers and cargo. It can comfortably accommodate five adults and offers ample cargo capacity for luggage and belongings.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA, being a more compact SUV, offers a slightly smaller interior space compared to the EQC. While it can still accommodate five passengers, it may have a bit less rear legroom and cargo capacity.


Ambient Lighting:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC features customizable ambient lighting options, allowing drivers to set the mood with various color choices. Ambient lighting enhances the overall ambiance of the cabin, adding to the sense of luxury and comfort.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA may also come with ambient lighting features, albeit with a focus on a more youthful and dynamic lighting design.


Interior Trim Options:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC offers a range of premium interior trim options, allowing buyers to customize the cabin to their preferences. Options may include open-pore wood, carbon fiber, or high-gloss finishes, adding a touch of personalization.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA may also offer interior trim options, catering to a sportier and more contemporary design theme.


Sound System:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The Mercedes-Benz EQC can be equipped with a premium sound system, providing immersive audio quality for an enhanced driving experience. Brands like Burmester may offer high-fidelity sound solutions for audiophiles.

Mercedes-Benz EQA: The Mercedes-Benz EQA may also offer a premium sound system, ensuring impressive audio performance for its passengers.
