Mercedes-Benz EQC vs. Mercedes-Benz EQS



Stock Motor

Performance Metrics:


a. 0-60 mph Acceleration:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: Approximately 4.8 seconds.

Mercedes-Benz EQS: Approximately 4.3 seconds.


b. Torque:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: Around 561 lb-ft (760 Nm).

Mercedes-Benz EQS: An impressive 630 lb-ft (855 Nm).


c. Horsepower:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: 402 horsepower.

Mercedes-Benz EQS: A staggering 523 horsepower.


d. Top Speed:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: Electronically limited to 112 mph (180 km/h).

Mercedes-Benz EQS: Electronically limited to 130 mph (209 km/h).


e. Quarter Mile Time:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: Approximately 12.7 seconds.

Mercedes-Benz EQS: Approximately 12.0 seconds.


f. Lap Times:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: Specific lap time data not available.

Mercedes-Benz EQS: Specific lap time data not available.


Powertrain Configuration:



a. Mercedes-Benz EQC:

Dual-motor setup (one motor per axle) for all-wheel drive capability.

Sophisticated battery pack with a capacity of around 80 kWh.


b. Mercedes-Benz EQS:

Dual-motor setup with one motor per axle, delivering all-wheel drive performance.

Advanced battery pack with a capacity of around 108 kWh.

Transmission Type:


a. Mercedes-Benz EQC:

Single-speed automatic transmission.


b. Mercedes-Benz EQS:

Single-speed automatic transmission.

Power-to-Weight Ratio:


a. Mercedes-Benz EQC:

Approximate power-to-weight ratio of 192.3 W/kg.


b. Mercedes-Benz EQS:

Approximate power-to-weight ratio of 219.5 W/kg.

RPM (Shaft Speed):

a. Mercedes-Benz EQC:

Maximum RPM of the electric motor: Not specified.


b. Mercedes-Benz EQS:

Maximum RPM of the electric motor: Not specified.





Suspension

Suspension Type:

a. Mercedes-Benz EQC:

Front Suspension: Independent MacPherson struts.

Rear Suspension: Multi-link independent suspension.


b. Mercedes-Benz EQS:

Front Suspension: Multi-link independent suspension.

Rear Suspension: Multi-link independent suspension.

Air Suspension (Optional):


a. Mercedes-Benz EQC:

Optional Air Suspension: Available as an add-on for enhanced ride comfort and adjustable ride height.


b. Mercedes-Benz EQS:

Optional Air Suspension: Equipped with E-ACTIVE BODY CONTROL, a groundbreaking system that uses air suspension with adaptive dampers to offer unparalleled ride quality and stability.

Ride Comfort:


a. Mercedes-Benz EQC:

The standard suspension provides a balanced ride, but it can feel slightly firm over rough road surfaces.

With the optional air suspension, the EQC gains improved ride comfort, particularly on long journeys.


b. Mercedes-Benz EQS:

The EQS with its optional E-ACTIVE BODY CONTROL air suspension delivers exceptional ride comfort, smoothing out road imperfections and offering a supremely smooth driving experience.

The adaptive dampers adjust to road conditions in real-time, enhancing comfort and handling simultaneously.

Handling and Agility:


a. Mercedes-Benz EQC:

The standard suspension setup provides reasonable handling and stability.

While it is agile for an SUV, it may not match the sporty feel of some competitors.


b. Mercedes-Benz EQS:

The EQS, with its advanced multi-link independent suspension and E-ACTIVE BODY CONTROL, delivers exceptional handling characteristics.

The air suspension helps minimize body roll during cornering, contributing to a more composed and engaging driving experience.

Driving Modes:


a. Mercedes-Benz EQC:

The EQC typically comes with several driving modes, including Comfort, Eco, and Sport modes.

These modes may adjust the throttle response and steering feel but have limited impact on the suspension behavior.


b. Mercedes-Benz EQS:

The EQS features driving modes that influence the air suspension's characteristics significantly.

In Comfort mode, the suspension prioritizes a smooth and plush ride, whereas Sport mode enhances responsiveness and reduces body roll.

Load-Carrying Capacity:


a. Mercedes-Benz EQC:

The EQC offers a respectable load-carrying capacity suitable for everyday needs.


b. Mercedes-Benz EQS:

The EQS, being a luxury sedan, may have a slightly smaller trunk compared to the EQC, but it still provides ample space for most users.





Interior

Design Philosophy:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The EQC follows a more conventional design approach with a blend of contemporary and classic elements. The focus is on comfort and familiarity, catering to traditional luxury car enthusiasts.
Mercedes-Benz EQS: The EQS represents a paradigm shift in design philosophy. It embraces a futuristic and minimalist aesthetic, aimed at pushing the boundaries of automotive interior design.


Infotainment and Technology:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The EQC features a well-designed infotainment system with an intuitive user interface. It includes the MBUX (Mercedes-Benz User Experience) system with a central display and a touchpad controller on the center console.

Mercedes-Benz EQS: The EQS showcases the latest advancements in automotive technology. It incorporates the second generation of MBUX with a massive 56-inch curved Hyperscreen that spans the entire dashboard. This screen seamlessly integrates the instrument cluster, infotainment, and comfort controls.


Materials and Finishes:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The EQC's interior boasts high-quality materials such as leather, wood trims, and premium soft-touch surfaces. The cabin exudes elegance and comfort, creating a plush and inviting atmosphere.

Mercedes-Benz EQS: Going above and beyond, the EQS elevates the luxury quotient even further. It introduces new sustainable materials like Dinamica microfiber and uses high-end natural wood veneers with intricate detailing, making it a paragon of eco-luxury.


Cabin Space:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The EQC offers a spacious cabin, providing ample legroom and headroom for both front and rear passengers. It comfortably accommodates five occupants, making it a practical choice for families.

Mercedes-Benz EQS: As the EQS is a flagship luxury sedan, it takes passenger comfort to new heights. Its long wheelbase creates a vast amount of legroom in the rear, and the available Executive Rear Seat package offers first-class-like amenities.


Ambient Lighting:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The EQC includes ambient lighting with a variety of color options to create a soothing ambiance inside the cabin.

Mercedes-Benz EQS: The EQS takes ambient lighting to a whole new level with the "Energizing Comfort" feature, which synchronizes lighting, music, climate control, and fragrance to enhance the well-being of passengers.


Sound System:

Mercedes-Benz EQC: The EQC is equipped with a premium sound system that delivers a remarkable audio experience.

Mercedes-Benz EQS: For the music enthusiasts, the EQS offers an optional Burmester® High-End 4D Surround Sound system, utilizing 30 speakers and revolutionary 4D sound technology, further immersing occupants in an acoustic wonderland.
