BMW i8 vs. Corvette C8



Stock Motor

BMW i8 Stock Motor: 0-60 Acceleration: The BMW i8 accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in approximately 4.2 seconds, showcasing its impressive hybrid power and electric performance. Torque: The i8 produces a combined torque output of around 420 lb-ft, combining the power from its electric motor and gasoline engine. Horsepower: The stock motor of the BMW i8 generates approximately 369 horsepower, delivering a balanced mix of electric and gasoline-powered performance. Top Speed: The BMW i8 boasts an electronically limited top speed of 155 mph, ensuring controlled and exhilarating performance. Quarter Mile Time: The i8 completes the quarter-mile run in approximately 12.6 seconds, showcasing its brisk acceleration capabilities. Lap Times: While not designed as a track-focused car, the i8's hybrid powertrain allows for instantaneous torque delivery during corner exits. Powertrain Configuration: The BMW i8 features a plug-in hybrid electric powertrain, comprising a turbocharged three-cylinder gasoline engine and an electric motor. Transmission Type: The i8 is equipped with a 6-speed automatic transmission, complementing its hybrid setup for seamless power delivery. Power-to-Weight Ratio: With a curb weight of approximately 3,500 lbs and a horsepower output of 369 hp, the i8 achieves a commendable power-to-weight ratio. RPM (Shaft Speed): The BMW i8's gasoline engine can rev up to approximately 7,000 RPM, while the electric motor operates at variable RPMs based on demand. Chevrolet Corvette C8 Stock Motor: 0-60 Acceleration: The Chevrolet Corvette C8 accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in approximately 2.9 seconds, showcasing its raw mid-engine performance. Torque: The Corvette C8 produces around 470 lb-ft of torque, delivering rapid acceleration and exhilarating straight-line speed. Horsepower: The stock motor of the Chevrolet Corvette C8 generates approximately 490 horsepower, providing impressive power and performance. Top Speed: The Corvette C8 reaches a top speed of 194 mph, making it one of the fastest production Corvettes ever built. Quarter Mile Time: The C8 completes the quarter-mile run in approximately 11.2 seconds, showcasing its drag strip capabilities. Lap Times: The Corvette C8's mid-engine layout and track-focused suspension enable impressive lap times on race circuits. Powertrain Configuration: The Chevrolet Corvette C8 features a mid-engine layout, with a 6.2-liter V8 engine positioned behind the driver. Transmission Type: The C8 is equipped with an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, providing quick and precise gear changes. Power-to-Weight Ratio: With a curb weight of approximately 3,600 lbs and a horsepower output of 490 hp, the C8 boasts an impressive power-to-weight ratio. RPM (Shaft Speed): The Chevrolet Corvette C8's V8 engine can rev up to approximately 6,500 RPM, delivering a powerful soundtrack on acceleration.





Suspension

BMW i8 Suspension: Front Suspension: The BMW i8 features a double-wishbone front suspension, providing precise wheel control and responsive handling during cornering. Rear Suspension: At the rear, the i8 utilizes a multi-link suspension setup, contributing to a comfortable ride and stability during high-speed maneuvers. Adaptive Damping: The i8 comes with adaptive suspension damping, which continuously adjusts shock absorber stiffness based on driving conditions for improved comfort and performance. Lightweight Materials: The i8's suspension components are crafted from lightweight materials, reducing unsprung weight and enhancing agility. Comfort-Oriented: The i8's suspension tuning leans towards a comfortable ride, making it suitable for long-distance cruising and daily use. Reduced Body Roll: The i8's suspension setup minimizes body roll during cornering, enhancing the car's stability and control. Chevrolet Corvette C8 Suspension: Front Suspension: The Chevrolet Corvette C8 is equipped with a double-wishbone front suspension, providing precise steering response and excellent handling characteristics. Rear Suspension: At the rear, the C8 features a double-wishbone suspension, contributing to improved traction and stability during acceleration. Magnetic Ride Control: The Corvette C8 offers an available Magnetic Ride Control system, which uses magnetorheological fluid to adjust damping rates rapidly, delivering both comfort and enhanced performance. Stiff Chassis: The C8's rigid chassis, combined with its mid-engine layout, results in reduced body flex and improved handling precision. Sport-Tuned Handling: The Corvette C8's suspension tuning prioritizes sporty and performance-oriented handling, making it an ideal choice for track enthusiasts. Driver-Adjustable Settings: The C8 offers different driving modes, including Track, Sport, and Touring, allowing drivers to adjust the suspension to suit their preferences and driving conditions. On-Road Comfort - BMW i8: Smooth Ride: The i8's adaptive suspension offers a smooth and comfortable ride, ideal for daily commuting and longer journeys. Hybrid Efficiency: The i8's suspension tuning complements its hybrid powertrain, providing an eco-friendly and refined driving experience. On-Road Comfort - Chevrolet Corvette C8: Firm Sportiness: The Corvette C8's suspension setup, especially with the available Magnetic Ride Control, delivers a firm yet comfortable ride, ideal for spirited driving on both highways and twisty roads. Performance-oriented Handling: The C8's suspension is tuned to prioritize precise handling and response, offering drivers a connected and thrilling on-road experience. Track Performance - BMW i8: Suitable for Spirited Driving: While not a dedicated track car, the i8's suspension provides adequate handling for spirited driving and occasional track days. Track Performance - Chevrolet Corvette C8: Track-Focused Performance: The C8's suspension, combined with its mid-engine layout, delivers exceptional track performance, making it a formidable contender on race circuits.





Interior

BMW i8 Interior:

Futuristic Design: The BMW i8's interior boasts a futuristic and driver-centric design, embracing the car's hybrid nature and advanced technology.

High-Quality Materials: The i8 features premium materials, such as leather, carbon fiber, and metal accents, creating a luxurious and sporty ambiance.

Digital Instrument Cluster: The i8's digital instrument cluster displays crucial driving information and can be customized to suit the driver's preferences.

User-Friendly Controls: The i8's controls are intuitively placed, allowing for easy access to various functions, including hybrid drive modes and infotainment.

Seating and Comfort: The i8 offers supportive and comfortable sport seats, ensuring a pleasant driving experience during both spirited drives and daily commutes.

Limited Storage Space: Due to the car's compact size and hybrid powertrain components, the i8 has limited storage space in the cabin and trunk.

High-Tech Infotainment: The i8 comes with BMW's iDrive infotainment system, featuring a user-friendly interface and a large touchscreen display.

Hybrid-Specific Information: The i8's infotainment system provides specific information about the hybrid powertrain's performance and energy usage.


Chevrolet Corvette C8 Interior:

Driver-Centric Cockpit: The Corvette C8's interior follows a driver-centric design philosophy, with essential controls within easy reach for an immersive driving experience.

Quality Materials: The C8 features a combination of premium materials, such as leather, suede, and carbon fiber accents, exuding a high-quality and performance-oriented feel.

Digital and Customizable Cluster: The C8's digital instrument cluster offers a customizable display, allowing drivers to tailor the information according to their preferences.

Infotainment System: The C8 is equipped with Chevrolet's Infotainment 3 Plus system, providing seamless connectivity and user-friendly controls.

Spacious and Comfortable Seating: The C8's seats offer ample support and comfort, making it suitable for long drives and extended track sessions.

Dual-Zone Climate Control: The C8 comes with dual-zone climate control, ensuring both the driver and passenger can set their preferred temperature.

Storage Capacity: Compared to the i8, the Corvette C8 offers more generous storage space, both in the cabin and trunk, making it more practical for everyday use.

Performance Data Recorder: The C8 is available with an optional Performance Data Recorder, allowing drivers to record and analyze their track sessions.


On-Road Comfort - BMW i8:


Hybrid Comfort: The i8's interior provides a comfortable and luxurious cabin, suitable for daily driving and long-distance journeys.


On-Road Comfort - Chevrolet Corvette C8:


Performance Comfort: The C8's interior strikes a balance between performance-oriented features and driver comfort, making it ideal for both spirited driving and everyday use.


Driver-Focused Technology - BMW i8:


Hybrid-Specific Information: The i8's infotainment system offers specific details about the hybrid powertrain, encouraging efficient driving and energy management.


Driver-Focused Technology - Chevrolet Corvette C8:


Track-Focused Technology: The C8's Performance Data Recorder and driver-centric controls are geared towards enhancing the driving experience, especially during track sessions.
