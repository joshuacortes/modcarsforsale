Volkswagen ID.4 vs. Volkswagen ID.3



Stock Motor

Acceleration (0-60 mph):

Volkswagen ID.4: The ID.4 is equipped with a responsive electric motor, allowing it to reach 0 to 60 mph in approximately 8.5 seconds.

Volkswagen ID.3: On the other hand, the ID.3 showcases its sportier side, achieving the 0-60 mph sprint in an impressive 7.0 seconds.


Torque:

Volkswagen ID.4: The ID.4 boasts a robust torque figure of around 229 lb-ft, providing excellent low-end power for a smooth driving experience.

Volkswagen ID.3: With a slightly higher torque of 240 lb-ft, the ID.3 delivers an exhilarating acceleration and responsive performance.


Horsepower:

Volkswagen ID.4: The ID.4's electric motor generates approximately 201 horsepower, offering a balanced combination of power and efficiency.

Volkswagen ID.3: The ID.3 outshines its sibling in this aspect, boasting around 201 horsepower as well, but its sportier tuning makes it feel more dynamic.


Top Speed:

Volkswagen ID.4: The ID.4 showcases an electronically limited top speed of approximately 99 mph, catering to the demands of daily commutes and city driving.

Volkswagen ID.3: With a higher top speed of around 99 mph, the ID.3 exhibits an eagerness for spirited highway driving.


Quarter Mile Time:

Volkswagen ID.4: The ID.4 achieves a quarter-mile time of roughly 16.5 seconds, demonstrating its competency in straight-line acceleration.

Volkswagen ID.3: On the other hand, the ID.3 manages to conquer the quarter-mile in around 15.5 seconds, highlighting its performance-oriented nature.


Lap Times:

Volkswagen ID.4: Although not designed for racetrack domination, the ID.4 delivers respectable lap times, making it a reliable and comfortable urban companion.

Volkswagen ID.3: The ID.3, with its sportier suspension and handling setup, fares better on the track, offering enjoyable driving dynamics and competitive lap times.


Powertrain Configuration:

Both the Volkswagen ID.4 and ID.3 feature an all-electric powertrain, embracing sustainable mobility and zero-emission driving.


Transmission Type:

Volkswagen ID.4: The ID.4 incorporates a single-speed transmission, providing seamless acceleration and reduced complexity.

Volkswagen ID.3: Like its sibling, the ID.3 also utilizes a single-speed transmission, allowing for smooth power delivery and minimal maintenance requirements.


Power-to-Weight Ratio:

Volkswagen ID.4: With its larger and more spacious design, the ID.4 has a slightly lower power-to-weight ratio compared to the ID.3.

Volkswagen ID.3: The ID.3's sportier and more compact build gives it a slightly higher power-to-weight ratio, contributing to its energetic performance.


RPM (Shaft Speed):

Both the Volkswagen ID.4 and ID.3 operate at a high RPM (Revolutions Per Minute) to harness the full potential of their electric motors, providing instant acceleration and power delivery.





Tuning Ability

Upgraded Intake:

An improved intake system can boost airflow and efficiency, enhancing the overall performance of an electric vehicle.


Volkswagen ID.4: While the ID.4's stock intake already performs optimally for its intended purpose, some aftermarket companies offer high-flow air filters that can provide a marginal increase in airflow and slight performance gains.

Volkswagen ID.3: Similarly, the ID.3's intake system is well-designed, but aftermarket cold air intake kits can offer improved efficiency and power gains, especially when combined with other modifications.


Exhaust Upgrades:

Upgrading the exhaust system can enhance sound, reduce backpressure, and potentially lead to performance improvements.

Volkswagen ID.4: As an electric vehicle, the ID.4 does not have a traditional exhaust system. However, some companies offer simulated sound modules that can mimic the sound of a traditional engine, providing a more engaging driving experience.

Volkswagen ID.3: The ID.3, like the ID.4, lacks a conventional exhaust system. However, aftermarket companies offer exhaust tip enhancements to give the vehicle a sportier appearance.


Forced Induction:

Forced induction systems, such as turbochargers or superchargers, can significantly increase the power output of an internal combustion engine but are not applicable to electric vehicles.

Volkswagen ID.4: As an electric vehicle, the ID.4 does not have an internal combustion engine and, therefore, does not have the potential for forced induction upgrades.

Volkswagen ID.3: Likewise, the ID.3's electric powertrain does not allow for forced induction modifications.


Fuel Management:

Although electric vehicles do not use traditional fuel systems, optimizing power delivery and battery management can be vital in extracting maximum performance.

Volkswagen ID.4: The ID.4's stock power management system is designed to balance efficiency and performance. While aftermarket tuning options are limited, software updates from Volkswagen may become available to improve performance in the future.

Volkswagen ID.3: Similarly, the ID.3's power management system is finely tuned from the factory. However, over time, software updates may enhance performance and efficiency for ID.3 owners.


Ignition Options:

Electric vehicles do not have spark plugs or traditional ignition systems, but software updates can influence power delivery and performance.

Volkswagen ID.4: The ID.4's ignition system, driven by software and the vehicle's battery management, is optimized for efficiency and reliability. Any changes to the ignition system would be limited to official software updates.

Volkswagen ID.3: The ID.3's ignition system, like the ID.4, relies on software and battery management. Any modifications would depend on official software updates from Volkswagen.





Suspension

Ride Comfort:

Volkswagen ID.4: Known for its family-oriented design and spacious interior, the ID.4 prioritizes ride comfort. Its suspension is tuned to provide a smooth and cushioned ride, making it an ideal choice for daily commutes and long journeys. The suspension system effectively absorbs road imperfections, providing passengers with a comfortable and relaxed driving experience.

Volkswagen ID.3: With a sportier orientation, the ID.3's suspension is set up to offer a firmer ride. While not uncomfortably stiff, the focus here is on delivering a more connected and engaging driving experience. This setup may transmit slightly more road irregularities to the cabin, but it rewards the driver with better feedback and control during spirited driving.


Handling and Agility:

Volkswagen ID.4: As a compact SUV, the ID.4's suspension prioritizes stability and predictability. It offers a well-balanced and composed ride, making it easy to maneuver around corners and negotiate urban environments. While not as nimble as a smaller car, the ID.4's suspension strikes a commendable compromise between comfort and handling prowess.

Volkswagen ID.3: Geared towards offering a sportier driving experience, the ID.3's suspension is finely tuned for agility and responsiveness. Its lower center of gravity, compared to the ID.4, contributes to enhanced cornering capabilities and a more connected feel to the road. Enthusiasts will appreciate the ID.3's eagerness to take on twisty roads with confidence.


Ground Clearance:

Volkswagen ID.4: As an SUV, the ID.4 boasts a higher ground clearance than the ID.3. This feature allows the ID.4 to tackle uneven terrain and handle rough roads more adeptly. It also provides a commanding driving position, contributing to better visibility and a sense of security.

Volkswagen ID.3: The ID.3, being a compact hatchback, sits closer to the ground compared to the ID.4. While this lower profile contributes to improved aerodynamics and handling, it may not be as practical for those who frequently encounter challenging road conditions.


Weight Distribution:

Volkswagen ID.4: With its larger size and SUV architecture, the ID.4 exhibits a more rear-biased weight distribution. This characteristic enhances stability during straight-line driving and contributes to a planted feel on the road.

Volkswagen ID.3: As a smaller hatchback, the ID.3's weight distribution is relatively more balanced. This attribute, combined with its sportier suspension tuning, allows the ID.3 to deliver nimble and responsive handling, particularly during quick maneuvers.


Driving Modes:

Volkswagen ID.4: The ID.4 often offers a range of driving modes, including a comfort mode for relaxed driving and an eco mode for enhanced efficiency. These modes adjust the suspension settings and other vehicle parameters to match the desired driving style.

Volkswagen ID.3: Like the ID.4, the ID.3 is also equipped with multiple driving modes. However, these modes are tailored to suit the sportier nature of the vehicle, offering a sharper throttle response and a more engaging suspension setup.





Interior

Design and Layout:

Volkswagen ID.4: The ID.4 embraces a more family-friendly design with a focus on functionality and spaciousness. Its interior layout is well-organized, providing easy access to controls and a user-friendly interface. The cabin exudes a sense of simplicity, emphasizing practicality over extravagance.

Volkswagen ID.3: Taking a sportier approach, the ID.3's interior is crafted to reflect a more dynamic and driver-centric environment. The layout is driver-oriented, with a focus on essential controls within easy reach, providing a more engaging and immersive driving experience.


Space and Seating:

Volkswagen ID.4: As a compact SUV, the ID.4 offers generous cabin space, making it suitable for families or those who require ample legroom and headroom. The rear seats provide enough space for adult passengers, ensuring a comfortable ride for everyone.

Volkswagen ID.3: Being a hatchback, the ID.3 prioritizes practicality and maneuverability. While it may not offer as much space as the ID.4, the ID.3's interior is still well-designed and accommodates passengers comfortably, especially for urban driving.


Infotainment System:

Volkswagen ID.4: The ID.4 features Volkswagen's latest infotainment system, typically displayed on a large touchscreen. It provides seamless integration with smartphones, offering various connectivity options. The system focuses on user-friendliness, allowing occupants to access essential functions with ease.

Volkswagen ID.3: Similarly, the ID.3 is equipped with an advanced infotainment system, but it may differ slightly from the ID.4. The touchscreen display and overall user experience aim to cater to the driver's needs, emphasizing essential information and controls.


Material Quality:

Volkswagen ID.4: As a family-oriented SUV, the ID.4's interior materials prioritize durability and functionality. While it may not feature the highest-grade luxury materials, the fit and finish are of good quality, ensuring a reliable and comfortable cabin experience.

Volkswagen ID.3: As a more performance-oriented vehicle, the ID.3 may offer sportier touches and a slightly more refined interior. However, like the ID.4, it strikes a balance between practicality and aesthetics.


Interior Ambiance:

Volkswagen ID.4: The ID.4's interior ambiance aims for a relaxed and inviting atmosphere. Comfortable seats and thoughtful design elements create a welcoming environment, ideal for long journeys and daily commutes.

Volkswagen ID.3: With a sportier orientation, the ID.3's interior ambiance may feel more energetic and focused on driving engagement. Supportive seats and sporty accents enhance the driving experience, particularly during spirited drives.


Cargo Space:

Volkswagen ID.4: The ID.4's larger size and SUV architecture provide ample cargo space, making it suitable for families and outdoor enthusiasts. The rear cargo area offers enough room for luggage, groceries, or sports equipment.

Volkswagen ID.3: While the ID.3's cargo space may not be as extensive as the ID.4's, it still offers sufficient room for daily errands and urban living. Its hatchback design allows for more accessible loading and unloading of items.
