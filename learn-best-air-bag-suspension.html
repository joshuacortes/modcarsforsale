Best Air Bag Suspension



When it comes to optimizing the ride comfort and handling of street cars, air bag suspension systems have emerged as a popular choice among automotive enthusiasts. Air bags offer adjustability, allowing drivers to fine-tune their car's height, stiffness, and responsiveness to suit their preferences. In this article, we'll explore some of the best air bag suspension systems available for various popular car models, enhancing both aesthetics and performance.


Air Lift Performance 3P:

The Air Lift Performance 3P is a top-notch air bag suspension kit known for its reliability, ease of use, and exceptional performance. It features a 3P management system, allowing for precise adjustment of each wheel independently. This kit is suitable for the following popular car models:


Volkswagen Golf GTI: The Air Lift Performance 3P kit enhances the GTI's handling and provides a smoother ride quality, making it a popular choice among Golf GTI enthusiasts.

Subaru WRX/STI: The WRX and STI benefit from improved traction and cornering capabilities with this kit, elevating the driving experience to a new level.


AccuAir e-Level:

The AccuAir e-Level air bag suspension system stands out for its advanced technology and user-friendly interface. The e-Level system automatically adjusts the car's ride height based on pre-programmed settings, offering a hassle-free experience. This system is well-suited for the following car models:

Audi A4/A5: The AccuAir e-Level system complements the A4/A5's sophisticated ride with its seamless adjustment capabilities, delivering both comfort and performance.

BMW 3 Series (E46): For BMW E46 owners, the AccuAir e-Level kit provides improved stance and handling, elevating the car's driving dynamics.


KW Suspensions Height Adjustable Spring System (H.A.S):

The KW Suspensions Height Adjustable Spring System is an excellent option for drivers seeking the convenience of a coilover-like system without sacrificing the stock shocks. This setup offers height adjustability while retaining the car's factory dampers. Popular car models that can benefit from this system include:

Honda Civic Type R: The KW Suspensions H.A.S kit allows Type R owners to fine-tune their ride height and achieve a perfect balance of comfort and sportiness.

Ford Mustang GT: Mustang GT owners can experience improved handling and adjustability with this suspension system, making it an ideal choice for street driving and occasional track days.

AirRide Technologies (RideTech):

AirRide Technologies, now known as RideTech, is a well-established brand in the air suspension industry. They offer a wide range of air suspension products, including air bags, air compressors, and management systems. RideTech's suspension systems are known for their durability, adjustability, and excellent ride quality.


Airlift Company:

Airlift Company, the manufacturer behind Air Lift Performance, is a leading name in the air suspension market. They produce air bag kits designed for various vehicle makes and models, ensuring a smooth ride and improved handling capabilities. Airlift's suspension products are known for their user-friendly setup and reliability.


AccuAir Suspension:

Apart from their e-Level system, AccuAir Suspension offers a comprehensive range of air suspension components, including air bags, compressors, and electronic management systems. Their products are highly regarded for their precision and innovative technology, providing drivers with unmatched adjustability and ride control.


Universal Air Suspension:

Universal Air Suspension (UAS) is another reputable brand known for manufacturing high-quality air suspension kits and components. UAS offers a diverse range of products suitable for various vehicles, allowing drivers to customize their ride height, stance, and handling characteristics.


Firestone Industrial Products:

Firestone Industrial Products is a trusted brand with decades of experience in manufacturing air springs and suspension components. They provide reliable and durable air bags suitable for a wide range of applications, from passenger cars to heavy-duty trucks.


Slam Specialties:

Slam Specialties focuses on producing premium air suspension bags that cater to the needs of automotive enthusiasts. Their air springs are known for their performance-oriented design and durability, making them a popular choice for car enthusiasts who demand precise handling and ride comfort.


D2 Racing Air Struts:

D2 Racing is primarily known for their coilover suspension systems, but they also offer air struts that combine the benefits of coilovers and air suspension. Their air struts allow drivers to adjust ride height and damping, providing a versatile and customizable suspension solution.

Investing in a quality air bag suspension system can significantly enhance the ride comfort and performance of street cars. The Air Lift Performance 3P, AccuAir e-Level, and KW Suspensions H.A.S are among the best options available for popular car models, offering adjustability, reliability, and seamless integration.

Before selecting a specific air bag suspension kit, it's essential to consider factors such as the intended use, budget, and compatibility with the car model. Always consult with professional installers or automotive experts to ensure a proper fit and setup. With the right air bag suspension system, drivers can experience a whole new level of driving pleasure, combining style, comfort, and performance in perfect harmony.
