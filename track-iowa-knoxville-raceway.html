Common Events and Unforgettable Races Knoxville Raceway's calendar is marked by a series of thrilling events that cater to die-hard fans and curious newcomers alike. The track's reputation as the "Sprint Car Capital of the World" draws drivers and spectators from all corners of the globe. Here are some common events that take place at Knoxville Raceway: Knoxville Nationals: The crown jewel of Knoxville Raceway's schedule is undoubtedly the Knoxville Nationals. Held annually, this event is the pinnacle of sprint car racing, attracting the best drivers in the sport to battle for the prestigious title. The four-day spectacle is a celebration of horsepower, skill, and determination that culminates in the crowning of a champion. Weekly Racing: Knoxville Raceway's regular racing program provides local drivers with a platform to showcase their talents. The weekly racing events feature various sprint car divisions, modifieds, and more, creating a dynamic atmosphere that keeps fans coming back week after week. Corn Belt Nationals: This relatively new event has quickly become a fan favorite. The Corn Belt Nationals features a variety of sprint car divisions and showcases top-notch competition over multiple nights, delivering high-speed thrills and heart-pounding action. Capitani Classic: The Capitani Classic is a prelude to the Knoxville Nationals, serving as a thrilling showcase of drivers' abilities before the main event. It's a must-see event that offers a taste of what's to come during the Nationals. Fan-Focused Activities: Beyond the races, Knoxville Raceway offers interactive experiences for fans. Autograph sessions, driver meet-and-greets, and opportunities to explore the pits provide an up-close and personal connection to the world of dirt track racing.
