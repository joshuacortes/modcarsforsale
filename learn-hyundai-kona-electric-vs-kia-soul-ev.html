Hyundai Kona Electric vs. Kia Soul EV



Stock Motor

Acceleration (0-60 mph):

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Hyundai Kona Electric delivers swift acceleration, reaching 0 to 60 mph in approximately 6.4 seconds. Its electric powertrain provides instant torque, offering impressive acceleration from a standstill.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV also offers quick acceleration, achieving 0 to 60 mph in approximately 7.6 seconds. Its electric motor provides responsive acceleration for an enjoyable driving experience.


Torque:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Hyundai Kona Electric generates substantial torque, totaling around 291 lb-ft. This high torque output contributes to its quick acceleration and responsive performance.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's electric motor produces a commendable torque of approximately 291 lb-ft, matching the Kona Electric's impressive torque figures.


Horsepower:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Hyundai Kona Electric delivers a total horsepower output of approximately 201 horsepower. Its electric motor delivers ample power for a satisfying driving experience.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's electric motor generates a combined horsepower of approximately 201 horsepower, offering a similar power output to the Kona Electric.


Top Speed:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Hyundai Kona Electric has a top speed of around 104 mph, ensuring a controlled and safe driving experience.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's top speed is also around 104 mph, showcasing similar performance capabilities to the Kona Electric.


Quarter Mile Time:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Hyundai Kona Electric completes the quarter-mile sprint in about 15.3 seconds, highlighting its impressive acceleration and performance.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV achieves a quarter-mile time of approximately 16 seconds, reflecting its respectable acceleration and responsive electric motor.


Lap Times:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Hyundai Kona Electric is not typically considered a performance-oriented vehicle for track use. While it offers excellent acceleration and handling, its primary focus is on delivering a comfortable and efficient driving experience.

Kia Soul EV: Like the Kona Electric, the Kia Soul EV is not intended for track use, and its suspension setup prioritizes ride comfort and everyday drivability.


Powertrain Configuration:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Hyundai Kona Electric features a front-wheel-drive powertrain configuration. Its electric motor drives the front wheels, providing efficient and responsive power delivery.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV is also equipped with a front-wheel-drive powertrain, similar to the Kona Electric, optimizing efficiency and power distribution.


Transmission Type:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Hyundai Kona Electric utilizes a single-speed transmission, characteristic of electric vehicles. The absence of gear changes ensures smooth and seamless acceleration.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's electric powertrain similarly uses a single-speed transmission, optimizing efficiency and power delivery.


Power to Weight Ratio:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Hyundai Kona Electric's power-to-weight ratio is influenced by its power output and overall weight, ensuring a balance of performance and efficiency.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's power-to-weight ratio may be similar to the Kona Electric, considering both models share the same electric motor and battery technology.


RPM (Shaft Speed):

Hyundai Kona Electric: Electric motors in the Hyundai Kona Electric operate at high RPMs, delivering instant torque and responsive acceleration. The precise RPM value varies depending on driving conditions and power demands.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's electric motor also operates at high RPMs, ensuring a quick and seamless power delivery for a smooth driving experience.





Tuning Ability

Performance Software Tuning:

Hyundai Kona Electric: Performance software tuning, also known as "chipping" or "remapping," is a popular method to optimize an EV's power delivery, torque management, and throttle response. Reputable tuning companies can offer custom software upgrades that enhance the Kona Electric's acceleration and overall driving experience.

Kia Soul EV: Similarly, the Kia Soul EV can benefit from performance software tuning to fine-tune its electric powertrain. Customized software upgrades can improve responsiveness and power delivery, making the Soul EV even more engaging to drive.


Regenerative Braking Settings:

Hyundai Kona Electric: Regenerative braking is a key feature of EVs, and adjusting its settings can affect the vehicle's energy recuperation and braking performance. Tuning the regenerative braking settings can provide a more natural driving experience for enthusiasts and improve energy efficiency.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's regenerative braking system can also be adjusted through software tuning. Fine-tuning the regenerative braking levels can offer a more personalized driving experience and optimize energy regeneration during deceleration.


Suspension Upgrades:

Hyundai Kona Electric: Upgraded suspension components can enhance the Kona Electric's handling and cornering capabilities. Aftermarket performance springs, dampers, and sway bars can be considered for sportier dynamics.

Kia Soul EV: Similarly, the Kia Soul EV can benefit from suspension upgrades, providing improved handling and stability during spirited driving.


Brake Upgrades:

Hyundai Kona Electric: Upgraded brake components, such as performance brake pads and high-performance rotors, can improve the Kona Electric's braking performance, providing confident and precise stopping power.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's brake system can also be upgraded with high-performance brake components for improved braking performance during spirited driving.


Tire and Wheel Upgrades:

Hyundai Kona Electric: Upgrading to performance-oriented tires can enhance the Kona Electric's grip and handling characteristics. Additionally, lightweight aftermarket wheels can reduce unsprung weight, further improving agility and responsiveness.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV can also benefit from tire and wheel upgrades, providing better grip and enhancing the car's overall driving dynamics.


Battery Management System (BMS) Calibration:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Kona Electric's battery management system plays a crucial role in controlling energy flow and battery efficiency. Optimizing the BMS calibration can improve the vehicle's range and performance.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's BMS calibration can also be fine-tuned to optimize energy management and battery performance.


Weight Reduction:

Hyundai Kona Electric: Reducing weight is an essential aspect of performance tuning for any vehicle. While extensive weight reduction may not be practical for an EV, minor weight-saving modifications can still contribute to improved acceleration and handling.

Kia Soul EV: Similar to the Kona Electric, the Kia Soul EV can benefit from minor weight reduction efforts to enhance its electric performance.


Cooling System Upgrades:

Hyundai Kona Electric: Keeping the electric motor and battery cool is vital for sustained performance. Upgraded cooling systems can ensure the Kona Electric operates optimally during spirited driving or extended use.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's cooling system can also be upgraded to maintain optimal operating temperatures and maximize performance.





Suspension

Ride Comfort:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Hyundai Kona Electric boasts a comfortable and compliant ride quality, designed to provide a smooth journey for occupants. Its suspension setup is tuned to absorb road imperfections and deliver a cushioned feel, making it an ideal choice for daily commuting and long-distance drives.

Kia Soul EV: Similarly, the Kia Soul EV prioritizes ride comfort with its suspension tuning. Its well-calibrated suspension absorbs road irregularities and enhances passenger comfort, providing a relaxed and enjoyable ride.


Handling and Agility:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Kona Electric's suspension strikes a balance between ride comfort and agility. While it may not offer sports car-like handling, it still provides a stable and predictable driving experience, making it suitable for urban maneuvering and highway cruising.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV also offers responsive handling, although it leans more towards comfort-oriented suspension tuning. It's nimble enough for city driving, offering a fun and engaging driving experience.


Ground Clearance:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Kona Electric features a slightly elevated ground clearance, which is beneficial for handling rough roads and minor off-road adventures. This feature adds to its versatility and practicality as a compact SUV.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's ground clearance is comparable to that of the Kona Electric, making it equally suitable for handling urban roads and occasional light off-road conditions.


Weight Distribution:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Kona Electric's weight distribution is well-balanced, thanks to its battery pack's positioning. This balanced weight distribution contributes to the vehicle's stability and confident handling.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV also maintains a balanced weight distribution, ensuring a stable and controlled ride.


Driving Modes:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Kona Electric offers selectable driving modes, such as Eco, Comfort, and Sport. These modes allow drivers to tailor the vehicle's suspension and power delivery to their preferences and driving conditions.

Kia Soul EV: Similarly, the Kia Soul EV comes with driving modes that enable drivers to customize the vehicle's performance characteristics, including its suspension response.


Suspension Components:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Kona Electric is equipped with a MacPherson strut front suspension and a multi-link rear suspension. This setup provides a stable and well-balanced ride, complementing the vehicle's overall performance.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's suspension setup also includes a MacPherson strut front suspension and a coupled torsion beam rear suspension, offering a comfortable and composed ride for passengers.


Brake Upgrades:

Hyundai Kona Electric: Upgraded brake components can improve the Kona Electric's braking performance, providing confident and precise stopping power.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's brake system is designed for effective braking performance, ensuring safety and control during everyday driving.





Interior

Design and Layout:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Hyundai Kona Electric's interior design is modern and well-crafted, featuring high-quality materials and a user-friendly layout. Its cabin exudes a contemporary ambiance, with a focus on simplicity and functionality.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's interior design is equally attractive, with a blend of soft-touch materials and appealing aesthetics. Its cabin offers a playful and vibrant vibe, reflecting the Soul's distinctive personality.


Infotainment System:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Kona Electric comes equipped with a user-friendly infotainment system. A large touchscreen display provides access to navigation, multimedia, and various vehicle settings, making it convenient for the driver and passengers.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV also boasts an intuitive and feature-rich infotainment system. Its touchscreen display offers seamless smartphone integration and user-friendly controls.


Digital Instrument Cluster:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Kona Electric features a digital instrument cluster that displays essential driving information, including battery status, range, and driving mode. Its clear and informative layout keeps the driver informed during the journey.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's digital instrument cluster also provides relevant driving information, emphasizing efficiency and battery status. Its design complements the Soul EV's modern interior theme.


Seating and Comfort:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Hyundai Kona Electric offers comfortable and supportive seats for all occupants. The front seats are power-adjustable, and some trims may include heating and ventilation options for enhanced comfort.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's seats prioritize comfort, providing ample space and support for both the driver and passengers. Heated front seats come standard, with available ventilation for additional comfort.


Interior Space:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Kona Electric's cabin is well-designed to maximize space and practicality. Its compact SUV design offers generous headroom and legroom for occupants.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV's boxy design contributes to its spacious interior. It offers ample headroom and legroom for passengers, ensuring a comfortable ride for all.


Cargo Capacity:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Kona Electric's cargo capacity is commendable for a compact SUV. Its rear cargo area provides enough space for daily essentials and weekend getaways.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV also offers a spacious cargo area, thanks to its boxy shape. The rear seats can be folded to create more space for larger items.


Interior Features:

Hyundai Kona Electric: The Hyundai Kona Electric comes with a range of interior features, including multi-zone climate control, a panoramic sunroof, and premium audio systems. It may also include advanced driver-assistance systems for enhanced safety.

Kia Soul EV: The Kia Soul EV is equipped with similar interior features, such as climate control, a panoramic sunroof, and premium audio systems. Like the Kona Electric, it also offers advanced safety technologies.
