Audi TT Base vs Roadster



Stock Motor

Audi's iconic TT series has captivated enthusiasts with its blend of style and performance. The Audi TT Base and TT Roadster models each offer their unique take on the driving experience, featuring distinct characteristics that cater to different preferences. In this article, we'll provide a detailed comparison of the stock motors of the Audi TT Base and Audi TT Roadster, covering key metrics such as 0-60 acceleration, torque, horsepower, top speed, quarter mile time, lap times, powertrain configuration, transmission type, power-to-weight ratio, and RPM (shaft speed).


Audi TT Base Stock Motor:

0-60 Acceleration: The Audi TT Base model accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in approximately 5.2 to 6.0 seconds, showcasing its respectable performance capabilities.

Torque: The TT Base generates torque ranging from approximately 207 lb-ft to 258 lb-ft, depending on the engine variant.

Horsepower: The TT Base boasts a horsepower output ranging from approximately 184 to 228, offering a balanced blend of power and efficiency.

Top Speed: The top speed of the Audi TT Base reaches around 130 to 155 mph, depending on the specific engine configuration.

Quarter Mile Time: The TT Base covers the quarter mile in approximately 13.8 to 15.3 seconds, reflecting its moderate performance characteristics.

Lap Times: The TT Base's lap times are influenced by its dynamic chassis and handling characteristics, delivering competitive performance on the track.

Powertrain Configuration: The Audi TT Base features a front-engine, front-wheel-drive (FWD) or quattro all-wheel-drive (AWD) layout, ensuring balanced traction and handling.


Audi TT Roadster Stock Motor:

0-60 Acceleration: The Audi TT Roadster accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in approximately 5.5 to 6.4 seconds, showcasing its open-top driving experience.

Torque: The TT Roadster generates torque ranging from approximately 207 lb-ft to 258 lb-ft, akin to its coupe counterpart.

Horsepower: The TT Roadster's horsepower output ranges from approximately 184 to 228, delivering similar power levels as the TT Base model.

Top Speed: The top speed of the Audi TT Roadster reaches around 130 to 155 mph, similar to the coupe variant.

Quarter Mile Time: The TT Roadster covers the quarter mile in approximately 14.0 to 15.5 seconds, reflecting its slightly slower acceleration due to its open-top design.

Lap Times: The TT Roadster's lap times may be influenced by its open-top construction, which could marginally affect aerodynamics and handling precision.

Powertrain Configuration: The Audi TT Roadster also features a front-engine layout with either FWD or quattro AWD, providing a balanced and enjoyable driving experience.


Transmission Type:

Both Models: The Audi TT Base and TT Roadster offer either a manual or automatic transmission, providing drivers with the choice between engaging gear changes and convenient automatic shifting.


Power-to-Weight Ratio:

Both Models: The power-to-weight ratio of the Audi TT Base and TT Roadster depends on the specific engine and configuration, contributing to their respective performance characteristics.


RPM (Shaft Speed):

Both Models: The RPM (shaft speed) of the Audi TT Base and TT Roadster's engines varies based on the engine variant, delivering peak torque and power within specific RPM ranges.


The Audi TT Base and TT Roadster models offer a range of performance characteristics while sharing similarities in their stock motors. The TT Base focuses on delivering a balanced blend of power and efficiency, making it suitable for daily driving and spirited jaunts. On the other hand, the TT Roadster enhances the driving experience with open-top enjoyment, providing a similar performance package with the added thrill of wind-in-your-hair motoring.





Tuning Ability

Audi TT Base Tuning Potential: The Audi TT Base, with its balanced performance and dynamic handling, presents a canvas for tuning enthusiasts to enhance its power and driving characteristics.


Specific Products for Audi TT Base:

Performance Intake System: Upgrading to a high-flow air intake system improves air intake, optimizing engine combustion for increased horsepower and torque.

Performance Exhaust System: A performance exhaust system with larger diameter pipes and free-flowing mufflers enhances exhaust flow, delivering a sportier sound and power gains.

ECU Remapping: Custom engine tuning through Electronic Control Unit (ECU) remapping can optimize fuel and ignition settings, unlocking hidden power potential.

Ignition Upgrades: High-performance ignition coils and spark plugs improve spark energy, enhancing combustion efficiency and power output.


Audi TT Roadster Tuning Potential:

The Audi TT Roadster, providing open-top driving excitement, offers tuning enthusiasts the opportunity to enhance its performance and driving dynamics.


Specific Products for Audi TT Roadster:

Cold Air Intake: Upgrading to a cold air intake system maximizes air supply to the engine, increasing power output and responsiveness.

Performance Exhaust System: A performance exhaust enhances exhaust gas flow, adding horsepower and torque while creating an invigorating sound.

Turbocharger Kit: Forced induction upgrades like a turbocharger kit can significantly boost power, transforming the TT Roadster into a more powerful machine.

Fuel Management System: Upgrading to a standalone fuel management system ensures precise fuel delivery for increased power and optimal engine performance.

Adjustable Suspension: Enhance handling by installing an adjustable suspension system that allows drivers to fine-tune ride height and damping characteristics.


Affordability and Accessibility:

Audi TT Base: Tuning options for the Audi TT Base may be more accessible and affordable compared to high-performance models, making it an ideal entry point for modification.

Audi TT Roadster: The TT Roadster offers a wide range of aftermarket products, given its popularity and demand, enabling enthusiasts to tailor its performance and appearance.

Sporty Dynamics: The Audi TT Base boasts a suspension system designed to deliver sporty and agile handling, aligning with its reputation for performance.

Struts and Multi-Links: The TT Base typically features MacPherson strut front suspension and a four-link rear suspension, providing a combination of responsive cornering and stability.


Audi TT Roadster Suspension:

Open-Top Driving Pleasure: The Audi TT Roadster's suspension is tuned to maintain the model's sporty character while accommodating the open-top driving experience.

Reinforcements: The TT Roadster's suspension may include additional reinforcements to compensate for the absence of a fixed roof, ensuring chassis rigidity and handling balance.


Driving Dynamics:

Audi TT Base: The TT Base's suspension focuses on delivering precise handling and responsive steering, creating an engaging and dynamic driving experience.

Audi TT Roadster: The TT Roadster's suspension maintains the TT's sporty demeanor while factoring in the open-top dynamics, offering an exhilarating driving experience.


Comfort vs. Sportiness:

Audi TT Base: The TT Base's suspension strikes a balance between ride comfort and sporty handling, catering to driving enthusiasts seeking an everyday sports coupe.

Audi TT Roadster: The TT Roadster's suspension maintains a similar balance but may offer slightly adjusted damping settings to account for the convertible's characteristics.


Adjustable Suspension (Optional):

Both Models: The Audi TT Base and TT Roadster may offer an optional adjustable suspension package that allows drivers to customize the ride characteristics based on their preferences.

Adaptation to Driving Conditions: Adjustable suspensions provide the flexibility to switch between comfort and sport modes, enhancing the overall driving experience.


Ground Clearance:

Both Models: The Audi TT Base and TT Roadster's suspensions ensure adequate ground clearance for urban driving while also providing a dynamic stance for spirited driving.


Weight Distribution:

Both Models: The weight distribution of the Audi TT Base and TT Roadster remains consistent, contributing to the models' stability and handling capabilities.


Ride Comfort:

Audi TT Base: The TT Base's suspension focuses on delivering a comfortable ride while preserving its sporty characteristics, making it suitable for everyday use.

Audi TT Roadster: The TT Roadster's suspension offers a comfortable ride while accounting for the convertible's body structure, maintaining a balanced driving experience.
