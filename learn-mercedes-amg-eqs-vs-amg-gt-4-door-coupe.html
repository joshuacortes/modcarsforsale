Mercedes-AMG EQS vs. Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe



Stock Motor

Mercedes-AMG is renowned for crafting high-performance vehicles that blend luxury, style, and impressive power. The Mercedes-AMG EQS and Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe are two exceptional examples of AMG's engineering prowess. In this article, we'll compare the stock motors of these two models, examining their 0-60 acceleration, torque, horsepower, top speed, quarter mile time, lap times, powertrain configuration, transmission type, power to weight ratio, and RPM (shaft speed).


0-60 Acceleration:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS, powered by its electric drivetrain, delivers astonishing acceleration, reaching 0-60 mph in approximately 3.8 seconds.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: Equipped with a potent internal combustion engine, the GT 4-Door Coupe boasts an impressive 0-60 mph acceleration time of around 3.1 seconds.


Torque:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS showcases the instantaneous torque delivery typical of electric vehicles, generating an awe-inspiring peak torque figure of over 560 lb-ft.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe produces an incredible peak torque of 590 lb-ft, accentuating its powerful performance capabilities.


Horsepower:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS combines its electric motors to produce an exceptional power output of around 580 horsepower, ensuring a thrilling driving experience.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe packs a formidable punch with an astounding power output of approximately 630 horsepower, delivering breathtaking performance on the road.


Top Speed:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS reaches an electronically-limited top speed of 130 mph, emphasizing its focus on luxury and electric driving refinement.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe offers a higher electronically-limited top speed of 195 mph, showcasing its sports car DNA and high-performance capabilities.


Quarter Mile Time:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS conquers the quarter mile in approximately 12.5 seconds, making it one of the fastest electric sedans in this segment.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe achieves an impressive quarter mile time of around 11.3 seconds, underlining its performance-oriented nature.


Lap Times:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: As a luxury-oriented electric vehicle, the EQS is not designed for track racing; thus, lap times are not a primary focus.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe is built for thrilling driving experiences on the track, showcasing impressive lap times with its agile handling and sports car dynamics.


Powertrain Configuration:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS boasts an all-electric powertrain configuration, utilizing electric motors to deliver instantaneous torque and smooth acceleration.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe features a traditional internal combustion engine setup, combining performance with the characteristic AMG exhaust note.


Transmission Type:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS utilizes a single-speed automatic transmission, efficiently channeling the electric motor's power to the wheels.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe is equipped with a responsive nine-speed automatic transmission, optimizing gear shifts for maximum performance.


Power-to-Weight Ratio:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS exhibits a commendable power-to-weight ratio, thanks to the instant torque delivery and lightweight construction of its electric drivetrain.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe's power-to-weight ratio is optimized to provide dynamic handling and exhilarating acceleration.


RPM (Shaft Speed):

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS's electric motors offer an incredibly wide RPM range, ensuring constant power delivery across different driving conditions.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe's high-revving internal combustion engine delivers power across a broad RPM spectrum, emphasizing its performance pedigree.





Suspension

Mercedes-AMG EQS Suspension:

Air Suspension: The EQS is equipped with a sophisticated air suspension system, allowing for adaptive and variable ride height adjustments. This feature enables the car to maintain a comfortable ride in various driving conditions.

Comfort Focus: The EQS suspension tuning emphasizes luxury and comfort, delivering a smooth and serene driving experience. It is ideal for long-distance cruising and providing passengers with a premium ride.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe Suspension:

Adaptive Damping System: The GT 4-Door Coupe features an advanced adaptive damping system that can adjust the suspension settings to match different driving modes. This system enhances handling and performance while maintaining ride comfort.

Sport Performance Focus: The GT 4-Door Coupe's suspension is designed to provide exceptional handling characteristics, catering to driving enthusiasts who seek agility and precise cornering abilities.


Ride Comfort:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS excels in ride comfort, thanks to its air suspension system that absorbs road imperfections and delivers a plush ride. It prioritizes the well-being of occupants with a luxurious and refined driving experience.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: While the GT 4-Door Coupe still offers a comfortable ride, it leans more towards a sporty feel. The suspension's focus on performance means it may be slightly stiffer than the EQS, providing a more engaging driving experience.


Handling and Cornering:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS handles competently, offering responsive steering and stability, making it well-suited for everyday driving and long journeys.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe shines in handling and cornering, with precise steering and enhanced agility that elevate its sports car persona. It delivers confidence-inspiring performance on twisty roads and track days.


Driving Modes:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS offers various driving modes, including comfort and sport settings, allowing drivers to adjust the suspension's characteristics to match their preferences.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe provides multiple driving modes, including sport and sport+ settings, which further enhance the suspension's firmness and responsiveness for spirited driving.


Weight Distribution:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS's weight distribution is optimized to ensure balanced handling, contributing to a stable and composed ride.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe's weight distribution is designed to emphasize its sporty character, enhancing agility and performance on the road.


Ground Clearance:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS has adjustable ride height capabilities due to its air suspension system, offering better ground clearance when needed, such as when navigating uneven terrains.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe has a lower ride height, providing a sportier stance and improved aerodynamics, suitable for performance-oriented driving.





Interior

Interior Design: Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS boasts a futuristic and elegant interior design, emphasizing modernity and sustainability. The cabin is adorned with premium materials, including sustainable leather upholstery and natural wood trims, creating a harmonious and eco-friendly ambiance.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe exudes a more sporty and performance-oriented vibe. The interior design features a driver-focused cockpit, complemented by exquisite Nappa leather upholstery and carbon fiber accents, symbolizing its high-performance DNA.


Infotainment and Connectivity:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS comes equipped with the latest MBUX Hyperscreen, a massive curved display that spans across the entire dashboard, seamlessly integrating the instrument cluster, infotainment system, and passenger displays. The MBUX Hyperscreen offers intuitive controls and supports voice-activated commands.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe features a more traditional dual-screen setup for infotainment and instrumentation. While not as expansive as the EQS's MBUX Hyperscreen, it still provides seamless smartphone integration and access to various vehicle settings.


Digital Instrument Cluster:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS features a stunning digital instrument cluster with high-definition graphics and customizable layouts, allowing drivers to personalize the displayed information.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe also offers a digital instrument cluster, providing essential driving information with a sporty and performance-oriented display.


Interior Space:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS offers a spacious and comfortable interior with ample legroom and headroom for all passengers. Its electric platform allows for a more open and uncluttered cabin space.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: Despite being a four-door coupe, the GT 4-Door Coupe provides a sporty and intimate ambiance, with slightly more limited rear-seat space compared to the EQS.


Materials and Finishes:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS is adorned with top-tier materials, featuring high-quality leather, sustainable elements, and premium soft-touch surfaces throughout the cabin.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe offers a blend of luxury and sportiness with premium Nappa leather seats and carbon fiber trim, accentuating its performance-oriented character.


Seating Comfort:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS's seats prioritize long-distance comfort, featuring multi-contour and heated options, making it perfect for luxurious and serene road trips.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe's seats are designed to provide excellent lateral support during spirited driving, delivering a balance of comfort and support.


Ambient Lighting:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS comes with customizable ambient lighting, allowing drivers to select from a wide range of colors to create a serene and captivating atmosphere.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe also offers ambient lighting, but with a focus on sportiness, illuminating the cabin with a more dynamic and dramatic flair.

Sound System:

Mercedes-AMG EQS: The EQS is equipped with a premium sound system, offering immersive audio quality, perfect for music enthusiasts and audiophiles.

Mercedes-AMG GT 4-Door Coupe: The GT 4-Door Coupe boasts a high-end Burmester sound system, providing concert-like audio clarity for all passengers to enjoy.
