Volkswagen ID.4 vs. Audi Q4 e-tron



Stock Motor

As electric vehicles continue to revolutionize the automotive industry, two prominent contenders from the Volkswagen Group, the Volkswagen ID.4 and the Audi Q4 e-tron, showcase the latest advancements in electric mobility. In this article, we will compare and contrast the stock motors of these electric SUVs, focusing on key performance metrics and technical specifications. Let's dive into the details and see how these EVs stack up against each other.


Performance Metrics:

0-60 mph (0-96.5 km/h):

Volkswagen ID.4: Approximately 7.5 seconds

Audi Q4 e-tron: Approximately 6.2 seconds


Torque:

Volkswagen ID.4: 229 lb-ft (310 Nm)

Audi Q4 e-tron: 229 lb-ft (310 Nm)


Horsepower:

Volkswagen ID.4: 201 hp (150 kW)

Audi Q4 e-tron: 201 hp (150 kW)


Top Speed:

Volkswagen ID.4: Approximately 99 mph (160 km/h)

Audi Q4 e-tron: Approximately 99 mph (160 km/h)


Quarter Mile Time:

Specific quarter-mile time figures are not readily available in the public domain. Both vehicles offer respectable acceleration, with the Audi Q4 e-tron having a slight edge.


Lap Times:

As lap times can vary significantly depending on the track, conditions, and driver, no official lap time comparisons are available for these vehicles. However, both the Volkswagen ID.4 and the Audi Q4 e-tron are geared more towards daily driving and practicality rather than track-focused performance.


Powertrain Configuration:

Volkswagen ID.4:

The ID.4 features a rear-wheel-drive setup with a single electric motor mounted on the rear axle. This configuration provides a balanced weight distribution and efficient power delivery.


Audi Q4 e-tron:

The Q4 e-tron is equipped with an all-wheel-drive system, featuring two electric motors - one on the front axle and another on the rear axle. This configuration offers enhanced traction and stability in various driving conditions.


Transmission Type:

Both the Volkswagen ID.4 and the Audi Q4 e-tron come with a single-speed direct-drive transmission. This simplicity ensures smooth and seamless acceleration without the need for gear changes.

Power-to-Weight Ratio:

Volkswagen ID.4: Approximately 10.2 lb/hp

Audi Q4 e-tron: Approximately 10.5 lb/hp

RPM (Shaft Speed):

Specific RPM figures for the stock motors of the Volkswagen ID.4 and the Audi Q4 e-tron are not readily available in the public domain. However, like most electric vehicles, their electric motors can deliver power and torque instantly across a wide range of RPMs, ensuring responsive and smooth acceleration.


Both the Volkswagen ID.4 and the Audi Q4 e-tron offer impressive performance and cutting-edge technology, showcasing the capabilities of electric mobility from the Volkswagen Group. While the Audi Q4 e-tron exhibits slightly quicker acceleration and comes with an all-wheel-drive system for added traction, the Volkswagen ID.4 excels in practicality and efficiency with its rear-wheel-drive configuration.


Ultimately, the choice between these electric SUVs depends on individual preferences. If you prioritize all-wheel-drive capability and sportier acceleration, the Audi Q4 e-tron might be the preferred choice. On the other hand, if you seek a more straightforward and efficient daily driver, the Volkswagen ID.4 offers a compelling package with a focus on practicality and comfort. Regardless of your choice, both vehicles represent a step forward in the world of electric mobility, contributing to a more sustainable and eco-friendly automotive future.





Suspension

Suspension Design:


Volkswagen ID.4:

The ID.4 is equipped with a front MacPherson strut suspension and a rear multi-link suspension setup.

The MacPherson strut is a tried and tested design known for its simplicity and cost-effectiveness.

The multi-link rear suspension offers improved handling and stability during cornering.


Audi Q4 e-tron:

The Q4 e-tron features a front MacPherson strut suspension and a rear multi-link suspension, similar to the ID.4.

Audi's suspension tuning may differ, offering a unique feel to the ride and handling.

Ride Comfort:


Volkswagen ID.4:

The ID.4's suspension is designed with a focus on ride comfort, ensuring a smooth and cushioned experience on various road surfaces.

The MacPherson strut and multi-link setup work together to absorb road imperfections and reduce vibrations in the cabin.


Audi Q4 e-tron:

Audi's suspension tuning tends to emphasize a balance between comfort and sportiness.

The Q4 e-tron's suspension may offer a slightly firmer ride, catering to drivers who enjoy a more engaging driving experience.


Handling and Stability:

Volkswagen ID.4:


The ID.4's suspension is optimized for stable and predictable handling, making it ideal for everyday driving and family-oriented use.

The multi-link rear suspension enhances cornering stability, allowing the ID.4 to maintain composure during turns.


Audi Q4 e-tron:

Audi's suspension tuning typically prioritizes precise handling and responsiveness.

The Q4 e-tron's suspension may deliver a more agile and sporty feel, especially during spirited driving.


Adjustability and Driving Modes:

Volkswagen ID.4:


The ID.4 may offer adjustable shock absorbers as an option, allowing drivers to fine-tune the suspension characteristics to their preferences.


Various driving modes can also influence the suspension's responsiveness and ride quality.

Audi Q4 e-tron:

Audi's suspension setup may come with adaptive damping or air suspension as an option, further enhancing the vehicle's ride comfort and handling capabilities.

Like the ID.4, the Q4 e-tron also features different driving modes that can tailor the suspension settings to suit various driving scenarios.

Ground Clearance:


Volkswagen ID.4:

The ID.4 boasts sufficient ground clearance, making it suitable for light off-road excursions and tackling uneven terrains with ease. Audi Q4 e-tron:

Audi's suspension setup is designed to provide ample ground clearance, ensuring the Q4 e-tron is equally capable on rough surfaces and challenging road conditions.





Interior

Interior Design and Aesthetics:


Volkswagen ID.4:

The ID.4 features a clean and contemporary interior design, exuding a sense of modernity and simplicity.

The cabin is characterized by well-organized controls and a user-friendly layout, emphasizing practicality and ease of use.


Audi Q4 e-tron:

The Q4 e-tron boasts a luxurious and premium interior design, setting it apart from the ID.4.

Audi's attention to detail and use of upscale materials create a more opulent and refined ambiance inside the cabin.


Materials and Finishes:



Volkswagen ID.4:

The ID.4 offers high-quality materials, but it focuses more on functionality and durability.

Expect to find comfortable cloth or synthetic leather seats and soft-touch plastics throughout the cabin.


Audi Q4 e-tron:

Audi elevates the interior with top-notch materials, including genuine leather seats and premium trim accents.

The Q4 e-tron's interior feels more luxurious and sophisticated, appealing to those seeking a higher level of opulence.

Infotainment and Technology:


Volkswagen ID.4:

The ID.4 comes equipped with a user-friendly infotainment system, featuring a large touchscreen display.

It offers smartphone integration, including Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, for seamless connectivity.

Audi Q4 e-tron:

Audi's renowned MMI infotainment system takes center stage in the Q4 e-tron, offering a more sophisticated user interface.

The Q4 e-tron also incorporates the latest tech features, including Audi Virtual Cockpit and advanced driver-assistance systems.

Space and Practicality:


Volkswagen ID.4:

The ID.4 boasts a spacious and practical interior, accommodating passengers and cargo with ease.

It features a comfortable seating arrangement and ample headroom and legroom for both front and rear passengers.


Audi Q4 e-tron:

The Q4 e-tron also provides generous space for occupants, ensuring a comfortable and relaxing journey.

It may offer additional premium touches, such as adjustable seats with massage functions, for a more luxurious experience.

Unique Features:


Volkswagen ID.4:


The ID.4 emphasizes a user-centric approach, focusing on functionality and everyday practicality.

It may offer innovative storage solutions and thoughtful features to enhance the driving experience.


Audi Q4 e-tron:

Audi's Q4 e-tron places a greater emphasis on luxury and driver-oriented features.

Expect to find unique Audi design elements and upscale amenities, catering to those who seek a premium EV experience.
