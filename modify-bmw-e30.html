BMW E30



BMW 3 Series (E30)

The first 3 Series to be produced in 4-door sedan and wagon/estate body types, the model range featured 2-door coupe and convertible body variants[4][5] as well. It was propelled by four-, six-, and six-cylinder diesel engines, the latter of which was a first for the 3 Series. The first BMW with all-wheel drive was the E30 325iX.


Body Styles

The E30 eventually became available as a four-door sedan and five-door station wagon in addition to the two-door sedan and Baur convertible body types of its E21 forebears (marketed as "Touring").

Drivetrain

For the various E30 variants, a total of six transmissions—four manual and two automatic—were offered. Rear-wheel drive or permanent all-wheel drive were available for the E30 (325iX saloons and estaes only).



Modified BMW E30

Performance Enhancements: Unleashing the Beast

One of the primary motivations for modifying an E30 is to enhance its performance. The E30 comes with a range of engines, from the peppy 1.8-liter 4-cylinder to the thrilling 2.3-liter inline-six in the M3 model. With aftermarket parts and engineering prowess, the E30's power can be significantly upgraded. Popular modifications include ECU tuning, turbocharging, and exhaust system upgrades. These enhancements can transform the E30 from a respectable performer into a powerhouse, providing thrilling acceleration and precise handling.


Aftermarket Support: A Treasure Trove of Options

The E30 has a rich aftermarket ecosystem, with countless suppliers offering performance parts, body kits, and interior upgrades. You can choose from various suspension kits, brakes, wheels, and even modern engine swaps to create a personalized masterpiece. For enthusiasts, this extensive support ensures that your E30 project can cater to your specific desires, whether you prioritize performance, aesthetics, or a combination of both.

Design: Classic Beauty with a Modern Twist

The E30's timeless design has made it an icon in the automotive world. Modifying the exterior can range from subtle changes like lowering the suspension and fitting a tasteful set of wheels to more radical transformations with body kits and custom paintwork. Many E30 enthusiasts opt for the "retro-mod" approach, blending classic style with modern touches like LED lighting or contemporary wheel designs. The result is a striking balance between vintage charm and contemporary aesthetics.


Motorsports Success: A Racing Legacy

The BMW E30's racing pedigree is another compelling reason to modify it. The E30 M3, in particular, dominated touring car racing during the late '80s and early '90s. The boxy yet aerodynamic shape, combined with a high-revving engine, made it a fierce competitor on the track. Enthusiasts who modify their E30s often draw inspiration from this racing history, fitting performance upgrades that mirror the spirit of the legendary E30 M3.

Driving Experience: Nostalgia Meets Thrills

Ultimately, what sets the modified E30 apart is the driving experience. This car offers a pure and connected feel that's hard to replicate in modern vehicles. The precise steering, responsive chassis, and communicative feedback through the driver's seat create an engaging experience. While modifying an E30 can enhance its performance, it's essential to preserve the car's core driving characteristics that make it so beloved among enthusiasts.


Modifying a BMW E30 is not merely an exercise in automotive customization; it's a passionate pursuit of excellence in performance, aesthetics, and driving enjoyment. Whether you seek to pay homage to its racing heritage, build a jaw-dropping show car, or create the ultimate driving machine, the E30 provides an incredible platform for your automotive dreams. Embracing the past while looking towards the future, modifying an E30 is a journey filled with satisfaction, and it allows you to take part in a rich automotive tradition that shows no signs of fading away.

BMW E30 Suspension

H&R Sport Springs and Bilstein B8 Shocks

For those who want a well-rounded upgrade that enhances both performance and ride comfort, the H&R Sport Springs paired with Bilstein B8 Shocks are a winning combination. H&R Sport Springs offer a slight drop in ride height, improving handling without sacrificing ride quality. Bilstein B8 Shocks provide exceptional damping, making them a perfect match for the E30's chassis. This setup will deliver a noticeable improvement in both cornering stability and daily driving comfort.


KW Variant 3 Coilovers

If you desire a more customizable suspension setup, the KW Variant 3 Coilovers are an excellent choice. These high-quality coilovers offer adjustable ride height, rebound, and compression damping. This allows you to fine-tune the suspension to your preferences and driving style. Whether you want a softer, more comfortable ride for daily driving or a stiff setup for spirited track days, the KW Variant 3 can accommodate it all.


Eibach Pro-System Suspension Kit

The Eibach Pro-System Suspension Kit provides an easy and comprehensive upgrade for the BMW E30's suspension. It includes performance springs and dampers, which are perfectly matched to provide improved handling and ride quality. This kit is an ideal option for those who want a straightforward upgrade without the need for extensive adjustments or tuning.


Ground Control Coilover Conversion Kit

For the ultimate in adjustability and performance, the Ground Control Coilover Conversion Kit is the way to go. This kit allows you to convert your E30's existing struts into a full coilover setup. With adjustable ride height, spring rates, and damping, you can tailor the suspension to your precise requirements. This kit is perfect for enthusiasts who love to fine-tune their vehicles for specific applications, such as track racing or autocross.


Polyurethane Bushings

Don't forget the small but crucial components of your suspension system – the bushings. Upgrading to polyurethane bushings can significantly improve the E30's handling characteristics. These bushings are stiffer than the factory rubber ones, reducing play in the suspension components. This results in sharper steering response and better overall handling.


Wheel and Tire Upgrades

When upgrading your suspension, don't overlook the impact of your wheel and tire choices. Wider, stickier tires can greatly enhance grip and handling, while lightweight alloy wheels can reduce unsprung weight and improve acceleration and braking. Make sure your tire and wheel choice complements your chosen suspension setup.


Choosing the best suspension upgrades for your BMW E30 depends on your specific driving preferences and needs. Whether you're looking for improved comfort during daily commutes or seeking a more aggressive setup for track days, there are options to suit your desires. The key is to strike the perfect balance between ride comfort and performance, taking into account the type of driving you'll do most often. Be sure to consult with experienced professionals or fellow E30 enthusiasts for advice before making your final decision, as they can provide valuable insights based on their own experiences. With the right suspension upgrades, your BMW E30 can become an even more thrilling and capable driving machine.




BMW E30 Brakes

Brembo GT Series Brake Kit

When it comes to upgrading your BMW E30's brakes, the Brembo GT Series Brake Kit is a top choice. Brembo is renowned for its high-performance braking systems, and this kit is no exception. It includes larger, slotted or drilled rotors and high-quality Brembo calipers. This upgrade significantly improves heat dissipation and reduces brake fade, making it an excellent choice for spirited driving or track use.


StopTech Sport Brake Kit

The StopTech Sport Brake Kit is another compelling option for E30 owners who crave exceptional stopping power. The kit includes slotted or drilled rotors and high-performance pads that can withstand the rigors of spirited driving and demanding track conditions. StopTech's reputation for delivering top-notch brake performance makes this kit a worthwhile investment.


Wilwood Superlite 6 Big Brake Kit

If you're seeking an upgrade that not only improves braking performance but also adds a bold visual statement to your E30, the Wilwood Superlite 6 Big Brake Kit is an excellent choice. This kit includes larger, six-piston calipers, and slotted or drilled rotors, offering substantial stopping power and improved pedal feel. Plus, the eye-catching calipers can add a touch of style to your car's appearance.


EBC Brakes Yellowstuff Pads

For those who want to enhance their E30's braking performance without a complete overhaul of the system, EBC Brakes Yellowstuff Pads are a cost-effective option. These high-performance brake pads offer excellent stopping power and reduced brake fade. They are ideal for aggressive street driving and occasional track days, and they pair well with OEM or upgraded rotors.


Stainless Steel Brake Lines

While upgrading your brake components is essential, don't overlook the benefits of replacing your E30's stock rubber brake lines with stainless steel brake lines. These lines provide a more consistent and firmer pedal feel by reducing brake line expansion. This upgrade is cost-effective and complements other brake enhancements, ensuring that the entire braking system works seamlessly.


Performance Brake Fluid

To complete your brake upgrade, consider using a high-performance brake fluid. Brands like Motul, Castrol, and ATE offer brake fluids with higher boiling points, which reduce the risk of brake fluid boiling and maintain consistent brake pedal feel, especially during aggressive driving.


Upgrading the brakes on your BMW E30 can significantly enhance your driving experience, whether you're on the open road or at the track. Each of the brake upgrades mentioned in this article offers various levels of performance, allowing you to choose the one that best suits your driving style and budget. Keep in mind that the best brake upgrade may vary depending on your specific needs, so it's essential to consult with experienced professionals or fellow E30 enthusiasts who can provide valuable insights based on their experiences. Whichever brake upgrade you choose, it's certain to elevate your E30's stopping power and provide a safer and more enjoyable driving experience.
