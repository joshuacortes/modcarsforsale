Toyota MR2

Three generations of the Toyota MR2 line of two-seat, mid-engined, rear-wheel-drive sports cars were built and sold between 1984 and 2007: (1984–1989), (1989–1999), and (2007). (2000–2007). It is the first Japanese rear mid-engined production car.


Modified Toyota MR2

Modifying a toyota mr2, building a showcar/project drift car, racecar or anything in between? -Be aware of the aftermarket ability and overall support available for your mr2 build.

Although the Toyota MR2 had always enjoyed a devoted fan base, its popularity in the aftermarket modification scene experienced fluctuations over the years. In recent times, however, there has been a noticeable resurgence in the interest of customizing these iconic sports cars:

Classic Appeal: As the first and second-generation MR2s gain vintage status, they attract a new generation of enthusiasts eager to embrace the retro charm and timeless design. This has led to a spike in demand for aftermarket parts and accessories that help restore and improve these classic vehicles.

Affordability and Accessibility: Compared to other sports cars and exotics, the MR2 provides a more accessible entry point for individuals seeking a thrilling driving experience without breaking the bank. The relatively affordable base price and a wide availability of used MR2s make it an attractive option for those seeking to embark on a customization journey.

Thriving Online Communities: The rise of social media and online forums has facilitated the creation of vibrant MR2 enthusiast communities. These digital hubs provide a platform for owners to share their experiences, showcase their modified MR2s, and seek advice from fellow enthusiasts. The sense of camaraderie and support within these communities has contributed to the growing popularity of MR2 customization.

Common Toyota MR2 Modifications:

One of the most popular modifications for the Toyota MR2 is the addition of a turbocharger. This can significantly increase the car's horsepower and torque, allowing it to accelerate quickly and reach higher top speeds. Other popular modifications include aftermarket suspension, brakes, and exhaust systems, which can improve the car's handling and overall performance.

In addition to performance upgrades, many car enthusiasts choose to modify the exterior of the Toyota MR2 to make it stand out from the crowd. This can include body kits, custom paint jobs, and aftermarket wheels. Interior modifications, such as racing seats and upgraded audio systems, are also common among MR2 owners.

The Toyota MR2 is a favorite among the tuning community due to its lightweight design, mid-engine layout, and potential for high performance. With the right modifications, the MR2 can easily compete with other high-performance sports cars on the road.




Toyota MR2 Turbocharger

A turbocharger can significantly increase the car's horsepower and torque, providing quick acceleration and higher top speeds. Best Turbochargers to modify the Toyota MR2 include:


HKS GTII Series Turbochargers

Renowned for their high-quality performance products, HKS offers a range of GTII series turbochargers that are a popular choice among MR2 enthusiasts. The GTII turbos are engineered to deliver excellent response, reliability, and power gains throughout the rev range. With various sizes available, enthusiasts can choose a turbocharger that matches their desired power level, whether it's for mild street performance or full-blown track use.


Garrett GTX Series Turbochargers

Garrett is a leading name in the turbocharger industry, and their GTX series offers a variety of options for the Toyota MR2. These turbochargers incorporate advanced aerodynamics and cutting-edge materials, resulting in improved efficiency and durability. The GTX series provides a wide range of sizes and configurations, making it possible to select a turbo that precisely suits the MR2's engine and intended performance goals.


BorgWarner EFR Series Turbochargers

BorgWarner's EFR (Engineered For Racing) series has gained popularity among MR2 enthusiasts seeking a balance of performance and responsiveness. These turbochargers utilize advanced technologies, such as low-inertia Gamma-Ti turbine wheels and dual ceramic ball-bearing cartridges, to reduce lag and improve overall performance. The EFR series offers various sizes, allowing for customization based on specific engine setups and horsepower targets.


Precision Turbo & Engine PT Series

Precision Turbo & Engine (PTE) is another reputable brand known for its high-performance turbochargers. The PT series offers a diverse range of turbochargers suitable for the MR2, from smaller units for quick spool and street use to larger options for maximum horsepower output on the track. PTE's turbochargers are known for their robust construction and reliability, making them a popular choice for MR2 enthusiasts looking for a long-lasting performance upgrade.


GReddy T518Z Turbo Kit

For MR2 owners seeking a comprehensive turbo upgrade solution, the GReddy T518Z Turbo Kit provides a well-engineered package. This kit includes a T518Z turbocharger, exhaust manifold, downpipe, intercooler, and all necessary hardware for installation. GReddy's extensive experience in aftermarket performance parts ensures a reliable and efficient upgrade for the MR2, with power gains tailored to suit various driving applications.




Toyota MR2 Suspension

Upgraded suspension can improve the car's handling and stability, allowing it to take corners more easily. Best suspenion options to modify the Toyota MR2 include:


Tein Flex Z Coilover Kit

Tein is renowned for producing high-quality suspension components, and the Flex Z Coilover Kit is an excellent choice for MR2 owners. This kit provides a perfect balance between street comfort and performance, making it ideal for daily driving and occasional track use. With 16 levels of damping force adjustment, you can fine-tune the suspension to suit various driving conditions and personal preferences. The inclusion of full-length ride height adjustment allows for easy customization of the car's stance, giving it a sporty and aggressive look.


KW Variant 3 Coilover Kit

For MR2 enthusiasts seeking uncompromising performance and adjustability, the KW Variant 3 Coilover Kit is a top-tier option. This high-end kit features independently adjustable compression and rebound damping, allowing you to fine-tune the suspension to suit any driving situation. With its stainless steel construction and corrosion-resistant components, the KW Variant 3 offers durability and longevity. Whether you're pushing your MR2 on the track or navigating daily commutes, this coilover kit ensures an exceptional balance of comfort and handling.


Whiteline Adjustable Sway Bars

To enhance the MR2's cornering capabilities and minimize body roll, upgrading to Whiteline Adjustable Sway Bars is an effective choice. These sway bars come in various thicknesses and are adjustable, allowing you to tailor the handling characteristics to your liking. Whiteline's high-quality materials and engineering ensure improved stability and responsiveness during spirited driving. With these sway bars, you can confidently tackle tight corners and enjoy a more engaging driving experience.


Cusco Strut Tower Bar

Reinforcing the front suspension mounting points with a Cusco Strut Tower Bar significantly improves chassis rigidity and enhances steering response. This easy-to-install bar reduces flex during aggressive cornering, contributing to a more precise and confident driving feel. Cusco's Strut Tower Bar is made from durable materials, ensuring its ability to handle the demands of spirited driving and performance applications.


Energy Suspension Hyper-Flex Master Bushing Kit

Replacing the factory rubber bushings with Energy Suspension's Hyper-Flex Master Bushing Kit is a cost-effective way to improve suspension responsiveness and reduce unwanted movement. This kit includes a comprehensive set of polyurethane bushings for various suspension components, ensuring a complete upgrade for enhanced handling and longevity. By reducing deflection and improving alignment, these bushings contribute to a more stable and predictable driving experience.


Megan Racing Adjustable Rear Toe Control Arms

To fine-tune the MR2's rear wheel alignment for better handling and tire contact patch, consider the Megan Racing Adjustable Rear Toe Control Arms. These control arms allow for precise adjustments of toe settings, which can be particularly beneficial for track enthusiasts seeking optimal handling characteristics. Megan Racing's durable construction ensures longevity and consistent performance under demanding conditions.



Toyota MR2 Brakes

Upgraded brakes can improve the car's stopping power and reduce brake fade during hard driving.


Brembo GT Brake Kit

Brembo is a renowned name in the performance braking industry, and their GT Brake Kit is an exceptional option for MR2 owners looking for top-tier stopping power. The Brembo GT Kit includes high-performance calipers, slotted or drilled rotors, performance brake pads, stainless steel lines, and all necessary hardware. With their superior heat dissipation properties, Brembo brakes offer consistent performance during aggressive driving and spirited track sessions.


StopTech Big Brake Kit

The StopTech Big Brake Kit is another excellent option for MR2 owners seeking to upgrade their braking performance. This kit features oversized calipers, high-quality rotors, and performance brake pads, providing significant improvements in stopping power and fade resistance. The StopTech kit is available in different configurations, allowing enthusiasts to choose the setup that best suits their intended driving style.


Wilwood Superlite Brake Kit

Wilwood's Superlite Brake Kit offers a combination of high-performance components designed to deliver excellent braking performance. These kits include lightweight, forged calipers, large diameter rotors, and high-friction brake pads. The Superlite Brake Kit reduces unsprung weight, resulting in improved handling and overall driving dynamics. Wilwood's reputation for quality ensures that this brake kit can withstand the demands of aggressive driving and track use.


EBC Brakes Yellowstuff Brake Pads

For MR2 owners seeking a cost-effective brake upgrade, EBC Brakes' Yellowstuff Brake Pads are a popular choice. These high-friction brake pads provide improved stopping power and are well-suited for spirited street driving and occasional track use. They offer a good balance between performance and everyday drivability, making them an excellent option for enthusiasts on a budget.




Stainless Steel Brake Lines

Upgrading to stainless steel brake lines is a simple yet effective modification that can enhance brake pedal feel and response. Unlike stock rubber lines, stainless steel lines do not expand under pressure, resulting in a firmer brake pedal and improved brake modulation. Brands like Goodridge and StopTech offer high-quality stainless steel brake line kits specifically designed for the Toyota MR2.



Toyota MR2 Exhaust

An aftermarket exhaust system can improve the car's performance and provide a more aggressive sound.


HKS Hi-Power Exhaust System

As a well-respected name in the automotive aftermarket, HKS offers the Hi-Power Exhaust System, an excellent choice for MR2 enthusiasts seeking a performance-oriented upgrade. The Hi-Power exhaust is constructed from high-quality materials such as stainless steel and features mandrel-bent piping to maximize exhaust flow. This results in improved engine breathing, better power delivery, and a noticeable increase in horsepower and torque. Additionally, the HKS Hi-Power exhaust produces a deep, aggressive tone that complements the MR2's sporty character.


Invidia N1 Exhaust System

The Invidia N1 Exhaust System is a popular option for MR2 owners aiming to achieve a perfect balance between performance and sound. This exhaust system features large-diameter piping and a straight-through muffler design to enhance exhaust flow, contributing to improved horsepower and throttle response. The Invidia N1 exhaust also offers a sporty and aggressive exhaust note, providing a thrilling soundtrack to accompany spirited driving.


Borla Cat-Back Exhaust System

Known for its high-quality craftsmanship, Borla's Cat-Back Exhaust System offers MR2 enthusiasts an exhaust upgrade that enhances performance and reduces weight. Borla utilizes premium stainless steel construction, ensuring durability and resistance to corrosion. The Cat-Back system improves exhaust flow, helping the MR2 breathe better and unleashing hidden power potential. Additionally, Borla's exhaust produces a deep, throaty sound that perfectly complements the MR2's sporty character.


Apexi N1 Evolution-R Exhaust System

For MR2 owners seeking a performance exhaust with a unique and aggressive look, the Apexi N1 Evolution-R Exhaust System is an attractive option. This exhaust features a single-exit design with a stylish burnt titanium tip that adds a touch of flair to the MR2's rear end. The N1 Evolution-R exhaust offers improved performance, reduced weight, and an attention-grabbing exhaust note that's sure to turn heads.


Megan Racing Cat-Back Exhaust System

The Megan Racing Cat-Back Exhaust System is a budget-friendly option that doesn't compromise on performance or sound. With its large-diameter piping and free-flowing design, this exhaust system improves exhaust flow and helps the MR2 achieve a noticeable increase in horsepower and torque. The Megan Racing exhaust also produces a deep, aggressive tone, adding excitement to every drive.

With the TRD Widebody Kit, your MR2 will sport a race-inspired look that's both tasteful and purposeful.


Rocket Bunny Pandem Widebody Kit

VIS Racing Magnum Body Kit

Toyota MR2 Wheels

Upgraded wheels can improve the car's performance, handling, and provide a more stylish appearance.


Aggressive Fitment with Wide Wheels

One popular option for modifying the fitment of your Toyota MR2 is to go with aggressive wheel sizes and widths. By choosing wider wheels, you can accommodate wider tires, which improve traction and cornering performance. An aggressive fitment with wide wheels also fills out the wheel arches, giving the MR2 a more assertive and sporty stance.


Example:

Wheel Model: Volk Racing TE37

Wheel Size: 18x9.5 inches (Front), 18x10.5 inches (Rear)

Offset: +22mm (Front), +15mm (Rear)

Tire Size: 245/40R18 (Front), 275/35R18 (Rear)



Flush Fitment for a Clean and Sleek Look

A flush fitment aims to align the wheel's outer lip with the fender's edge, creating a clean and sleek appearance. This setup enhances the MR2's aesthetics while ensuring the tires remain within the fender lines for a neat and well-proportioned look.


Example:

Wheel Model: Enkei RPF1

Wheel Size: 17x8 inches

Offset: +35mm

Tire Size: 225/45R17



Staggered Fitment for Improved Handling

A staggered fitment involves using different wheel widths and tire sizes on the front and rear axles. This setup prioritizes handling and balance, providing increased grip on the rear wheels for improved traction during acceleration and cornering.


Example:

Front Wheel Model: Work Emotion CR Kiwami

Front Wheel Size: 18x8 inches

Front Offset: +40mm

Front Tire Size: 215/40R18

Rear Wheel Model: Work Emotion CR Kiwami

Rear Wheel Size: 18x9.5 inches

Rear Offset: +38mm

Rear Tire Size: 245/35R18



Air Suspension for Adjustable Stance

If you desire the flexibility to adjust your MR2's stance at will, an air suspension system is an excellent choice. Air suspension allows you to raise or lower the car's height according to your preference, offering the versatility to switch between comfortable daily driving and an aggressive show stance.


Example:

Air Suspension Kit: Air Lift Performance 3H

Air Management: Viair 444C Compressor with 5-gallon tank



Coilover Suspension for Performance and Customization

A coilover suspension system is ideal for those seeking enhanced handling performance and the ability to customize their MR2's ride height and damping settings. Coilovers provide precise control over the car's suspension, allowing you to fine-tune the stance and optimize handling characteristics for various driving scenarios.


Example:

Coilover Kit: KW Variant 3

Adjustability: Independently adjustable compression and rebound damping

Lowering Range: Adjustable from mild to aggressive lowering
