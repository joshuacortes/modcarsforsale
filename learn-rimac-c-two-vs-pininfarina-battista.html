Rimac C_Two vs. Pininfarina Battista



Stock Motor

Rimac C_Two:

0-60 Acceleration: The Rimac C_Two achieves an astonishing 0-60 mph time of around 1.85 seconds, making it one of the quickest-accelerating production cars in the world.

Torque: The C_Two generates a jaw-dropping 1,696 lb-ft (2,300 Nm) of torque, providing instantaneous acceleration and exceptional off-the-line performance.

Horsepower: The electric powertrain of the C_Two produces an astounding 1,888 horsepower, delivering mind-numbing speed and acceleration.

Top Speed: The C_Two has an electronically limited top speed of 258 mph (412 km/h), placing it among the fastest production cars in existence.

Quarter Mile Time: The quarter mile time of the C_Two is estimated to be around 9.1 seconds, showcasing its incredible straight-line performance.

Lap Times: Official lap times for the C_Two are not yet available, but its advanced technology and exceptional performance capabilities suggest it will be a formidable contender on the track.

Powertrain Configuration: The C_Two utilizes a sophisticated all-electric powertrain with four individual electric motors, one for each wheel, offering precise torque vectoring and exceptional handling.

Transmission Type: The C_Two features a single-speed gearbox for seamless power delivery and optimal acceleration.

Power-to-Weight Ratio: With a curb weight of approximately 4,300 pounds (1,950 kg), the C_Two achieves a remarkable power-to-weight ratio, allowing for blistering performance.

RPM (Shaft Speed): Electric motors do not have a traditional RPM (shaft speed) in the same way as internal combustion engines, as their power delivery is instant and linear.


Pininfarina Battista:

0-60 Acceleration: The Pininfarina Battista delivers an incredible 0-60 mph time of around 2 seconds, showcasing its exceptional acceleration capabilities.

Torque: The Battista generates a mind-boggling 1,696 lb-ft (2,300 Nm) of torque, offering instantaneous power delivery and electrifying performance.

Horsepower: The electric powertrain of the Battista produces an astonishing 1,877 horsepower, providing exhilarating speed and performance.

Top Speed: The Battista has an electronically limited top speed of 217 mph (350 km/h), making it one of the fastest electric production cars.

Quarter Mile Time: The quarter mile time of the Battista is estimated to be around 9 seconds, reflecting its exceptional acceleration.

Lap Times: Official lap times for the Battista are not yet available, but its cutting-edge technology and performance capabilities suggest it will be a formidable track performer.

Powertrain Configuration: The Battista features a sophisticated all-electric powertrain with four electric motors, one at each wheel, delivering precise torque vectoring and exceptional handling.

Transmission Type: The Battista utilizes a single-speed gearbox for seamless power delivery and optimal acceleration.

Power-to-Weight Ratio: With a curb weight of approximately 4,400 pounds (2,000 kg), the Battista achieves an impressive power-to-weight ratio, contributing to its outstanding performance.

RPM (Shaft Speed): As with the C_Two, the Battista's electric powertrain does not have a traditional RPM (shaft speed) due to its instant and linear power delivery.





Suspension

Rimac C_Two:

Double-Wishbone Suspension: The C_Two is equipped with a sophisticated double-wishbone suspension setup, known for its precise control and handling capabilities.

Active Suspension: The C_Two features an advanced active suspension system that adjusts the damping characteristics in real-time, providing optimal handling and ride comfort.

Adaptive Dampers: The C_Two's suspension incorporates adaptive dampers, which automatically adjust the suspension stiffness based on road conditions and driving dynamics.

Air Suspension: The C_Two is equipped with air suspension that allows the ride height to be adjusted, offering customization for different driving scenarios and preferences.

Carbon Fiber Components: The C_Two utilizes lightweight carbon fiber components in its suspension system, optimizing weight reduction without compromising rigidity.


Pininfarina Battista:

Double-Wishbone Suspension: Similar to the C_Two, the Battista also features a double-wishbone suspension system, offering precise control and adjustability.

Active Suspension: The Battista incorporates an advanced active suspension system that continuously adjusts the damping characteristics for optimal handling and ride comfort.

Adaptive Dampers: The Battista's suspension is equipped with adaptive dampers that automatically adjust suspension stiffness, enhancing handling and comfort.

Air Suspension: The Battista also features air suspension, allowing for adjustable ride height to suit different driving conditions and preferences.

Aluminum Components: The Battista utilizes aluminum components in its suspension system, balancing weight reduction and rigidity for improved performance.


Differences:

Suspension Tuning: While both the C_Two and the Battista utilize double-wishbone suspension and adaptive dampers, their suspension tuning may vary. The C_Two may prioritize a more track-focused performance, while the Battista may offer a balance between performance and comfort for everyday use.

Material Choices: The C_Two incorporates lightweight carbon fiber components in its suspension system, emphasizing weight reduction and performance. The Battista, on the other hand, utilizes aluminum components, balancing weight reduction with structural rigidity.

Ride Height Adjustment: Both vehicles offer adjustable ride height through their air suspension systems, allowing drivers to customize the ride height based on driving conditions. This feature provides flexibility and adaptability for different scenarios.

Driving Characteristics: The specific suspension tuning and characteristics of each vehicle contribute to their overall driving experience. The C_Two's suspension may provide a more focused and engaging driving experience, while the Battista's suspension may offer a balance between comfort and performance.





Interior

Rimac C_Two:

Futuristic Design: The C_Two's interior showcases a futuristic and minimalist design, reflecting its high-performance and technologically advanced nature.

Driver-Centric Cockpit: The C_Two's cockpit is designed with a strong emphasis on the driver, placing controls and information within easy reach.

Advanced Displays: The C_Two incorporates advanced digital displays for instrument clusters, infotainment systems, and other vital information.

High-Tech Controls: The interior features a range of touchscreens, touch-sensitive surfaces, and advanced controls for various functions.

Lightweight Materials: The C_Two utilizes lightweight materials such as carbon fiber and Alcantara, reducing weight and enhancing performance.

Racing-Inspired Seats: The C_Two offers supportive and sculpted seats, providing excellent comfort and stability during high-performance driving.

Limited Storage Space: Due to its focus on performance, the C_Two's interior provides limited storage compartments and cargo space.


Pininfarina Battista:

Luxurious Ambiance: The Battista's interior exudes a sense of luxury, combining high-quality materials, craftsmanship, and attention to detail.

Handcrafted Finishes: Pininfarina's renowned craftsmanship is evident in the Battista's interior, featuring premium leather upholstery, aluminum accents, and bespoke touches.

Driver-Focused Layout: The Battista's interior places a strong emphasis on the driver, ensuring optimal comfort and accessibility to controls and information.

High-Tech Features: The interior incorporates advanced infotainment systems, digital displays, and connectivity options for a seamless user experience.

Comfort-Oriented Seating: The Battista offers well-cushioned and comfortable seats, providing a balance of support and luxury for extended drives.

Generous Storage Space: Despite its performance-oriented nature, the Battista provides relatively generous storage compartments, offering practicality for longer journeys.


Differences:

Design Philosophy: The C_Two's interior design embraces a futuristic and high-tech aesthetic, focusing on minimalism and advanced controls. The Battista's interior design, on the other hand, combines luxury materials and handcrafted finishes for a more traditional and opulent ambiance.

Material Choices: While both vehicles incorporate high-quality materials, the C_Two emphasizes lightweight materials such as carbon fiber and Alcantara for performance optimization. The Battista, on the other hand, showcases premium leather, aluminum accents, and bespoke craftsmanship for a luxurious experience.

Technological Features: Both vehicles offer advanced digital displays and infotainment systems, but the C_Two's interior may showcase more cutting-edge and high-tech features, reflecting its technologically advanced nature.

Storage Space: The Battista provides more generous storage compartments compared to the C_Two, offering practicality for everyday use and longer journeys.
