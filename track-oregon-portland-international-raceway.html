Common Events at Portland International Raceway Rose Cup Races: The Rose Cup Races is an annual highlight at PIR, featuring a thrilling lineup of road course racing across various classes. This event draws top-tier drivers and showcases the best of regional and national talent. Portland Vintage Racing Festival: Vintage racing enthusiasts are treated to the Portland Vintage Racing Festival, where classic and historic race cars take to the track, transporting spectators back in time to the golden age of motorsports. Portland Grand Prix: PIR occasionally hosts the Portland Grand Prix, an event that brings professional racing series to the track, offering fans an opportunity to witness high-speed action from renowned drivers. Track Days and High-Performance Driving Schools: PIR hosts track days and high-performance driving schools, allowing enthusiasts to experience the thrill of racing while improving their driving skills under controlled conditions. Drag Racing Spectaculars: The drag strip at PIR is a hotbed of action during drag racing spectaculars, where fans are treated to intense acceleration, tire smoke, and side-by-side battles. Automotive Festivals and Shows: PIR hosts various automotive festivals and shows, including car shows, swap meets, and manufacturer events, creating a vibrant atmosphere for car enthusiasts of all kinds.
