Subaru WRX STi vs. Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack



Stock Motor

0-60 Acceleration:

Subaru WRX STi: The WRX STi boasts a turbocharged 2.5-liter flat-four engine that accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in approximately 5.2 seconds, showcasing its impressive acceleration capabilities.

Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack: The Charger R/T Scat Pack features a robust 6.4-liter V8 engine, reaching 0-60 mph in around 4.3 seconds, making it a potent contender in the acceleration department.


Torque:

Subaru WRX STi: The WRX STi generates a torque of approximately 290 lb-ft, providing ample power for spirited driving and responsive handling.

Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack: The Charger R/T Scat Pack delivers an astounding torque of around 475 lb-ft, displaying its muscle car heritage and high-performance capabilities.


Horsepower:

Subaru WRX STi: The WRX STi's turbocharged flat-four engine produces a horsepower of approximately 310 hp, providing a balanced power output for a fun driving experience.

Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack: The Charger R/T Scat Pack's formidable V8 engine churns out an impressive horsepower of around 485 hp, showcasing its raw power and performance.


Top Speed:

Subaru WRX STi: The WRX STi's top speed is electronically limited to approximately 155 mph, ensuring a safe and controlled driving experience.

Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack: The Charger R/T Scat Pack boasts a top speed of approximately 175 mph, allowing for exhilarating high-speed runs on straightaways.


Quarter Mile Time:

Subaru WRX STi: The WRX STi completes the quarter-mile in approximately 13.7 seconds, demonstrating its capability for quick acceleration.

Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack: The Charger R/T Scat Pack impressively finishes the quarter-mile in around 12.4 seconds, solidifying its status as a high-performance muscle car.


Lap Times:

Subaru WRX STi: The WRX STi is engineered for agile handling and precise control, allowing it to achieve competitive lap times on the track.

Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack: The Charger R/T Scat Pack's powerful engine and performance-oriented setup contribute to impressive lap times for a full-size sedan.


Powertrain Configuration:

Subaru WRX STi: The WRX STi features a symmetrical all-wheel-drive system, distributing power to all four wheels for optimal traction and stability.

Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack: The Charger R/T Scat Pack is equipped with a rear-wheel-drive configuration, channeling its powerful engine's torque to the rear wheels for exhilarating performance.


Transmission Type:

Subaru WRX STi: The WRX STi offers a 6-speed manual transmission, appealing to driving enthusiasts who prefer a more engaging and involved driving experience.

Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack: The Charger R/T Scat Pack comes with an 8-speed automatic transmission, providing smooth and quick gear changes for a comfortable and sporty drive.


Power-to-Weight Ratio:

Subaru WRX STi: With a curb weight of approximately 3,395 lbs and a horsepower of 310 hp, the WRX STi achieves a favorable power-to-weight ratio, contributing to its agile handling.

Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack: The Charger R/T Scat Pack has a curb weight of around 4,400 lbs and a horsepower of 485 hp, resulting in a commendable power-to-weight ratio, highlighting its impressive acceleration.


RPM (Shaft Speed):

Subaru WRX STi: The WRX STi's turbocharged engine can rev up to approximately 6,700 RPM (Revolutions Per Minute), delivering peak power and a thrilling driving experience.

Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack: The Charger R/T Scat Pack's V8 engine can achieve revs of around 6,000 RPM, providing a deep and powerful exhaust note.

Suspension

Subaru WRX STi Suspension:

All-Wheel Drive (AWD) System: The WRX STi is equipped with Subaru's renowned Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive system, providing exceptional traction and stability on various road conditions.

Performance-Tuned Suspension: The WRX STi's suspension is designed for agile handling and precise cornering, making it a popular choice among driving enthusiasts.

Inverted MacPherson Strut Front Suspension: The WRX STi features a sport-tuned front suspension, incorporating inverted MacPherson struts for enhanced rigidity and reduced unsprung weight.

Double-Wishbone Rear Suspension: At the rear, the WRX STi utilizes a double-wishbone suspension, contributing to improved rear-wheel traction and responsiveness during spirited driving.

Stiffer Springs and Dampers: The WRX STi comes with stiffer springs and dampers, providing a firm yet controlled ride for enthusiastic driving.


Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack Suspension:

Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD) Configuration: The Charger R/T Scat Pack boasts a traditional rear-wheel-drive layout, delivering a classic driving experience favored by muscle car enthusiasts.

Performance-Oriented Suspension: The Charger R/T Scat Pack is engineered for stable and planted handling, accommodating its powerful V8 engine and high-performance capabilities.

Performance-Tuned Bilstein Dampers: The Charger R/T Scat Pack features performance-tuned Bilstein dampers, enhancing ride control and handling during spirited driving.

Multi-Link Rear Suspension: The Charger R/T Scat Pack is equipped with a multi-link rear suspension, optimizing rear-wheel traction and stability during high-speed maneuvers.

Adaptive Damping Suspension: An available option for the Charger R/T Scat Pack is an adaptive damping suspension system, allowing the driver to adjust suspension settings based on driving preferences.


On-Road Comfort - Subaru WRX STi:

Sporty Firmness: The WRX STi's suspension tuning provides a sporty and firm ride, ensuring excellent road feedback and a connected driving experience.

Taut Handling: The precise suspension setup of the WRX STi enhances cornering capabilities, inspiring confidence during aggressive maneuvers.


On-Road Comfort - Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack:

Balanced Comfort: The Charger R/T Scat Pack's suspension strikes a balance between sportiness and comfort, offering a comfortable ride for daily driving without sacrificing performance.


Track Performance - Subaru WRX STi:

Track-Ready Handling: The WRX STi's performance-tuned suspension makes it an agile and capable track performer, with responsive handling during tight turns and corners.

High-Speed Stability: The all-wheel-drive system and suspension work together to provide stable and predictable handling during high-speed track sessions.


Track Performance - Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack:

Powerful Straight-Line Performance: The Charger R/T Scat Pack's suspension is optimized for straight-line acceleration, making it a formidable contender on drag strips and long straights.

Competent Cornering: While the Charger R/T Scat Pack may not match the WRX STi's agility, its multi-link rear suspension and performance-tuned dampers still provide competent cornering capabilities.


Weight Distribution - Subaru WRX STi:

Balanced Weight Distribution: The WRX STi's all-wheel-drive system and balanced chassis result in a relatively even weight distribution, contributing to its stable handling characteristics.


Weight Distribution - Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack:

Front-Heavy Weight Distribution: The Charger R/T Scat Pack's rear-wheel-drive layout results in a front-heavy weight distribution, requiring careful management during aggressive cornering.





Interior

Subaru WRX STi Interior:

Sporty Ambiance: The WRX STi's interior exudes a sporty ambiance, with performance-inspired elements such as bolstered seats and a three-spoke steering wheel.

Driver-Centric Layout: The WRX STi places emphasis on the driver, with controls and gauges positioned for easy access and an immersive driving experience.

Material Quality: While offering durable materials, the WRX STi's cabin can feel utilitarian compared to more premium offerings in its class.

Limited Passenger Space: The WRX STi's rear seating area is relatively compact, limiting rear legroom and headroom for taller passengers.

User-Friendly Infotainment: The WRX STi features a straightforward infotainment system, though it may lack some of the advanced features found in competitors.


Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack Interior:

Spacious and Comfortable: The Charger R/T Scat Pack's cabin offers more generous passenger space, providing ample legroom and headroom for all occupants.

Luxurious Appointments: The Charger R/T Scat Pack's interior is available with upscale materials and features, creating a more premium feel.

Versatility: As a full-size sedan, the Charger R/T Scat Pack offers a roomy trunk with practical cargo space for daily use and road trips.

Driver-Focused Cockpit: The Charger R/T Scat Pack's driver-oriented design provides easy access to essential controls, ensuring a comfortable driving experience.

Advanced Infotainment: The Charger R/T Scat Pack boasts a modern and intuitive infotainment system with larger touchscreens and advanced connectivity options.


Comfort and Ergonomics - Subaru WRX STi:

Supportive Sport Seats: The WRX STi comes with sporty, bolstered front seats, offering good lateral support during spirited driving.

Limited Comfort Features: While providing a sporty driving experience, the WRX STi's interior may lack some of the comfort-focused amenities found in luxury sedans.


Comfort and Ergonomics - Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack:

Comfortable Seating: The Charger R/T Scat Pack offers more plush and comfortable seats, making it an ideal choice for long-distance travel.

Driver and Passenger Convenience: The Charger R/T Scat Pack's cabin includes numerous convenience features, such as power-adjustable seats and available heated/ventilated options.


Technology and Infotainment - Subaru WRX STi:

Basic Infotainment: The WRX STi's infotainment system provides essential features, but it may not match the cutting-edge technology found in some rivals.

Limited Driver-Assistance Technology: The WRX STi may lack some advanced driver-assistance features that have become standard in many modern vehicles.


Technology and Infotainment - Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack:

Modern Infotainment System: The Charger R/T Scat Pack's infotainment system offers a larger touchscreen with user-friendly interfaces and smartphone integration.

Comprehensive Driver-Assistance Features: The Charger R/T Scat Pack provides various driver-assistance technologies, enhancing safety and convenience.


Interior Styling - Subaru WRX STi:

Sporty and Utilitarian Design: The WRX STi's cabin emphasizes a sporty feel, with straightforward design elements focused on driving performance.


Interior Styling - Dodge Charger R/T Scat Pack:

Upscale and Luxurious Design: The Charger R/T Scat Pack's interior offers an upscale feel, incorporating premium materials and refined styling cues.
