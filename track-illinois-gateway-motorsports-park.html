A Brief Overview Gateway Motorsports Park, now officially known as World Wide Technology Raceway, opened its gates in 1997. Since then, it has evolved into a multi-purpose racing complex that boasts a 1.25-mile oval track, a 1.6-mile road course, a state-of-the-art drag strip, and various supporting facilities. The park's strategic location near major highways and the city of St. Louis makes it easily accessible for both local and out-of-town visitors. Common Events The park's diverse offerings ensure there's something for everyone, whether you're a die-hard racing fan or a casual observer seeking an exciting weekend experience. Here are some of the common events that Gateway Motorsports Park hosts: 1. NASCAR Events: Gateway Motorsports Park is a host to NASCAR events, featuring some of the most talented drivers in stock car racing. The NASCAR Camping World Truck Series and the NASCAR Xfinity Series regularly grace the oval track, providing fans with high-speed battles and intense competition. 2. NHRA Drag Racing: The roar of engines and the smell of burning rubber are synonymous with Gateway Motorsports Park's NHRA drag racing events. The drag strip plays host to a variety of National Hot Rod Association (NHRA) events, including the NHRA Mello Yello Drag Racing Series. From Top Fuel and Funny Car to Pro Stock and Pro Mod, fans are treated to a sensory overload as these machines rocket down the quarter-mile track in a matter of seconds. 3. INDYCAR Races: The Verizon IndyCar Series, with its open-wheel machines and daring drivers, graces the Gateway Motorsports Park oval. These events showcase the finesse and speed of single-seater racing, drawing in a crowd of motorsport aficionados and newcomers alike. 4. Road Racing Competitions: For those who prefer the twists and turns of road racing, the park's road course offers an engaging experience. Various road racing events, such as sports car races and motorcycle events, take advantage of the track's challenging layout, providing competitors and spectators with exhilarating action. 5. Drift Events and Car Shows: Gateway Motorsports Park also caters to the car culture with events like drift competitions and car shows. These events allow enthusiasts to showcase their modified machines, engage in thrilling sideways action, and celebrate their shared passion for automobiles. 6. Driving Experiences: In addition to hosting spectator events, the park offers driving experiences that allow individuals to get behind the wheel of high-performance vehicles. From stock cars to exotic sports cars, participants can live out their racing fantasies under the guidance of experienced instructors. The Gateway Experience Beyond the track, Gateway Motorsports Park offers a complete experience for attendees. The park features modern amenities, comfortable seating, and convenient facilities that enhance the overall event experience. Spectators can enjoy a variety of food and beverage options, explore vendor displays, and immerse themselves in the motorsport culture that permeates the venue. Gateway Motorsports Park isn't just about racing; it's about creating lasting memories and fostering a sense of community among motorsport enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned race fan or someone new to the world of motorsports, the park's events offer an opportunity to witness the limits of speed, skill, and technology while being part of an electric atmosphere. In Conclusion Gateway Motorsports Park, with its impressive range of events catering to different tastes, stands as a testament to the enduring appeal of motorsport.
