Audi e-tron vs. Audi Q4 e-tron



Stock Motor

Acceleration (0-60 mph):

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron boasts powerful acceleration, capable of reaching 0 to 60 mph in approximately 5.5 seconds. Its dual electric motors deliver instant torque, ensuring swift and responsive acceleration.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron offers impressive acceleration, achieving 0 to 60 mph in approximately 6.3 seconds. Its electric powertrain provides a seamless power delivery, contributing to its spirited performance.


Torque:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron generates robust torque, totaling around 414 lb-ft. This high torque output contributes to its strong acceleration and towing capabilities.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron's dual electric motors produce a combined torque of approximately 229 lb-ft, providing ample low-end power for urban driving and highway merging.


Horsepower:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron delivers a total horsepower output of around 355 horsepower. Its dual electric motors work in tandem to provide impressive power and performance.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron's dual electric motors generate a combined horsepower of approximately 201 horsepower, making it a suitable option for daily commuting and urban driving.


Top Speed:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron boasts an electronically limited top speed of approximately 124 mph, ensuring a safe and controlled high-speed performance.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron achieves a top speed of around 99 mph, providing sufficient highway performance for most driving situations.


Quarter Mile Time:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron completes the quarter-mile sprint in about 13.9 seconds, showcasing its strong acceleration and performance capabilities.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron's quarter-mile time is approximately 15.6 seconds, delivering respectable performance for daily driving and city commuting.


Lap Times:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron is not typically considered a performance-oriented vehicle for track use. While it offers excellent acceleration and handling, its primary focus is on delivering a comfortable and luxurious driving experience.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron is designed as a versatile and practical electric SUV, making it more suitable for daily driving and urban environments rather than racetracks.


Powertrain Configuration:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron features a dual-motor all-wheel-drive powertrain configuration. Its two electric motors drive the front and rear wheels independently, providing enhanced traction and control.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron is also equipped with a dual-motor all-wheel-drive powertrain, delivering power to both the front and rear wheels for better stability and performance.


Transmission Type:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron uses a single-speed transmission, characteristic of electric vehicles. The absence of gear changes ensures smooth and seamless acceleration.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron's electric powertrain similarly utilizes a single-speed transmission, optimizing efficiency and power delivery.


Power-to-Weight Ratio:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron's power-to-weight ratio is influenced by its impressive power output and substantial weight due to its large battery pack and luxurious features.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron's power-to-weight ratio may be slightly higher compared to the Audi e-tron, as it is a more compact and lightweight electric SUV.


RPM (Shaft Speed):

Audi e-tron: Electric motors in the Audi e-tron operate at high RPMs, delivering instant torque and responsive acceleration. The precise RPM value varies depending on driving conditions and power demands.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron's electric motors also operate at high RPMs, ensuring a quick and seamless power delivery for a smooth driving experience.





Tuning Ability

Upgraded Intake: Audi e-tron: As an electric vehicle, the Audi e-tron does not have a traditional air intake system like a gasoline-powered car. However, some aftermarket companies offer performance air filters that can potentially improve airflow efficiency, optimizing battery cooling and improving overall system performance.

Audi Q4 e-tron: Similar to the Audi e-tron, the Q4 e-tron benefits from potential improvements in airflow management with high-quality air filters designed for EVs.


Forced Induction:

Audi e-tron: Electric vehicles do not rely on traditional forced induction systems like turbochargers or superchargers. Instead, they harness the instantaneous torque of their electric motors to deliver impressive acceleration and performance without the need for additional forced induction.

Audi Q4 e-tron: Similarly, the Audi Q4 e-tron's electric powertrain eliminates the need for forced induction, making its acceleration and power delivery naturally swift and responsive.


Fuel Management:

Audi e-tron: As an all-electric vehicle, the Audi e-tron does not have a conventional fuel management system. However, performance software tuning can optimize power delivery and torque vectoring for a more engaging driving experience.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron also benefits from software tuning, allowing for fine-tuning of the electric powertrain to enhance efficiency, power, and overall driving characteristics.


Ignition Options:

Audi e-tron: Electric vehicles like the Audi e-tron do not have spark plugs or a traditional ignition system. However, software tuning can optimize the ignition timing and power delivery of the electric motors to achieve better performance.

Audi Q4 e-tron: Similarly, the Audi Q4 e-tron's electric powertrain operates without spark plugs or a traditional ignition system, emphasizing the role of software tuning in enhancing performance.


Performance Software Tuning:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron's electric powertrain can benefit significantly from software tuning. Reputable tuners can optimize power delivery, torque management, and throttle response, allowing for a more engaging and dynamic driving experience.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron's electric powertrain also offers potential for software tuning, which can improve acceleration, responsiveness, and overall efficiency to cater to various driving preferences.


Suspension Upgrades:

Audi e-tron: Suspension upgrades can enhance the Audi e-tron's handling and cornering capabilities, providing a more engaging driving experience. Aftermarket performance springs, dampers, and sway bars can be considered for sportier dynamics.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron can also benefit from suspension upgrades to optimize its handling and ride comfort, ensuring a smooth and controlled driving experience.


Brake Upgrades:

Audi e-tron: Upgraded brake pads and rotors can improve the Audi e-tron's braking performance, providing better stopping power and reducing brake fade during spirited driving.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron can also be upgraded with enhanced brake components for confident and effective braking, ensuring safety and control during high-performance driving.





Suspension

Ride Comfort:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron is engineered to deliver a smooth and comfortable ride, befitting its status as a luxury electric SUV. Its suspension is specifically tuned to absorb road imperfections, providing a serene and quiet cabin ambiance for occupants.

Audi Q4 e-tron: Like its larger sibling, the Audi Q4 e-tron also offers a comfortable and refined ride, emphasizing a plush driving experience for both driver and passengers.


Handling and Agility:

Audi e-tron: While the Audi e-tron provides a comfortable ride, it also offers confident handling and stability. Its suspension strikes a balance between comfort and sportiness, making it a capable SUV for both daily commuting and long-distance journeys.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron is designed for nimble handling and urban maneuverability. Its suspension tuning emphasizes agility and responsiveness, making it an ideal choice for city driving and tight parking spaces.


Ground Clearance:

Audi e-tron: As a larger SUV, the Audi e-tron offers higher ground clearance, making it suitable for light off-road adventures and handling rough road conditions with ease.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron's more compact size provides a lower ground clearance, enhancing aerodynamics and maneuverability in urban environments.


Weight Distribution:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron's weight distribution is well-balanced, aided by its large battery pack positioned along the vehicle's floor. This balanced weight distribution contributes to its stable and predictable handling.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron's weight distribution is also well-managed, ensuring stability and control during various driving scenarios.


Driving Modes:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron offers various driving modes, including comfort, efficiency, and dynamic modes. These modes adjust the suspension and powertrain settings to cater to different driving preferences.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron is equipped with similar driving modes, allowing drivers to customize their driving experience based on road conditions and personal preferences.


Suspension Components:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron features an adaptive air suspension as standard, providing a comfortable and adjustable ride quality. Optional upgrades, such as adaptive dampers, offer further customization.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron is equipped with standard suspension tuned for a balance of comfort and agility. It may offer optional adaptive dampers to further fine-tune the suspension setup.


Brake Upgrades:

Audi e-tron: Upgraded brake components can improve the Audi e-tron's braking performance, providing confident and precise stopping power.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron's brake system is designed for effective braking performance, ensuring safety and control during all driving situations.





Interior

Design and Layout:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron features a sophisticated and modern interior design. The cockpit is driver-centric, with premium materials, clean lines, and refined aesthetics. The dashboard is characterized by its horizontal layout, adding to the sense of spaciousness.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron also boasts a contemporary interior design, with a focus on simplicity and functionality. The dashboard layout is elegant, and the cabin is designed to provide ample space for all passengers.


Materials and Finish:

Audi e-tron: Inside the Audi e-tron, you'll find top-notch materials, such as leather, Alcantara, and genuine wood trims. The fit and finish are impeccable, reflecting Audi's commitment to luxury craftsmanship.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron offers a high-quality interior, with premium materials and soft-touch surfaces. While it may not have the same extensive range of luxury options as the e-tron, it still provides a comfortable and upscale cabin environment.


Infotainment System:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron is equipped with Audi's advanced MMI (Multi-Media Interface) infotainment system. It features a large touchscreen display with intuitive controls, providing access to navigation, smartphone integration, and various vehicle settings.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron also comes with Audi's MMI infotainment system, featuring a touchscreen display with user-friendly functionality and seamless smartphone integration.


Digital Instrument Cluster:

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron is equipped with a similar Virtual Cockpit, offering a digital instrument cluster that keeps the driver informed and connected on the road.


Seating and Comfort:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron provides comfortable and supportive seats for both the driver and passengers. The front seats offer power adjustability and optional ventilation and massage functions for added comfort.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron's seats prioritize comfort and space, providing ample legroom and headroom for all occupants. Optional upgrades may include features like heated seats and power adjustability.


Storage Space:

Audi e-tron: As a larger SUV, the Audi e-tron offers generous cargo space with a rear cargo capacity of approximately 28.5 cubic feet. Folding the rear seats further increases the cargo capacity to accommodate larger items.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron's compact size results in a slightly smaller cargo space, offering around 18.7 cubic feet with the rear seats in place. Folding the rear seats expands the cargo area for added versatility.


Luxury Features:

Audi e-tron: The Audi e-tron comes with a host of luxury features, including multi-zone climate control, ambient lighting, and premium audio systems. Optional upgrades, such as panoramic sunroof and high-end sound systems, further enhance the luxurious ambiance.

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron also offers a range of luxury features, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable driving experience. Optional upgrades may include features like a panoramic sunroof and premium audio systems.


Driver Assistance and Safety:

Audi Q4 e-tron: The Audi Q4 e-tron also features a comprehensive suite of driver assistance technologies, providing added safety and convenience during daily driving.
