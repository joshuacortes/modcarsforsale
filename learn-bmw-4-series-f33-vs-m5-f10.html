BMW 4 Series F33 vs. M5 F10



Stock Motor

BMW 4er F33:

0-60 mph: Varies depending on the engine and trim level. Ranges from around 5.0 to 6.0 seconds.

Torque: Varies based on the engine choice. Ranges from approximately 258 lb-ft to 369 lb-ft.

Horsepower: Varies based on the engine choice. Ranges from approximately 184 hp to 440 hp.

Top Speed: Electronically limited to around 130 to 155 mph (depending on the trim and options).

Quarter Mile Time: Varies depending on the engine and configuration. Approximately mid-13 to low-15 seconds.

Lap Times: Not specifically recorded, as the 4er F33 is more focused on comfort and luxury than track performance.


Powertrain Configuration:

Typically rear-wheel drive (RWD) but also available with xDrive all-wheel drive (AWD) for certain trims.

Engine options include a range of gasoline and diesel engines, varying in displacement and output.


Transmission Type:

Typically equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Some models may have a 6-speed manual option.


Power-to-Weight Ratio:

The power-to-weight ratio varies depending on the specific engine and configuration chosen for the 4er F33.

RPM (Shaft Speed):

Engine speed varies depending on the specific engine and trim level. The redline typically ranges from around 6,000 to 7,000 RPM.



BMW M5 F10:

0-60 mph: Approximately 4.0 seconds.

Torque: 501 lb-ft.

Horsepower: 560 hp.

Top Speed: Electronically limited to 155 mph (189 mph with the optional M Driver's Package).

Quarter Mile Time: Approximately 12.0 to 12.5 seconds.

Lap Times (Nürburgring): Not officially recorded.

Powertrain Configuration:

Rear-wheel drive (RWD).

Transmission Type:

Equipped with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission.

Power-to-Weight Ratio:

Approximately 8.3 lb/hp.

RPM (Shaft Speed):

The redline is set at around 7,200 RPM.





Tuning Ability

BMW 4er F33:


Intake Upgrades:

Cold Air Intake Systems: AFE Power, Dinan, K&N.

Performance Air Filters: BMC, Green Filter, Mishimoto.


Exhaust Upgrades:

Cat-Back Exhaust Systems: Borla, Eisenmann, Remus.

Downpipes: Active Autowerke, Supersprint, Agency Power.

Performance Headers: Akrapovic, DINAN, Meisterschaft.


Forced Induction:

Supercharger Kits: DINAN, VF Engineering.

Turbocharger Upgrades: Pure Turbos, MMP Engineering.

Intercooler Upgrades: Wagner Tuning, CSF Performance.


Fuel Management:

Engine Control Units (ECU): DINAN, Active Autowerke, Cobb Tuning.

Performance Fuel Injectors: Bosch, Injector Dynamics, DeatschWerks.


Ignition Options:

High-Performance Ignition Coils: NGK, MSD, Bosch.



BMW M5 F10:


Intake Upgrades:

Cold Air Intake Systems: Eventuri, BMC, Dinan.

Performance Air Filters: K&N, AFE Power, Green Filter.


Exhaust Upgrades:

Cat-Back Exhaust Systems: Akrapovic, Eisenmann, Meisterschaft.

Downpipes: AMS, Armytrix, Evolution Racewerks.

Performance Headers: Dinan, Supersprint, Agency Power.


Forced Induction:

Supercharger Kits: DINAN, ESS Tuning.

Turbocharger Upgrades: Pure Turbos, Xona Rotor.

Intercooler Upgrades: Wagner Tuning, CSF Performance, Evolution Racewerks.


Fuel Management:

Engine Control Units (ECU): DINAN, Burger Motorsports, Cobb Tuning.

Performance Fuel Injectors: Injector Dynamics, Bosch, DeatschWerks.


Ignition Options:

High-Performance Ignition Coils: NGK, MSD, Bosch.


Both the BMW 4er F33 and BMW M5 F10 offer ample tuning potential for enthusiasts looking to extract more power and performance from their vehicles. The 4er F33, with its range of engine options, can benefit from upgraded intake and exhaust systems, as well as forced induction options such as superchargers or turbochargers. Upgrading fuel management and ignition components can further enhance performance.

The M5 F10, being a high-performance sports sedan, already possesses impressive power. However, enthusiasts can still explore additional performance gains through intake and exhaust upgrades, forced induction enhancements, and optimizing fuel management and ignition systems.

It's crucial to note that tuning modifications should be approached with caution and performed by experienced professionals. It's recommended to research reputable tuning companies and consult with experts to ensure compatibility, reliability, and optimal performance.





Suspension

BMW 4er F33:


Suspension Design:

The 4er F33 utilizes a double-wishbone front suspension and a multi-link rear suspension.

The suspension design provides a balance between comfort and sportiness, offering a smooth ride with responsive handling.


Damping and Spring Rates:

The suspension of the 4er F33 is typically tuned for a comfortable and refined driving experience.

The damping and spring rates are calibrated to offer a good balance between absorbing bumps and maintaining stability during cornering.


Ride Height and Stance:

The 4er F33 generally has a lower ride height compared to other BMW models, resulting in a more aggressive and sporty stance.

The lowered ride height contributes to improved aerodynamics and enhanced handling characteristics.


BMW M5 F10:

Suspension Design:

The M5 F10 is equipped with a performance-oriented suspension system that emphasizes precise handling and enhanced dynamics.

It features a combination of double-wishbone front suspension and multi-link rear suspension.


Damping and Spring Rates:

The suspension of the M5 F10 is significantly stiffer and more performance-oriented compared to the 4er F33.

The damping and spring rates are optimized for maximum grip and cornering performance, providing a more responsive and engaging driving experience.

Ride Height and Stance:

The M5 F10 has a lower ride height compared to its standard sedan counterparts, contributing to its aggressive appearance and improved aerodynamics.

The lower stance and sport-tuned suspension result in reduced body roll and enhanced stability during high-speed cornering.


Additional Features:

The M5 F10 may feature additional performance-enhancing components such as adjustable dampers, active suspension systems, or adaptive M suspension to further fine-tune the driving experience.

Some M5 F10 models offer adjustable suspension settings, allowing drivers to select between comfort and sport modes to suit their preferences.





Interior

BMW 4er F33:


Design and Layout:

The 4er F33 features a driver-centric cockpit with a focus on ergonomics and easy access to controls.

The dashboard layout is clean and streamlined, incorporating modern design elements.

Controls and buttons are intuitively placed for ease of use.

The convertible design offers an open-air driving experience, with retractable hardtop options available.


Materials and Finish:

The 4er F33 offers high-quality materials throughout the interior, including premium upholstery options.

Soft-touch surfaces and refined finishes contribute to a luxurious ambiance.

Aluminum or wood trim accents add sophistication to the overall design.


Features and Technology:

The 4er F33 comes equipped with modern features, including a central infotainment system with touchscreen functionality.

Integrated navigation, Bluetooth connectivity, and smartphone integration are standard or available options.

Advanced driver-assistance systems, such as blind-spot monitoring and parking sensors, may be included.



BMW M5 F10:

Design and Layout:

The M5 F10 features a sporty and performance-oriented interior design.

The cockpit is driver-focused, with controls and displays optimized for quick and easy access.

The dashboard design incorporates dynamic lines and angles, reflecting the car's sporty nature.


Materials and Finish:

The M5 F10 showcases premium materials and finishes, with high-quality leather upholstery options.

Carbon fiber or aluminum trim accents add a sporty and high-performance touch to the interior.


Features and Technology:

The M5 F10 offers advanced technology and features to enhance the driving experience.

The central infotainment system provides access to navigation, media, and vehicle settings.

Additional features may include a heads-up display, premium audio systems, and advanced driver-assistance systems.


Comfort and Space:

The M5 F10 provides ample space and comfort for occupants, with supportive and sporty seating.

Rear-seat legroom and headroom may be slightly compromised due to the car's sporty design.
