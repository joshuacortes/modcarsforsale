Stock Motor

Performance Metrics:


0-60 mph (0-96.5 km/h):

Volvo XC40 Recharge: Approximately 4.7 seconds

Polestar 2: Approximately 4.45 seconds


Torque:

Volvo XC40 Recharge: 487 lb-ft (660 Nm)

Polestar 2: 413 lb-ft (560 Nm)


Horsepower:

Volvo XC40 Recharge: 402 hp (300 kW)

Polestar 2: 408 hp (304 kW)


Top Speed:

Both the Volvo XC40 Recharge and the Polestar 2 are electronically limited to 112 mph (180 km/h) for safety reasons.


Quarter Mile Time:

Volvo XC40 Recharge: Approximately 12.7 seconds

Polestar 2: Approximately 12.5 seconds


Lap Times:

As lap times can vary significantly depending on the track, conditions, and driver, no official lap time comparisons are available for these vehicles. However, both the XC40 Recharge and the Polestar 2 boast sporty driving dynamics, making them enjoyable on the road and the occasional spirited driving.


Powertrain Configuration:

Volvo XC40 Recharge: The XC40 Recharge features a dual-motor setup, with one electric motor driving the front wheels and another powering the rear wheels. This configuration offers all-wheel-drive capability, enhancing stability and traction.

Polestar 2: Similarly, the Polestar 2 also utilizes a dual-motor setup with one motor for the front wheels and another for the rear wheels, providing an all-wheel-drive system that ensures balanced and controlled driving dynamics.


Transmission Type:

Both the Volvo XC40 Recharge and the Polestar 2 come with a single-speed direct-drive transmission. This simplicity ensures a smooth and seamless driving experience without the need for gear shifting.


Power-to-Weight Ratio:

Volvo XC40 Recharge: Approximately 170.3 W/kg

Polestar 2: Approximately 223.3 W/kg


RPM (Shaft Speed):

Both vehicles feature an electric motor that can spin at extremely high RPMs, allowing for instant torque delivery and rapid acceleration. However, specific RPM figures for the stock motors are not readily available in the public domain.





Suspension

Suspension Design:


Volvo XC40 Recharge:

Front Suspension: MacPherson Strut

Rear Suspension: Multi-Link


Polestar 2:

Front Suspension: Double Wishbone

Rear Suspension: Integral Link


Ride Comfort:


Volvo XC40 Recharge:

The XC40 Recharge's MacPherson Strut front suspension and Multi-Link rear suspension are optimized for a comfortable ride. It provides a smooth and plush driving experience, effectively absorbing road imperfections and minimizing vibrations.


Polestar 2:

With its Double Wishbone front suspension and Integral Link rear suspension, the Polestar 2 also delivers a comfortable ride. The double wishbone setup enhances suspension travel and improves road feedback, making it feel more engaging for spirited driving.


Handling and Stability:



Volvo XC40 Recharge:

The MacPherson Strut front suspension in the XC40 Recharge is well-tuned for predictable and stable handling. It offers a reassuring sense of control, making it a practical choice for everyday driving scenarios.




Polestar 2:

The Double Wishbone front suspension of the Polestar 2 is designed to provide excellent handling dynamics. It enhances cornering stability and reduces body roll, giving the driver a more sporty and responsive feel on the road.

Sportiness and Agility:


Volvo XC40 Recharge:

While the XC40 Recharge focuses on comfort and stability, its suspension setup may not offer the same level of sportiness and agility as the Polestar 2.

Polestar 2:

Thanks to its performance-oriented suspension design, the Polestar 2 offers a more dynamic and agile driving experience. The combination of Double Wishbone and Integral Link suspensions contributes to its sporty and spirited nature.


Adjustability and Driving Modes:

Volvo XC40 Recharge:


The XC40 Recharge may come equipped with adjustable shock absorbers, allowing the driver to fine-tune the suspension characteristics to some extent. It offers different driving modes to adjust the overall driving feel.


Polestar 2:

As a performance-oriented electric vehicle, the Polestar 2 offers a high degree of adjustability in its suspension. Drivers can select different driving modes that alter suspension settings, steering response, and overall performance, tailoring the driving experience to their preferences.





Interior

Design and Aesthetics:

Volvo XC40 Recharge:

The XC40 Recharge boasts a modern and sophisticated cabin design, featuring clean lines and minimalist styling.

The interior is practical and functional, with a focus on user-friendliness and ease of use.


Polestar 2:

The Polestar 2 takes a more futuristic and avant-garde approach to interior design.

It exudes a sense of premium luxury with its high-quality materials and attention to detail.


Materials and Finishes:


Volvo XC40 Recharge:

The XC40 Recharge offers a comfortable and inviting interior with a mix of premium materials, such as leather, soft-touch plastics, and aluminum accents.

The cabin is crafted with sustainable materials, reflecting Volvo's commitment to eco-friendly practices.


Polestar 2:

As a luxury performance EV, the Polestar 2 elevates the interior experience with upscale materials like Nappa leather, genuine wood trim, and metal inserts.

It features vegan interior options for those seeking an entirely animal-free cabin.


Infotainment and Technology:

Volvo XC40 Recharge:

The XC40 Recharge comes equipped with Volvo's user-friendly Sensus infotainment system, featuring a large vertically-oriented touchscreen.

It offers seamless smartphone integration, wireless charging, and a high-quality sound system for an enjoyable in-car experience.


Polestar 2:

The Polestar 2 showcases the latest technology with an impressive 11-inch portrait-oriented touchscreen, powered by Android Automotive OS.

It offers Google services, voice control, and over-the-air updates for enhanced connectivity and future-proofing.


Space and Comfort:

Volvo XC40 Recharge:

The XC40 Recharge provides a spacious and comfortable cabin with ample headroom and legroom for both front and rear passengers.

It offers a practical cargo space, making it ideal for family use and daily commuting.


Polestar 2:

The Polestar 2 also offers generous space for passengers, providing a comfortable and luxurious environment.

The interior design is more driver-centric, emphasizing the sporty and performance-oriented nature of the vehicle.


Unique Features:

Volvo XC40 Recharge:

The XC40 Recharge features Volvo's signature safety and driver-assistance systems, such as City Safety, Pilot Assist, and Blind Spot Information System (BLIS).

It emphasizes a cozy and relaxed atmosphere, perfect for long journeys and daily commutes.

Polestar 2:

The Polestar 2 highlights its performance-oriented features, including adjustable Öhlins dampers for improved handling and responsiveness.

It offers an optional Performance Pack, adding features like Brembo brakes and forged 20-inch wheels for an exhilarating driving experience.
