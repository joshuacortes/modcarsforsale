Top 10 Best Floor Jacks



A good floor jack is essential for mechanics and anyone who works on their own car. A jack is frequently included in new automobiles as part of the spare tire/roadside tool package, but they are hand-cranked and usually rather small; consider how much weight your entire car would be supporting on a platform no bigger than a smartphone.

With tons of floor jacks available in the market, we've narrowed down our select/top 10. Ideally, you'd want to have one that is simple to use and most importantly, secure:


1. Torin Hydraulic Ultra Low Profile Heavy Duty Steel Floor Jack

2. AC Hydraulics DK20Q 2.2 Ton Low Profile Hydraulic Service Jack

3. Low profile: Powerbuilt 2 Ton Extra Low Profile Floor Jack

4. Best budget: Pro-Lift F-767 Grey Low Profile Floor Jack

5. Long reach & low profile: Sunex 6602LP 2-Ton Low Rider Steel Service Jack

6. SUVs & Trucks: AFF 4 Ton Heavy Duty Floor Jack

7. Pro-Lift F-767 2-Ton Low-Profile Floor Jack

8. Pro-Lift F-2315PE Hydraulic Trolley Jack

9. Mobile: Torin Big Red T820014S 1.5-Ton Trolley Service/Floor Jack with Case

10. K Tool International 3 Ton Floor Jack


When shopping for a floor jack, select the appropriate capacity. Find your gross vehicle weight, as well as the front and rear weights, inside the car's door or in the user guide. The floor jack you use should have a lifting capacity greater than the weight of your car. But be careful not to overdo it; the higher the lifting capability, the heavier and slower your jack will be.
