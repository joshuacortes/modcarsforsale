Best Automotive/Car Racing Tracks in Nevada



Public & Track Day

Las Vegas Motor Speedway: Located in Las Vegas, this versatile facility features a NASCAR oval, a road course, and a drag strip. It hosts various racing events, track day experiences, and driving schools.

Spring Mountain Motor Resort and Country Club: Situated in Pahrump, this premium motorsports complex offers a road course and various driving experiences, including track days and performance driving schools.

SpeedVegas: Found in Las Vegas, SpeedVegas offers a road course and provides a range of driving experiences, including track days and exotic car driving sessions.

Apex Motor Club: Located in Maricopa, Arizona (near the Nevada border), Apex Motor Club is a high-end private motorsports facility that provides track day experiences and driving events.

Battle Born HPDE and Motorsports Park: Situated in Fernley, this grassroots track hosts track day events and driving experiences for enthusiasts.

Buttonwillow Raceway Park: While not located in Nevada, Buttonwillow Raceway Park in California might be accessible to some residents of Nevada. It features multiple track configurations and hosts track day events.

Local Car Clubs and Organizations: Nevada's car community might organize track day events or driving experiences at various tracks in the state.


Before planning a visit to any of these tracks, I recommend checking their official websites or contacting the track management to verify track availability, event schedules, and any specific requirements for participating in track day events. Additionally, ensure that you adhere to safety regulations and guidelines when participating in track day activities.





Spectator Car Racing Tracks in Nevada

Las Vegas Motor Speedway: Located in Las Vegas, Las Vegas Motor Speedway hosts a variety of racing events, including NASCAR races, INDYCAR races, NHRA drag racing events, and more. It provides an excellent atmosphere for spectators to witness high-speed action.

Reno-Fernley Raceway: Situated in Fernley, this road course hosts various racing events and might offer opportunities for spectators to enjoy road racing action.

The Strip at Las Vegas Motor Speedway: Found in Las Vegas, The Strip at Las Vegas Motor Speedway is a drag strip that hosts NHRA drag racing events, providing an exciting environment for watching drag racing action.

Spring Mountain Motor Resort and Country Club: Located in Pahrump, Spring Mountain offers various motorsports events, including road racing and driving experiences, providing a unique atmosphere for spectators.

SpeedVegas: Situated in Las Vegas, SpeedVegas offers a road course and provides driving experiences. While it's more focused on participants, spectators might still enjoy watching some events.

Local Car Shows and Festivals: Nevada hosts various car shows, festivals, and automotive events throughout the year. These events often feature a diverse range of cars and provide entertainment for enthusiasts.


When planning to attend events at these tracks, make sure to check the official websites for event schedules, ticketing information, and any COVID-19 related updates to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience. Additionally, consider reaching out to local motorsports organizations, car clubs, and enthusiasts' forums to stay updated on upcoming events in your area.
