Import and Sport Compact Racing: Englishtown Raceway Park has been a hotspot for import and sport compact racing events, attracting a diverse range of vehicles from the import tuning scene. Vintage Drag Racing Events: The raceway occasionally hosts vintage drag racing events that pay homage to the classic era of drag racing. Test and Tune Nights: Englishtown Raceway Park offers test and tune nights, providing amateur racers and enthusiasts the opportunity to refine their skills, test vehicle modifications, and improve their quarter-mile times. Specialty Events and Car Shows: In addition to drag racing, Englishtown Raceway Park hosts specialty events, car shows, and exhibitions that celebrate various aspects of automotive culture.
