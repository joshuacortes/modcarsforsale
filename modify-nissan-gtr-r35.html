Nissan GT-R R35

The Nissan GT-R is a high-performance sports car. A 3,799 cc (3.8 L; 231.8 cu in) 60° DOHC V6 engine with plasma transferred wire arc sprayed cylinder bores, the factory VR38DETT engine was specifically designed for the Nissan GT-R.


Modified Nissan GT-R R35

Modifying a Nissan GTR R35, building a showcar/project drift car, racecar or anything in between? Be aware of the aftermarket ability and overall support available for your R35 build:

Unmatched Performance: The GT-R R35 comes with a powerful twin-turbocharged V6 engine and advanced all-wheel-drive system, providing impressive horsepower and torque figures, making it an exhilarating and high-performance machine straight from the factory.

Thriving Aftermarket Support: The GT-R R35 has a robust aftermarket community with a vast array of performance parts readily available, from engine upgrades to suspension components, exhaust systems, and body kits. Enthusiasts can easily customize and tailor their GT-R to their specific preferences and driving style.

Iconic Design: The GT-R R35's aggressive stance, aerodynamic lines, and distinctive appearance have made it an automotive icon, recognized worldwide for its prowess on the racetrack and the road. Modifying the GT-R allows owners to enhance its bold and eye-catching presence, making it a showstopper at automotive events and gatherings.

Proven Motorsports Success: The GT-R's engineering excellence and tunability have led to impressive records in the world of high-performance cars. Many GT-R owners take pride in building their cars to achieve impressive quarter-mile times, lap records, and top speeds, solidifying its reputation as a highly respected and competitive platform.

Unparalleled Driving Experience: Modifying the GT-R R35 can result in significant power gains and improved handling, offering an unparalleled driving experience for enthusiasts seeking thrills on the road or track.

Personalized Expression: Building and modifying a GT-R R35 allows owners to create a unique expression of automotive excellence, showcasing their individual style and taste through customizations, while still benefiting from the car's engineering prowess.

Future-Proof Investment: The GT-R R35's enduring appeal and iconic status make it a sought-after collector's car. By building and modifying a GT-R, owners can potentially increase its value and desirability, turning it into a future-proof investment in the world of automotive enthusiasts.

COMMON NISSAN GT-R R35 MODIFICATIONS:

The allure of modifying the Nissan GT-R R35 lies in its potential for further performance refinement and personalization. Most people are drawn to the prospect of unlocking more power from the already impressive engine, propelling the GT-R to even greater speeds and acceleration.

Upgrading the suspension allows enthusiasts to tailor the car's handling characteristics, ensuring precise control and responsiveness during spirited driving. Enhanced braking systems offer peace of mind and improved safety, crucial for harnessing the GT-R's immense power. A captivating exhaust note is a sought-after modification, not only for its thrilling sound but also for improved engine efficiency.




NISSAN GT-R R35 Turbocharger

AMS Alpha 12X Turbo Kit

The AMS Alpha 12X Turbo Kit is renowned for pushing the GT-R's performance to extreme levels. This kit features larger Garrett GT-based turbochargers, a custom intake manifold, upgraded intercoolers, and high-flowing wastegates. With its optimized design and advanced engineering, the Alpha 12X kit allows the GT-R R35 to reach horsepower figures upwards of 1,200 horsepower. While this level of power requires supporting modifications and proper tuning, the AMS Alpha 12X Turbo Kit is an unrivaled option for those seeking record-breaking performance.


HKS GT800 Full Turbo Upgrade Kit

HKS, a well-respected name in the automotive aftermarket, offers the GT800 Full Turbo Upgrade Kit for the GT-R R35. This kit includes twin GT II Series ball-bearing turbochargers, designed to provide a substantial increase in power without compromising low-end response. With the HKS GT800 kit, the GT-R gains impressive horsepower gains while maintaining drivability for both street and track use. The kit also includes the necessary components for a straightforward installation, making it an appealing option for enthusiasts looking for a balance between power and practicality.


T1 Race Development T1 GTR Turbo Kit

The T1 GTR Turbo Kit from T1 Race Development is a comprehensive turbocharger upgrade designed for serious performance enthusiasts. This kit utilizes precision-machined billet turbochargers, high-flow exhaust manifolds, and larger intercoolers to optimize airflow and cooling efficiency. The T1 GTR Turbo Kit is available in different configurations to suit various power goals, ranging from around 800 to over 1,000 horsepower. With its high-quality components and reliable performance gains, the T1 GTR Turbo Kit is a popular choice among GT-R owners looking to elevate their car's power and acceleration.


Greddy TD06SH-20G Twin Turbo Upgrade Kit

Greddy, a trusted name in the automotive aftermarket, offers the TD06SH-20G Twin Turbo Upgrade Kit for the GT-R R35. This kit features twin TD06SH-20G turbochargers with upgraded billet compressor wheels, providing improved response and increased airflow. The Greddy kit offers significant horsepower gains while maintaining excellent street drivability and response. For GT-R owners seeking a well-rounded upgrade with proven performance, the Greddy TD06SH-20G Twin Turbo Kit is a solid choice.


Important Considerations:

Before opting for a turbocharger upgrade on the Nissan GT-R R35, consider the following:

Supporting Modifications: Upgrading the turbos to achieve higher power levels typically requires additional supporting modifications, such as upgraded fuel systems, intercoolers, exhaust systems, and engine management solutions.

Professional Tuning: Proper tuning is crucial to optimizing the turbocharger upgrade and ensuring safe and reliable performance. Seek the expertise of a reputable tuner with experience in GT-R modifications.

Driving Goals: Consider your driving goals and how the turbocharger upgrade aligns with your intended use. Whether it's street driving, track racing, or a combination of both, choose a turbo kit that suits your preferences.




NISSAN GT-R R35 Suspension

KW Variant 3 Coilovers

The KW Variant 3 Coilovers are a top-notch suspension upgrade for the GT-R R35. These coilovers offer individually adjustable compression and rebound damping, allowing enthusiasts to fine-tune their car's handling characteristics for different driving scenarios. The KW Variant 3's stainless steel construction ensures durability and resistance to corrosion, making it an ideal choice for daily driving and track use. With their precise adjustment capabilities, the KW Variant 3 Coilovers provide excellent body control and improved cornering performance, enhancing the GT-R's already impressive handling capabilities.


Ohlins Road & Track Coilovers

For GT-R owners seeking a premium suspension upgrade, the Ohlins Road & Track Coilovers are an excellent option. These coilovers feature a unique dual-flow valve design, providing consistent damping performance and a comfortable ride on the street while remaining responsive during spirited driving. The Ohlins Road & Track Coilovers also offer adjustable ride height and damping settings, allowing enthusiasts to customize the car's stance and handling characteristics according to their preferences. With its race-proven technology and high-quality construction, the Ohlins suspension delivers exceptional handling and control for the GT-R R35.


Eibach Pro-Street-S Coilovers

The Eibach Pro-Street-S Coilovers are a popular choice among GT-R owners looking for a performance-oriented suspension upgrade without sacrificing daily drivability. These coilovers feature a corrosion-resistant stainless steel construction and a mono-tube design, providing precise handling and improved responsiveness. With their easy-to-adjust damping settings, the Eibach Pro-Street-S Coilovers allow enthusiasts to fine-tune their GT-R's ride comfort and handling performance. Whether it's tackling the twists and turns of a track or cruising through city streets, the Eibach suspension offers a well-rounded upgrade for the GT-R R35.


Swift Spec-R Springs

For GT-R owners seeking to enhance their car's handling without replacing the entire suspension, the Swift Spec-R Springs are an excellent option. These progressive rate springs offer improved cornering performance and reduced body roll, providing a more agile and responsive driving experience. The Swift Spec-R Springs are designed to work seamlessly with the GT-R's stock dampers, making them an easy and cost-effective suspension upgrade. By lowering the car's ride height slightly, the Swift springs improve the GT-R's stance, giving it a more aggressive appearance while retaining ride comfort for everyday use.


Important Considerations:

Before modifying the suspension of your Nissan GT-R R35, consider the following:

Driving Style: Determine your primary driving style and intended use of the car. Different suspension setups cater to various driving preferences, from daily driving and spirited street use to track racing and high-performance applications.

Compatibility: Ensure that the chosen suspension products are specifically designed for the GT-R R35 and are compatible with other modifications you may have installed.

Professional Installation: Suspension upgrades often require proper installation and tuning to ensure optimal performance and safety. Seek the expertise of a reputable mechanic or tuning specialist for installation and setup.

NISSAN GT-R R35 Wheels

Wider Wheels and Staggered Fitment

One of the most popular ways to modify the fitment and stance of the Nissan GT-R R35 is by opting for wider wheels and a staggered fitment. This involves using different wheel widths and tire sizes on the front and rear axles. By choosing wider wheels, you can accommodate wider tires, which not only improve traction and handling but also fill out the wheel arches, giving the GT-R a more assertive and aggressive stance.


Example:
Front Wheel Model: Volk Racing TE37
Front Wheel Size: 20x9.5 inches
Front Offset: +22mm
Front Tire Size: 255/35R20
Rear Wheel Model: Volk Racing TE37
Rear Wheel Size: 20x11 inches
Rear Offset: +18mm
Rear Tire Size: 305/30R20



Flush Fitment for a Clean and Sleek Look

A flush fitment aims to align the wheel's outer lip with the fender's edge, creating a clean and sleek appearance. This setup involves wheels that are neither excessively tucked under the fenders nor aggressively extending outwards. A flush fitment enhances the GT-R's aesthetics while ensuring the tires remain within the fender lines for a well-proportioned look.


Example:

Wheel Model: Advan GT Premium Version
Wheel Size: 20x10 inches
Offset: +40mm
Tire Size: 275/35R20

For GT-R owners seeking the flexibility to adjust their car's stance at will, an air suspension system is an excellent option. Air suspension allows you to raise or lower the car's height according to your preference, offering the versatility to switch between comfortable daily driving and a more aggressive show stance.

Important Considerations:

Before modifying the fitment and stance of your Nissan GT-R R35, consider the following:

Alignment: Proper alignment is crucial to maintain optimal handling and tire wear. Ensure the wheels are correctly aligned after any fitment adjustments.

Fender Clearance: Verify that the chosen fitment does not cause rubbing or interference with the fenders during suspension compression or steering movements.

Professional Installation: Suspension and fitment modifications should be carried out by experienced professionals to ensure safety and proper functionality.
