Liberty Walk Body Kit

This kit includes wide fenders, a front splitter, side skirts, and a rear diffuser, giving the car a more aggressive and unique look. Many car enthusiasts have installed this kit on their GT-Rs, and some have even added custom touches, such as unique paint jobs or aftermarket wheels.


Alpha 12 Package

The Alpha 12 package is a high-performance upgrade package offered by AMS Performance. The result is a Nissan GT-R R35 that can produce over 1,000 horsepower, making it one of the most powerful cars on the road.


Switzer P800 Package

Another popular high-performance upgrade package for the Nissan GT-R R35 is the Switzer P800 package. Armytrix Exhaust System

Many car enthusiasts have installed this exhaust system on their GT-Rs, and some have even added custom exhaust tips or other modifications to make it stand out even more.


The Nissan GT-R R35 has always been one of the most popular sports cars on the market, known for its impressive performance and sleek design. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the modifications that can be made to a Nissan GT-R R35.


Engine Modifications

One of the most popular modifications for the Nissan GT-R R35 is upgrading the engine. There are many ways to do this, including adding a turbocharger or supercharger, upgrading the fuel injectors, and replacing the exhaust system. These modifications can significantly increase the car's horsepower and torque, resulting in faster acceleration and a higher top speed. With the right modifications, a Nissan GT-R R35 can produce over 1000 horsepower.


Suspension Upgrades

Another popular modification for the Nissan GT-R R35 is upgrading the suspension. This can include installing coilovers, sway bars, and strut braces. These upgrades can improve the car's handling and cornering ability, making it more responsive and agile on the road. They can also make the car more comfortable to drive, reducing the impact of bumps and rough roads.


Brake Upgrades

Upgrading the brakes is another common modification for the Nissan GT-R R35. This can include replacing the brake pads and rotors with high-performance alternatives, as well as installing larger calipers and brake lines. These upgrades can significantly improve the car's braking ability, allowing it to stop more quickly and safely.


Aerodynamic Modifications

Aerodynamic modifications can also be made to a Nissan GT-R R35 to improve its performance. This can include installing a front lip, rear spoiler, and side skirts. These modifications can help to reduce drag and increase downforce, improving the car's overall handling and stability at high speeds.


Interior Upgrades

Finally, there are many interior upgrades that can be made to a Nissan GT-R R35. This can include installing a new sound system, adding a custom steering wheel, and upgrading the seats. These modifications can improve the car's comfort and convenience, as well as its overall appearance.


There are many modifications that can be made to a Nissan GT-R R35 to improve its performance and make it stand out. Whether you're looking to increase horsepower, improve handling, or simply enhance the car's appearance, there are plenty of options available. However, it's important to remember that modifying a car can be expensive and may void the manufacturer's warranty, so it's important to do your research and work with a reputable mechanic or tuning shop.
