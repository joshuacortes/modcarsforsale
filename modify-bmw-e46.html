BMW 3 Series (E46)

The fourth generation of the compact executive BMW 3 Series line, which was built by the German carmaker BMW from 1997 to 2006 and was the replacement for the E36, whose manufacturing ended in 2000. The E46 was initially released in November 1997 and came in a variety of body types, including sedan, coupé, convertible, station wagon, and hatchback. The 3 Series Compact name has been used to promote the latter.

The coupé-bodied M3 performance model debuted in June 2000, and in April 2001, the convertible version of the vehicle was released. It has a 6-speed manual or 6-speed SMG-II automated manual transmission and a BMW S54 straight-six engine as its power source.

Common BMW E46 Modifications

Modifying a BMW E46 remains popular in contemporary car culture for various compelling reasons. Firstly, the E46's timeless design and reputation as a classic model captivate enthusiasts, making it an ideal canvas for customization.

The availability of a robust aftermarket support ecosystem further fuels the popularity, providing a wide range of performance upgrades, exterior enhancements, and interior refinements.

Among the most common BMW E46 modifications are cold air intake systems and exhaust upgrades, which optimize engine performance and sound. Lowering springs or coilover kits are popular for improving the car's stance and handling dynamics. Additionally, cosmetic changes, such as upgrading the wheels, installing a front lip or rear spoiler, and retrofitting modern lighting, are frequent modifications to enhance the E46's visual appeal. These modifications not only allow enthusiasts to showcase their individuality but also elevate the driving experience and rejuvenate the allure of this iconic BMW model.




BMW E46 Turbocharger

For BMW E46 enthusiasts seeking to elevate the performance of their beloved car, turbocharging presents an exhilarating opportunity. Installing a turbocharger on the E46 engine can significantly boost horsepower and torque, transforming the driving experience into a thrilling adventure.


Active Autowerke BMW E46 Turbo Kit

The Active Autowerke BMW E46 Turbo Kit is a highly regarded option known for its exceptional performance gains and reliability. This kit is designed specifically for the E46's 3.2-liter M54 engine, found in the E46 M3. It features a BorgWarner twin-scroll turbocharger, high-quality stainless steel exhaust manifolds, and an efficient intercooler system. With proper tuning, the Active Autowerke Turbo Kit can deliver significant power gains, unleashing the full potential of the E46 M3.


VF Engineering BMW E46 Supercharger Kit

While not a turbocharger, the VF Engineering Supercharger Kit deserves mention for its impressive performance upgrades. Designed for both the E46 330i and the E46 M3, this kit includes a Vortech V3 Si-trim supercharger, high-flow intake, and intercooler system. The E46 equipped with VF Engineering's Supercharger Kit experiences a substantial increase in power and torque, providing a thrilling driving experience with instant response and smooth power delivery.

Maximum PSI BMW E46 Single Turbo Kit

The Maximum PSI Single Turbo Kit is a popular option for those seeking a single-turbo setup on their E46. This kit is designed to fit both the E46 325i and 330i models with the M54 engine. It utilizes a precision turbocharger and a custom exhaust manifold to deliver enhanced performance without sacrificing reliability. With the right supporting modifications and tuning, the Maximum PSI Single Turbo Kit can achieve impressive power gains while maintaining daily drivability.


Precision 6266 BMW E46 Turbo Kit

For E46 enthusiasts with a focus on high-performance track or race applications, the Precision 6266 Turbo Kit offers an excellent solution. This kit is custom-built to fit various E46 models, featuring a Precision 6266 CEA turbocharger known for its exceptional flow and efficiency. With proper tuning and supporting modifications, the Precision 6266 Turbo Kit can deliver staggering power figures, making it a favorite among the performance-oriented E46 community.




BMW E46 Suspension

Modifying a BMW E46's suspension is one of the most effective ways to enhance its performance, handling, and overall driving experience. Whether you seek improved cornering, reduced body roll, or a smoother ride, the aftermarket offers a plethora of suspension products tailored for the E46 platform.


KW Variant 3 Coilover Kit

The KW Variant 3 Coilover Kit is a popular choice among BMW E46 enthusiasts seeking a comprehensive suspension upgrade. Renowned for its exceptional build quality and adjustable damping, this coilover kit provides a perfect balance between daily driving comfort and track-ready performance. The stainless steel construction ensures durability, while the independently adjustable rebound and compression settings allow for personalized fine-tuning to suit your driving preferences.


Bilstein B12 Pro-Kit Suspension System

For those seeking a sporty yet comfortable ride, the Bilstein B12 Pro-Kit Suspension System is an excellent option. This complete package includes Bilstein B8 high-performance shock absorbers paired with Eibach Pro-Kit springs. The combination of Bilstein's renowned damping technology and Eibach's progressive spring rates results in improved handling, reduced body roll, and enhanced aesthetics with a slight drop in ride height.

H&R Sport Springs

If you desire a simple and cost-effective suspension upgrade, H&R Sport Springs are a popular choice. These lowering springs provide a moderate drop in ride height, enhancing the E46's appearance and lowering its center of gravity for improved handling. While retaining factory shocks and struts, H&R Sport Springs deliver a sportier and more composed ride without sacrificing comfort for daily driving.


Dinan Stage 1 Suspension System

For those seeking a comprehensive suspension upgrade engineered by experts, the Dinan Stage 1 Suspension System is an excellent option. Developed specifically for the E46 platform, this system includes high-performance Dinan-tuned Koni shocks and struts, along with a set of matched springs. The result is a noticeable improvement in handling, responsiveness, and stability, without compromising ride quality.


Ground Control Suspension Kits

Ground Control Suspension Kits offer a customizable approach for BMW E46 owners seeking a tailored suspension setup. These kits feature threaded coilover sleeves that allow you to adjust ride height and corner weights to your preference. Paired with your choice of high-quality shock absorbers, such as Koni or Bilstein, Ground Control Suspension Kits offer versatility and performance for both street and track applications.

BMW E46 Wheels

For automotive enthusiasts, modifying the fitment and stance of the BMW E46 is a captivating way to enhance the vehicle's appearance and performance. Achieving the perfect stance involves optimizing wheel fitment, offset, and suspension settings to create a visually appealing and aggressive look. In this article, we explore options for modifying fitment and stance in the BMW E46, including specific wheel and rim specs that will give your ride an unparalleled presence on the road.


Wheel Fitment Basics

Wheel fitment refers to the relationship between the wheels and the vehicle's fenders. Proper fitment is crucial to achieve the desired stance while ensuring there is enough clearance for suspension travel and turning radius. The fitment process involves considering wheel width, diameter, offset, and tire size to achieve a flush or aggressive stance.


Flush Fitment

For a clean and modern look, many E46 owners opt for flush fitment, where the wheels sit nearly flush with the fenders. To achieve this, select wheels with an offset that pushes them closer to the outer edge of the fender. For example, you might consider a set of 18x8.5-inch wheels with a +35 offset for the front and 18x9.5-inch wheels with a +45 offset for the rear, paired with appropriately sized tires.


Aggressive Stance

To achieve an aggressive stance with wider wheels and a more pronounced fitment, consider opting for a staggered setup. This involves using wider rear wheels with a more aggressive offset to create a wider track in the rear. For example, you might choose 19x9.5-inch wheels with a +22 offset for the front and 19x11-inch wheels with a +25 offset for the rear, combined with wider rear tires.


Stretch and Poke

For a bold and attention-grabbing stance, some enthusiasts embrace the "stretch and poke" look. This involves fitting narrower tires on wider wheels, causing the tires to stretch slightly over the rims. Additionally, the wheels may have a more negative offset, causing them to extend beyond the fenders. This aggressive stance gives the E46 a distinct and unique appearance.


Air Suspension

To further enhance fitment adjustability and stance customization, consider upgrading to an air suspension system. Air suspension allows you to adjust the ride height at the touch of a button, giving you the ability to achieve the desired height and stance for various driving scenarios, from daily driving to show events. With air suspension, you can easily raise the vehicle for practical use and lower it for a jaw-dropping show stance.
